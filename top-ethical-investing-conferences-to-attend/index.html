Top Ethical Investing Conferences to Attend


As the world becomes increasingly aware of the importance of responsible investing, the demand for conferences that focus on ethical investing continues to grow. These events provide a platform for industry experts, investors, and thought leaders to share knowledge, best practices, and innovative ideas on how to create a more sustainable financial future. In this article, we will explore the top ethical investing conferences to attend, covering a range of topics from environmental, social, and governance (ESG) investing to impact investing and sustainable finance.

  1. Ethical Corporation's Responsible Business Summit The Responsible Business Summit, organized by Ethical Corporation, is a premier event that brings together business leaders, investors, and experts to discuss the latest trends and innovations in responsible business practices. The conference features keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as sustainable supply chain management, diversity and inclusion, and social impact measurement. This event is a must-attend for those interested in ESG investing, corporate social responsibility, and impact measurement. Location: London, UK Dates: Typically held in March
  2. The Social Stock Exchange's Impact Investing Conference The Impact Investing Conference, organized by The Social Stock Exchange, is a leading event that focuses on the intersection of finance and social impact. The conference features keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as impact investing, social entrepreneurship, and sustainable finance. This event is a great opportunity to learn from industry experts and network with like-minded professionals. Location: London, UK Dates: Typically held in November
  3. CFA Institute's Global Investment Conference The Global Investment Conference, organized by the CFA Institute, is a flagship event that brings together investment professionals from around the world to discuss the latest trends and innovations in investment management. The conference features keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as ESG investing, alternative investments, and asset allocation. This event is a great opportunity to learn from industry experts and network with fellow investment professionals. Location: Various locations worldwide Dates: Typically held in June
  4. The Financial Times' Sustainable Business Summit The Sustainable Business Summit, organized by the Financial Times, is a premier event that brings together business leaders, investors, and experts to discuss the latest trends and innovations in sustainable business practices. The conference features keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as sustainable supply chain management, renewable energy, and sustainable agriculture. This event is a great opportunity to learn from industry experts and network with like-minded professionals. Location: London, UK Dates: Typically held in September
  5. The Skoll World Forum on Social Entrepreneurship The Skoll World Forum on Social Entrepreneurship, organized by the Skoll Foundation, is a leading event that brings together social entrepreneurs, investors, and experts to discuss the latest trends and innovations in social entrepreneurship. The conference features keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops on topics such as social impact measurement, sustainable finance, and social entrepreneurship. This event is a great opportunity to learn from industry experts and network with like-minded professionals. Location: Oxford, UK Dates: Typically held in March Conclusion Attending conferences is an excellent way to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and innovations in ethical investing. These events provide a platform for industry experts, investors, and thought leaders to share knowledge, best practices, and innovative ideas on how to create a more sustainable financial future. Whether you're interested in ESG investing, impact investing, or sustainable finance, there's a conference out there for you. By attending these conferences, you'll be able to network with like-minded professionals, learn from industry experts, and gain valuable insights into the latest trends and innovations in ethical investing. Additional Resources

Ethical Corporation: A leading provider of research, training, and conferences on responsible business practices and ESG investing. The Social Stock Exchange: A leading provider of research, training, and conferences on impact investing and social entrepreneurship. The Skoll Foundation: A leading provider of research, training, and conferences on social entrepreneurship and social impact measurement.

By attending these conferences and staying up-to-date with the latest trends and innovations in ethical investing, you'll be well on your way to creating a more sustainable financial future for yourself and the world around you.
