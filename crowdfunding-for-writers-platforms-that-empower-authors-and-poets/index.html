1. Kickstarter

Kickstarter 是最受欢迎的众筹平台之一，允许创作者在 30 天内筹集资金，实现项目目标。Kickstarter is one of the most popular crowdfunding platforms, allowing creators to raise funds within 30 days to achieve their project goals. 作家和诗人可以在 Kickstarter 上创建项目，分享他们的作品，吸引支持者和投资者。Writers and poets can create projects on Kickstarter, share their work, and attract supporters and investors.
