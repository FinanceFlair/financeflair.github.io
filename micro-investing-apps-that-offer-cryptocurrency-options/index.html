Micro Investing Apps That Offer Cryptocurrency Options


The rise of micro investing apps has transformed the way people engage with financial markets. These platforms simplify investing by allowing users to contribute small sums of money, making it accessible to almost anyone. With the increasing popularity of cryptocurrencies, many micro investing apps have expanded their offerings to include digital assets alongside traditional stocks and ETFs. This fusion opens up new opportunities for those looking to diversify their portfolios without needing a significant upfront investment. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore the ecosystem of micro investing apps that include cryptocurrency options, their benefits, the features to look for, and how to make the most of these tools for your financial future.

What Is Micro Investing?

Micro investing refers to the process of investing small amounts of money regularly rather than large lump sums. This strategy has gained popularity, especially among younger generations and those with limited disposable income, because it lowers the barrier to entry into the investment world. The core advantage is that even minor contributions accumulate over time to build significant investment portfolios.

Traditional micro investing platforms primarily offered stocks and exchange-traded funds (ETFs). However, with cryptocurrencies gaining traction as viable investment assets, some apps have integrated the ability to buy and sell popular digital currencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others.

Why Cryptocurrency on Micro Investing Apps?

Cryptocurrency markets have opened a new frontier in the investment landscape. For many people, cryptocurrencies represent a high-risk, high-reward asset class that can diversify beyond conventional financial instruments.

By combining micro investing strategies with cryptocurrencies, these apps allow users to:

  • Start small and experiment with digital currencies without risking large amounts of money.
  • Dollar-cost average into volatile cryptocurrency markets, reducing exposure to fluctuations.
  • Diversify their portfolios by including both traditional and digital assets.
  • Access an intuitive platform that integrates all holdings in one place.

This democratization of crypto investment attracts both novices wanting to dip their toes and experienced traders looking for convenient options.

Leading Micro Investing Apps Featuring Crypto

Let’s look at some of the top micro investing apps that offer seamless cryptocurrency access along with traditional investments.

1. Acorns

Acorns is widely recognized for its round-up investment feature where spare change from everyday purchases is automatically invested. Recently, Acorns introduced cryptocurrency trading, allowing users to include digital assets within their diversified portfolios.

  • Crypto offerings: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash.
  • Investment approach: Users allocate a portion of their portfolio to crypto, balanced with stocks and bonds.
  • Unique feature: Round-up spare change is partially invested into cryptocurrencies.
  • Accessibility: Beginner-friendly with educational content and automatic portfolio rebalancing.
  • Fees: Subscription-based pricing, starting at $3 per month.

Acorns is ideal for someone who wants an automated system that eases entry into cryptocurrencies alongside traditional investments.

2. Stash

Stash started as a micro investing app designed to educate and empower individual investors. It now supports cryptocurrency trading, enabling users to invest in fractional shares of cryptocurrencies.

  • Crypto offerings: Bitcoin and Ethereum (with plans to expand).
  • Investment approach: Self-directed or guided portfolios, allowing more personalized control.
  • Unique feature: Rich educational ecosystem plus customizable plans.
  • Accessibility: Supports recurring investments and fractional crypto shares.
  • Fees: Monthly subscription fee ranges from $1 to $9, based on features.

Stash suits individuals looking for a blend of education and control, making micro investing with crypto more comprehensible.

3. Robinhood

Though not a traditional micro investing app, Robinhood appeals to many beginner investors with features making fractional shares and cryptocurrencies accessible.

  • Crypto offerings: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Dogecoin, Litecoin, and others.
  • Investment approach: Commission-free trading for both stocks and crypto.
  • Unique feature: One app for stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies.
  • Accessibility: Instant deposits and streamlined interface.
  • Fees: $0 trading fees but might have wider spreads.

Robinhood attracts investors who want flexibility trading a mix of assets without commissions, with the option to invest small amounts.

4. Public

Public blends social networking with investing, allowing users to share insights and learn from others. It recently expanded to provide cryptocurrency trading.

  • Crypto offerings: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and select altcoins.
  • Investment approach: Commission-free fractional shares and crypto trading.
  • Unique feature: Community-driven insights and transparent company ownership.
  • Accessibility: No minimum deposit and user-friendly design.
  • Fees: No commissions; generates revenue via optional tipping and order flow.

Public is a good fit for users interested in community engagement as they explore both traditional stocks and cryptocurrencies.

5. SoFi Invest

SoFi Invest is a versatile financial platform with micro investing features, including automatic rebalancing and a range of cryptocurrencies.

  • Crypto offerings: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, and others.
  • Investment approach: Automated investing or self-directed crypto trading.
  • Unique feature: Access to financial advisors and additional personal finance services.
  • Accessibility: Low minimum investment and easy funding methods.
  • Fees: No management fees for automated portfolios; crypto trading fees included in spread.

SoFi’s integrated service makes it attractive for users who want combining investments with personal finance management.

Key Features to Consider When Choosing a Micro Investing App with Crypto

Selecting the right platform requires evaluating several factors beyond just the availability of cryptocurrencies.

Security and Regulation

Crypto trading platforms must follow security protocols like two-factor authentication (2FA), cold storage for customer funds, and compliance with federal regulations. Look for apps backed by reputable firms with solid security histories.

Fees and Cost Structure

Understand the fee mechanisms: some apps charge monthly subscription fees, others take a spread on crypto trades, and some have commissions. Lower costs can significantly improve long-term returns, especially for small investments.

Cryptocurrency Selection

Each platform offers different cryptocurrencies. While Bitcoin and Ethereum remain staples, some apps support emerging altcoins as well. Choose apps that align with your crypto interests.

Ease of Use and Accessibility

A clean interface, mobile-friendly design, and minimal deposit requirements are crucial to encourage consistent engagement. Features like round-up investing or recurring contributions add convenience.

Education and Support

Especially for new users, apps that provide tutorials, market insights, and responsive customer service can improve investment confidence.

Portfolio Diversification Tools

Apps that allow mixing between stocks, ETFs, bonds, and cryptocurrencies provide a built-in diversification method, lowering risk exposures.

How to Successfully Use Micro Investing Apps for Cryptocurrencies

  1. Start Small and Grow with Confidence

Taking small positions in cryptocurrencies reduces risk from volatility. Use recurring investments to employ dollar-cost averaging, buying regularly regardless of market conditions.

  1. Diversify Your Portfolio

Avoid putting all your funds into crypto assets alone. Balance crypto with other investments like ETFs or bonds through these apps to mitigate risk.

  1. Stay Educated

Cryptocurrency markets evolve rapidly. Leverage in-app educational resources and stay updated on market trends.

  1. Keep an Eye on Fees

Monitor transaction fees and potential hidden costs that can erode gains in small-scale investing.

  1. Use Security Best Practices

Enable 2FA, use strong passwords, and avoid public Wi-Fi when managing your accounts.

  1. Leverage Additional Features

If your micro investing app offers features like auto-rebalancing, round-ups, or social sharing, use them to optimize your investing process.

Potential Drawbacks to Watch Out For

  • Limited Crypto Choices: Some apps offer only a handful of popular cryptocurrencies.
  • Higher Fees for Small Transactions: Spreads on crypto trades can be wider for small investments.
  • Less Control: Automated portfolios may not cater well to all investor preferences.
  • Regulatory Risks: Crypto regulations vary and could affect platform services.
  • Volatility: Cryptocurrency prices are highly unpredictable and can impact portfolio value drastically.

The Future of Micro Investing and Crypto Integration

The growing fusion of micro investing platforms with cryptocurrency options represents the future trend of retail investing. As blockchain technologies mature and regulatory frameworks stabilize, expect more sophisticated features:

  • Enhanced DeFi (Decentralized Finance) access integrated into apps.
  • Expanded crypto asset options, including NFTs and tokens with utility.
  • AI-driven personalized advice blending traditional finance and crypto.
  • Cross-border investing democratized through seamless fiat and crypto conversions.
  • Deeper community engagement and social investing around digital assets.

This evolution will further empower many to participate safely in modern financial markets with minimal barriers.

Final Thoughts

Micro investing apps that offer cryptocurrency options create exciting opportunities for diverse, accessible, and engaging investing experiences. They allow individuals to begin their investing journey with small capital, experiment with innovative crypto assets, and build balanced portfolios. By carefully selecting the right platform based on factors like security, fees, crypto offerings, and user experience, anyone can cultivate a smart financial future combining traditional and digital assets.

Embracing these tools with thoughtful strategies and continual education can help unlock the true potential of modern micro investing amid a rapidly shifting investment landscape. Whether you’re an absolute beginner or seasoned investor, there’s never been a better time to explore the synergy of micro investing and cryptocurrency.
