The Best Robo-Advisors for ESG Investing in 2023


In 2023, the world of investing continues to evolve with a growing emphasis on environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors. Investors are increasingly seeking ways to align their financial goals with their values, and robo-advisors have become one of the most popular tools for achieving these objectives. These automated investment platforms provide cost-effective, accessible, and easy-to-use solutions for individuals looking to incorporate ESG principles into their portfolios. In this post, we will explore some of the best robo-advisors for ESG investing in 2023, highlighting their unique features and how they cater to socially-conscious investors.

Understanding ESG Investing

Before diving into the top robo-advisors, it’s important to understand what ESG investing entails. ESG investing refers to the strategy of selecting investments based on companies’ performance in three critical areas:

  • Environmental: This focuses on a company’s environmental impact, including its efforts to reduce carbon emissions, conserve water, manage waste, and promote sustainable practices.

  • Social: This aspect evaluates a company’s relationships with its employees, customers, and the broader community. Factors such as diversity, labor practices, and human rights are key considerations.

  • Governance: Governance involves assessing a company’s leadership, ethical standards, transparency, and accountability. It includes factors such as board diversity, executive compensation, and shareholder rights.

ESG investing has grown significantly in popularity, as more investors recognize the long-term benefits of supporting companies that prioritize sustainable and ethical practices. The rise of robo-advisors has made it easier than ever for individuals to invest in accordance with their ESG values, thanks to their low fees, automatic portfolio management, and simplified user experience.

Why Choose a Robo-Advisor for ESG Investing?

Robo-advisors are digital platforms that use algorithms to manage investments. They offer a number of advantages for ESG investors:

  1. Cost-Effective: Traditional financial advisors often charge high fees, but robo-advisors generally offer much lower fees, making them an attractive option for individuals who want to minimize costs while maintaining a diversified portfolio.

  2. Accessibility: Robo-advisors are available to anyone with an internet connection, removing barriers to entry for investors who may not have large amounts of capital to invest.

  3. Personalization: Many robo-advisors allow users to customize their portfolios based on specific values, such as ESG criteria. This ensures that investors can create a portfolio that aligns with their personal beliefs.

  4. Automation: Robo-advisors take the guesswork out of investing. With automated portfolio rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting, they help ensure that your investments stay aligned with your goals without requiring constant attention.

  5. Transparency: Most robo-advisors provide clear insights into your portfolio, making it easier to track the performance of your investments and understand how ESG factors are being incorporated.

Given these advantages, it’s no surprise that more investors are turning to robo-advisors as a tool for ESG investing. But with so many options available, which ones are the best in 2023?

Top Robo-Advisors for ESG Investing in 2023

Here are some of the best robo-advisors for ESG investing in 2023, each offering unique features to help you align your investments with your ethical and financial goals.

1. Betterment

Betterment is one of the most popular robo-advisors in the market and offers a highly customizable ESG investment option. With Betterment’s Socially Responsible Investing (SRI) portfolio, investors can align their investments with ESG principles.

  • Customization: Betterment allows users to choose their level of ESG focus, ensuring that your portfolio is tailored to your personal values. The platform also offers the option to exclude certain sectors or companies that don’t align with your beliefs, such as fossil fuels, tobacco, or weapons.

  • Diversification: Betterment’s SRI portfolio provides broad exposure to U.S. and international stocks, bonds, and other asset classes, helping investors achieve a well-rounded, diversified portfolio.

  • Automated Rebalancing: Betterment’s algorithms automatically rebalance your portfolio to maintain your desired asset allocation, ensuring that your investments stay aligned with your ESG goals.

  • Low Fees: Betterment offers competitive fees, with no additional costs for ESG investing. Their standard fee is just 0.25% annually, which is relatively low compared to traditional financial advisors.

Betterment’s combination of customization, low fees, and strong ESG investment options makes it an excellent choice for socially-conscious investors.

2. Wealthfront

Wealthfront is another well-known robo-advisor that offers ESG-focused portfolios. Their approach to ESG investing is centered around sustainable investing, and they offer a range of socially responsible investment options.

  • ESG Investing Strategy: Wealthfront’s ESG portfolios are designed to help investors align their investments with their values, particularly around issues such as climate change and human rights. Their portfolios include ETFs that screen for companies with high ESG ratings.

  • Tax-Loss Harvesting: Wealthfront offers tax-loss harvesting, which can help reduce your tax liability by selling investments at a loss. This feature is particularly beneficial for investors who want to maximize returns while keeping taxes low.

  • Low Fees: Wealthfront charges an annual fee of 0.25%, which is competitive in the robo-advisor market. This fee includes access to their ESG portfolios and automated financial planning tools.

Wealthfront’s combination of advanced features, including tax optimization and a strong focus on ESG criteria, makes it a great option for those looking to incorporate sustainability into their investment strategy.

3. Ellevest

Ellevest is a robo-advisor specifically designed for women, and it offers an ESG investment portfolio that is ideal for investors looking to support companies with strong social and environmental records.

  • Personalized ESG Portfolios: Ellevest offers an ESG portfolio that is built to help investors focus on companies that support gender equality, environmental sustainability, and fair labor practices.

  • Impact Investing: Ellevest’s impact investing strategy incorporates a wide range of companies that contribute positively to society and the environment, from renewable energy companies to those promoting diversity in the workplace.

  • Goals-Based Approach: Ellevest focuses on helping investors meet their personal financial goals, such as saving for retirement or buying a home, while also ensuring their investments reflect their values.

  • Financial Planning Tools: Ellevest offers personalized financial planning and advice, including tools to help you plan for major life events, such as retirement, buying a house, or starting a family.

Ellevest’s combination of gender-focused investing and commitment to ESG principles makes it a standout option for women looking to invest with purpose.

4. M1 Finance

M1 Finance is a unique robo-advisor that allows users to create customized portfolios based on their values. M1’s ESG investing option enables users to select specific ETFs that align with their social and environmental preferences.

  • Customizable Portfolios: M1 Finance allows investors to build their own portfolios by selecting individual stocks and ETFs. This gives you the flexibility to choose companies and funds that meet your ESG criteria.

  • Fractional Shares: M1 Finance offers fractional shares, which means you can invest in high-cost stocks or ETFs without needing to purchase a full share. This feature makes it easier for smaller investors to build a diversified ESG portfolio.

  • Low Fees: M1 Finance charges no management fees for its basic account, making it an affordable option for investors. There are additional fees for certain premium services, but the platform is generally cost-effective.

M1 Finance’s customization options and ability to invest in fractional shares make it an attractive choice for DIY investors who want to take a hands-on approach to ESG investing.

5. SoFi Invest

SoFi Invest is another strong player in the robo-advisor market and offers a variety of investment options, including ESG portfolios. SoFi’s ESG investing strategy is centered on sustainable growth and impact investing.

  • Automated ESG Portfolios: SoFi Invest offers automated ESG portfolios that invest in companies with strong environmental and social practices. These portfolios are managed based on a set of ESG criteria and are designed to help you achieve sustainable returns.

  • Low Fees: SoFi Invest charges no management fees for its automated investing service, making it one of the most affordable options for ESG investing.

  • Educational Resources: SoFi Invest provides a wide range of educational resources to help investors understand the benefits of ESG investing and how to make smart, socially-conscious investment decisions.

SoFi Invest’s low-cost structure, educational tools, and commitment to sustainability make it an appealing option for those looking to incorporate ESG values into their portfolios.

Conclusion

In 2023, ESG investing is no longer just a niche strategy—it’s a mainstream approach to investing that aligns financial goals with social and environmental values. Robo-advisors provide an easy, affordable, and efficient way to implement an ESG-focused investment strategy, allowing investors to take control of their financial futures while supporting companies that prioritize sustainability and social responsibility.

Betterment, Wealthfront, Ellevest, M1 Finance, and SoFi Invest are among the best robo-advisors for ESG investing in 2023, offering a range of features to cater to different investor needs. Whether you’re looking for a fully automated solution, a customizable approach, or a focus on gender equality, these platforms provide excellent options for socially-conscious investors.

As the demand for ESG investing continues to rise, these robo-advisors will undoubtedly evolve to offer even more sophisticated and accessible solutions. By choosing one of these platforms, you can ensure that your investments reflect your values while working toward your long-term financial goals.
