The Best Credit Cards for Travel Rewards in 2023


Travel enthusiasts know that the right credit card can unlock a world of benefits, from earning free flights to enjoying VIP experiences. If you’re someone who dreams of flying first-class, staying in luxurious hotels, or simply finding ways to make travel more affordable, getting a travel rewards credit card can help you achieve your goals. But with so many options on the market, figuring out which one is best for your needs can be overwhelming. This guide breaks down the best credit cards for travel rewards in 2023 to help you make an informed decision.

What Makes a Great Travel Rewards Credit Card?

Before diving into the specific recommendations, it’s important to understand what sets travel rewards credit cards apart. These cards are designed to reward you for your travel-related spending while offering perks that enhance your journeys. Key features to look for include:

  • Earning Potential: High rewards rates on travel-related spending like flights, hotels, car rentals, and even dining or groceries if applicable.
  • Sign-Up Bonuses: A competitive bonus when you meet a minimum spending requirement, giving you a head start in your rewards journey.
  • Redemption Flexibility: Options to redeem points or miles for flights, hotels, rental cars, or statement credits without blackout dates.
  • Travel Perks: Added benefits such as free checked luggage, airport lounge access, trip insurance, and no foreign transaction fees.
  • Annual Fees vs. Value: Some cards charge hefty annual fees, but the value of the rewards and perks often outweigh them.

Top Travel Rewards Credit Cards in 2023

Here’s a closer look at some of the best travel rewards credit cards available this year, each offering unique benefits tailored to different types of travelers. Whether you’re a frequent flyer or an occasional adventurer, there’s a card designed for you.

1. The Everyday Explorer: Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card

The Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card is consistently ranked as one of the best travel credit cards on the market, and for good reason. It strikes a perfect balance between rewards, versatility, and affordability.

Highlights:

  • Earn 2x points on travel and dining, and 1x point on all other purchases.
  • Get a generous sign-up bonus: 60,000 points after spending $4,000 in the first 3 months. That’s worth $750 when redeemed for travel through Chase Ultimate Rewards.
  • Enjoy 25% more value when redeeming for travel via the Chase portal.

What makes this card stand out is the flexibility of the Chase Ultimate Rewards program. Points can be redeemed for travel, transferred to over a dozen travel partners like United Airlines or Hyatt, or cashed out. The card’s modest $95 annual fee makes it an exceptional choice for both frequent and casual travelers.

2. The Premium Experience: The Platinum Card® from American Express

If luxury travel is your style, The Platinum Card® from American Express offers unparalleled perks and services that elevate your adventures. Though the $695 annual fee might give some pause, its benefits far exceed the cost for the right user.

Highlights:

  • Earn 5x Membership Rewards® points on flights booked directly with airlines or via AmexTravel, and 5x points on hotels booked via the same portal.
  • Receive a welcome bonus of 80,000 points after spending $6,000 in the first 6 months.
  • Unlock a $200 credit annually for airline incidentals and $200 for fine hotels and resorts.
  • Complimentary lounge access through The Centurion® Lounge and Priority Pass™ Select.

With an array of travel credits, concierge service, and exclusive partnerships, this card is an aspiration for travelers seeking first-class treatment during their journeys.

3. The Budget-Friendly Adventurer: Capital One Venture Rewards Credit Card

Simple and straightforward, the Capital One Venture Rewards Credit Card is perfect for those who want flexibility without complicated point systems. It’s particularly suited for budget-conscious travelers who still want to maximize their rewards.

Highlights:

  • Earn 2x miles on every purchase, regardless of category.
  • Enjoy a sign-up bonus of 75,000 miles after spending $4,000 in the first 3 months.
  • Redeem miles as a statement credit for travel expenses, including hotels, flights, rental cars, and even travel agencies.
  • TSA PreCheck or Global Entry fee credit (up to $100).

With its flat rewards rate and no foreign transaction fees, this card is ideal for both domestic and international travel.

4. The Hotel Lover: Hilton Honors American Express Surpass® Card

For those who prioritize hotel stays, the Hilton Honors American Express Surpass® Card is a must-have. It’s tailored to travelers who frequently stay at Hilton properties and want to maximize their benefits with each booking.

Highlights:

  • Earn 12x Hilton Honors points at Hilton hotels, 6x points on dining, groceries, and gas, and 3x points on other purchases.
  • Receive a welcome bonus of 130,000 points after spending $2,000 in the first 3 months.
  • Complimentary Hilton Honors Gold Status, which includes free breakfast, room upgrades, and bonus points.
  • 10 Priority Pass™ airport lounge visits per year.

This card allows you to rack up points quickly and enjoy exclusive hotel perks that elevate your stay experience.

5. The Frequent Flyer: Citi® / AAdvantage® Executive World Elite Mastercard®

If you’re loyal to American Airlines, the Citi® / AAdvantage® Executive World Elite Mastercard® lets you earn miles quickly while gaining premium travel benefits. This card is a standout option for frequent flyers looking to maximize their rewards and enjoy elite perks.

Highlights:

  • Earn 2x miles on American Airlines purchases and 1x mile on all other purchases.
  • Gain 75,000 bonus miles after spending $4,000 in the first 3 months.
  • Complimentary Admirals Club® membership for you and authorized users.
  • First checked bag free and priority boarding on American Airlines flights.

For American Airlines travelers, this card is a no-brainer, providing lounge access and airline-specific perks.

How to Choose the Right Travel Rewards Card for You

Ultimately, the best travel rewards credit card depends on factors like your spending habits, travel preferences, and lifestyle. Here’s a checklist to help narrow it down:

  1. Consider Your Travel Goals: Are you saving for international flights, enjoying hotel bonuses, or looking for luxury?
  2. Analyze Your Spending Patterns: Choose a card that rewards you in categories you spend the most on, such as dining, groceries, or transportation.
  3. Factor in the Annual Fee: High-fee cards often offer more perks, but ensure the value outweighs the cost for your situation.
  4. Evaluate Redemption Options: Flexible cards like the Chase Sapphire Preferred are great for those who prefer choices, while co-branded cards suit loyal travelers.
  5. Check for Add-On Benefits: Lounge access, insurance, and credits can make all the difference for frequent travelers.

Take Advantage of Travel Rewards Today

Travel rewards credit cards will always be an invaluable tool for those who love to explore. By choosing the right card tailored to your travel style, you can unlock incredible savings and exclusive experiences worldwide. While the options above represent some of the best picks for 2023, each is unique in how it helps travelers accomplish their adventures in smarter, more rewarding ways. Take some time to review your financial habits and priorities, and you’ll soon be reaping the benefits of your chosen travel card.

Safe travels!
