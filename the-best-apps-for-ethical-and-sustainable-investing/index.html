The Best Apps for Ethical and Sustainable Investing


With the growing concern for the environment and the increasing interest in socially responsible investing (SRI), the landscape of mobile applications for ethical and sustainable investing has expanded dramatically. These platforms allow individuals to align their financial goals with their values, promoting a more sustainable future for generations to come. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore the best apps for ethical and sustainable investing, enabling you to make informed decisions and contribute to a better world. What is Ethical and Sustainable Investing? Ethical and sustainable investing involves considering the environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance of companies when making investment decisions. This approach seeks to balance financial returns with the need to promote positive impact on society and the environment. ESG factors include a wide range of considerations, such as resource depletion, CO2 emissions, labor practices, and supply chain management. The Top Apps for Ethical and Sustainable Investing

  1. FutureFish FutureFish is an investment app that focuses on environmentally responsible investing. It allows users to discover and invest in companies that are carbon footprint-reduced and committed to sustainable practices. The platform utilizes machine learning algorithms to provide personalized investment recommendations and promote a more sustainable portfolio.
  2. Betterment Betterment is a pioneer in the robo-advisory space, offering a robust platform for sustainable investing. It allows users to create customized investment portfolios and leverages ESG data to identify companies with impressive environmental, social, and governance practices.
  3. Schwab ETFs Charles Schwab's ETFs platform offers a wide range of ESG-focused exchange-traded funds (ETFs), allowing users to track various sustainability indices, such as the MSCI ESG Leaders Index or the FTSE4Good Emerging Index. Schwab ETFs are highly diversified, providing an efficient way to gain exposure to the largest and most liquid global equities.
  4. Sturdy Sturdy is an innovative platform that invests in renewable energy projects, helping users contribute to the global shift towards a low-carbon economy. By partnering with various cleantech companies, Sturdy offers a unique and planet-centric investment opportunity.
  5. Kasasa Kasasa is a free mobile app that invests in socially responsible companies committed to creating positive impact. By leveraging community banking principles, Kasasa generates revenue from customers while channeling investments toward impact-oriented causes, thereby fostering a positive effect on society.
