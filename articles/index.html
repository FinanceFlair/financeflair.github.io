However, there’s another important document that is often overlooked but can be incredibly beneficial in ensuring your final wishes are carried out smoothly: the pour-over will. In this blog post, we’ll explore the benefits of a pour-over will and why it’s a valuable addition to your estate planning toolkit. What is a Pour-Over Will? A pour-over will is a legal document that works in conjunction with a revocable living trust. Unlike a traditional will, which directs the distribution of assets upon death, a pour-over will “pours” any assets that were not transferred to the trust during the individual’s lifetime into the trust upon their death. This means that any assets that were not specifically titled in the name of the trust will be transferred into the trust and distributed according to its terms. ...
