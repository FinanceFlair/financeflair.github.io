Understanding Syndication in Private Placements


Private placements are a popular fundraising option for companies, allowing them to raise capital from a limited number of investors without undergoing a public stock offering. However, many of these firms struggle to effectively manage and promote their private placement offerings to potential investors, or syndication group.
