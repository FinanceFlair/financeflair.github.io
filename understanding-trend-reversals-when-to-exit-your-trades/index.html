Understanding Trend Reversals: When to Exit Your Trades


As a successful trader, you've likely encountered numerous market trends throughout your career. Riding a winning trend can be exhilarating, but it's equally important to know when to exit your trades and minimize losses when the trend reverses. Trend reversals can be devastating if you're not prepared, but understanding how to identify and navigate them can save you from substantial financial losses. Trend reversals occur when the momentum of the market changes direction, signaling that the dominant trend is shifting. Recognizing these changes is crucial for making informed investment decisions and minimizing losses.
