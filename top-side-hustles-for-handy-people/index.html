Top Side Hustles for Handy People


Handy people possess a unique set of skills that can be turned into lucrative side hustles. From repairing household items to creating custom furniture, the opportunities are vast and varied. If you’re looking to leverage your handyman abilities into a profitable side business, here are some of the top side hustles you can consider. Furniture repair and restoration is a fantastic side hustle for those who are skilled with tools and have an eye for detail. Many people have antique or vintage furniture that they love but need some tender loving care. By offering services to repair, refinish, and restore these pieces, you can help clients preserve their cherished items.

Getting Started

  • Learn the trade: If you’re not already familiar with furniture repair, take some time to learn the basics. There are plenty of online courses and tutorials that can help you get started.
  • Acquire tools and materials: Invest in high-quality tools and materials. Having the right equipment will make the job easier and the results better.
  • Market your services: Use social media, local bulletin boards, and word-of-mouth to spread the word about your services.

Potential Earnings

The earnings from furniture repair and restoration can vary widely based on the complexity of the job and the value of the furniture. On average, you can charge anywhere from $50 to $500 per piece, depending on the work required.

2. Home Maintenance and Repair Services

Providing home maintenance and repair services is another excellent side hustle for handy individuals. Homeowners often need help with small repairs and maintenance tasks, such as fixing leaky faucets, patching drywall, or replacing light fixtures.

Getting Started

  • Identify your skills: Make a list of the maintenance and repair tasks you’re comfortable doing.
  • Get licensed: Depending on your location, you might need a license or certification to perform certain types of work.
  • Create a service list: Outline the services you offer and set your rates.

Potential Earnings

Home maintenance and repair services can be quite profitable. You can charge by the hour or by the job. Hourly rates typically range from $30 to $100, depending on your location and the complexity of the tasks.

3. Custom Woodworking

Custom woodworking is a highly rewarding side hustle for those who enjoy working with wood and creating unique pieces. From custom furniture to decorative items, there’s a wide range of products you can make and sell.

Getting Started

  • Set up a workshop: You’ll need a dedicated space to work on your projects. Ensure it’s well-equipped with the necessary tools and safety equipment.
  • Develop a portfolio: Create a portfolio of your work to showcase your skills and attract clients.
  • Sell your creations: You can sell your items through local craft fairs, online marketplaces like Etsy, or even set up your own website.

Potential Earnings

Custom woodworking can be very lucrative, especially if you specialize in high-end or niche products. Prices can range from $50 for small items to several thousand dollars for custom furniture pieces.

4. Handyman Services

Offering general handyman services is a versatile and flexible side hustle. As a handyman, you can tackle a variety of tasks, from assembling furniture to installing appliances and performing minor repairs.

Getting Started

  • Define your services: Clearly outline the types of jobs you’re willing to take on.
  • Set your rates: Decide whether you’ll charge by the hour or per project.
  • Advertise locally: Use flyers, business cards, and local online classifieds to promote your services.

Potential Earnings

Handyman services are in high demand, and you can charge a competitive rate based on your experience and the complexity of the jobs. Rates typically range from $30 to $80 per hour.

5. Gardening and Landscaping Services

If you have a green thumb, consider offering gardening and landscaping services. Many homeowners need help maintaining their gardens, planting flowers, and creating beautiful outdoor spaces.

Getting Started

  • Develop your skills: If you’re not already an expert gardener, take some time to learn about plants, soil, and landscaping techniques.
  • Invest in equipment: You’ll need basic gardening tools, as well as possibly a lawnmower, hedge trimmer, and other equipment.
  • Market your services: Use local advertising and social media to reach potential clients.

Potential Earnings

Gardening and landscaping services can be quite profitable, especially during the growing season. You can charge hourly rates or offer package deals for regular maintenance. Rates typically range from $25 to $60 per hour.

6. Home Cleaning Services

Home cleaning services are always in demand, and this side hustle can be particularly profitable if you’re thorough and detail-oriented. From regular cleaning schedules to deep-cleaning sessions, there are numerous opportunities to make money.

Getting Started

  • Gather supplies: Invest in high-quality cleaning supplies and equipment.
  • Set your rates: Decide on your pricing structure, whether it’s hourly rates or flat fees for specific tasks.
  • Build a client base: Start with friends and family, then expand through referrals and local advertising.

Potential Earnings

Home cleaning services can be very profitable, especially if you develop a loyal client base. Rates typically range from $20 to $50 per hour.

7. Painting and Decorating

If you have an eye for color and detail, offering painting and decorating services can be a highly rewarding side hustle. From painting rooms to applying wallpaper and decorative finishes, there are many ways to help homeowners enhance their living spaces.

Getting Started

  • Hone your skills: If you’re new to painting and decorating, take some time to learn techniques and best practices.
  • Acquire tools and supplies: Invest in high-quality brushes, rollers, paint, and other necessary supplies.
  • Promote your services: Use social media, local ads, and word-of-mouth to attract clients.

Potential Earnings

Painting and decorating services can command high rates, especially for detailed or large projects. You can charge anywhere from $200 to $1,500 per room, depending on the scope of work.

8. Plumbing Services

If you have plumbing skills, this can be a highly lucrative side hustle. From fixing leaks to installing new fixtures, plumbing services are always in demand.

Getting Started

  • Get certified: Depending on your location, you might need a certification to offer plumbing services.
  • Invest in tools: Plumbing requires specific tools, so make sure you have what you need.
  • Market your services: Use local advertising, online listings, and referrals to build your client base.

Potential Earnings

Plumbing services are among the higher-paying handyman side hustles. Hourly rates typically range from $50 to $150, depending on the complexity of the job.

9. Electrical Services

If you’re skilled with electrical work, offering electrical services can be a highly profitable side hustle. From installing new outlets to troubleshooting electrical issues, there’s a steady demand for qualified electricians.

Getting Started

  • Get licensed: Electrical work often requires a license, so make sure you meet the necessary qualifications.
  • Equip yourself: Ensure you have the proper tools and safety gear.
  • Advertise locally: Use online listings, local ads, and word-of-mouth to attract clients.

Potential Earnings

Electrical services can command high rates due to the specialized skills required. Hourly rates typically range from $50 to $150.

10. Appliance Repair

Appliance repair is another great side hustle for handy people. Whether it’s fixing a malfunctioning refrigerator or repairing a washing machine, there’s always a need for skilled appliance repair technicians.

Getting Started

  • Learn the trade: If you’re not already experienced, take some courses or find online tutorials to learn appliance repair.
  • Invest in tools: Ensure you have the necessary tools to diagnose and fix common appliance issues.
  • Market your services: Use local advertising and online listings to reach potential clients.

Potential Earnings

Appliance repair can be quite profitable. You can charge per job, with rates typically ranging from $50 to $200, depending on the complexity of the repair.

Conclusion

Handy people have a wealth of opportunities to turn their skills into profitable side hustles. Whether you’re restoring furniture, offering home repair services, or tackling electrical work, there’s a demand for your expertise. By leveraging your skills and marketing your services effectively, you can build a successful and rewarding side business. Whether you’re looking to make a little extra money or start a full-fledged business, these side hustles can help you achieve your financial goals.
