Side Hustle Ideas for Interior Designers: Styling Spaces for Profit


Interior designers are known for their creative flair and attention to detail, skills that can be leveraged to generate additional income through side hustles. With the rise of the gig economy and the increasing demand for unique and personalized living spaces, interior designers have a vast array of opportunities to monetize their skills. In this post, we’ll explore some of the most lucrative side hustle ideas for interior designers, allowing them to style spaces for profit.

1. Virtual Interior Design Services

One of the most significant advantages of being an interior designer is the ability to work remotely. Virtual interior design services allow designers to style spaces for clients without the need for physical presence. This can be done through video conferencing, phone calls, or even email consultations. Designers can offer customized design plans, 3D renderings, and shopping lists to clients, all from the comfort of their own homes.

Virtual interior design services can be marketed through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with furniture retailers. With the rise of e-commerce and online shopping, clients are increasingly comfortable with the idea of purchasing furniture and decor online, making virtual interior design services a lucrative side hustle opportunity.

2. Home Staging for Real Estate Agents

Home staging has become a crucial aspect of the real estate industry, with many agents recognizing the importance of presenting properties in their best light to attract potential buyers. Interior designers can offer home staging services to real estate agents, helping them to prepare properties for sale. This can include decluttering, rearranging furniture, and selecting decorative items to create a welcoming atmosphere.

Home staging services can be marketed through local real estate agents, property management companies, and even online platforms. With the increasing demand for homes and the competitive nature of the real estate market, home staging services can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

3. Online Marketplaces for Custom Furniture

With the rise of e-commerce and online shopping, interior designers can capitalize on the demand for unique and customized furniture pieces. Online marketplaces such as Etsy, Redbubble, and even Amazon can be used to sell customized furniture pieces, including custom-made sofas, chairs, and tables.

Designers can create their own online stores or sell through existing platforms, offering customized furniture pieces that cater to clients’ specific needs and tastes. With the increasing demand for unique and personalized living spaces, customized furniture pieces can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

4. Interior Design Workshops and Classes

Interior design workshops and classes can be a great way for designers to monetize their skills and share their knowledge with others. These can be held online or in-person, and can cover a range of topics, from basic design principles to advanced topics such as color theory and textiles.

Designers can market their workshops and classes through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with local community centers or colleges. With the increasing demand for DIY and self-improvement content, interior design workshops and classes can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for designers.

5. Affiliate Marketing for Home Decor

Affiliate marketing is a popular way for designers to monetize their online presence and promote products from other companies. Designers can partner with home decor brands, promoting their products through social media platforms, blogs, or even email newsletters.

Designers can earn a commission for each sale made through their unique referral link, allowing them to generate passive income. With the increasing demand for home decor and the rise of e-commerce, affiliate marketing can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

6. Freelance Writing for Home Decor Magazines

Freelance writing is a great way for designers to monetize their writing skills and share their knowledge with others. Designers can write articles for home decor magazines, blogs, or even online publications, covering topics such as design trends, product reviews, and DIY projects.

Designers can market their writing services through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with local publishing companies. With the increasing demand for home decor content and the rise of online publishing, freelance writing can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

7. Customized Design Consultations

Customized design consultations can be a great way for designers to monetize their skills and offer personalized design services to clients. Designers can offer customized consultations, providing clients with tailored design plans, shopping lists, and even personalized design advice.

Designers can market their customized design consultations through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with local real estate agents or property management companies. With the increasing demand for unique and personalized living spaces, customized design consultations can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

8. Online Interior Design Courses

Online interior design courses can be a great way for designers to monetize their knowledge and skills, offering clients a comprehensive and interactive learning experience. Designers can create their own online courses, covering topics such as design principles, color theory, and textiles.

Designers can market their online courses through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with local colleges or community centers. With the increasing demand for online learning and self-improvement content, online interior design courses can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

9. Interior Design Coaching

Interior design coaching can be a great way for designers to monetize their expertise and offer personalized guidance to clients. Designers can offer coaching services, providing clients with personalized design advice, goal setting, and even accountability.

Designers can market their coaching services through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with local real estate agents or property management companies. With the increasing demand for personalized and unique living spaces, interior design coaching can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

10. Interior Design Photography

Interior design photography can be a great way for designers to monetize their skills and offer clients a unique and personalized service. Designers can offer photography services, capturing high-quality images of clients’ spaces and even creating customized photo albums or prints.

Designers can market their photography services through social media platforms, online directories, and even partnerships with local real estate agents or property management companies. With the increasing demand for high-quality images and the rise of social media, interior design photography can be a lucrative side hustle opportunity for interior designers.

In conclusion, interior designers have a vast array of side hustle opportunities available to them, from virtual interior design services to customized design consultations. By leveraging their skills and creativity, designers can generate additional income and build a successful and profitable business. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to expand your services, these side hustle ideas can help you style spaces for profit and achieve your goals.
