Side Hustles for Event Planners: Supplement Your Income with Event Services


As an event planner, you have a unique set of skills that can be utilized beyond your regular job. With the gig economy on the rise, side hustles have become a popular way to supplement income and explore new opportunities. In this blog post, we will explore various side hustles that event planners can engage in to boost their earnings and expand their professional horizons.

DJ Services

Event planners are well-versed in creating the perfect ambiance for any occasion. Why not put your music knowledge and knack for creating a great atmosphere to use by offering DJ services? Whether it’s a wedding, corporate event, or private party, your expertise in selecting the right songs and keeping the crowd entertained will make you a sought-after DJ.

Event Decor and Styling

Event planners have a keen eye for aesthetics and an innate ability to transform any space into a captivating setting. Leverage your creativity and offer event decor and styling services as a side hustle. From floral arrangements to table settings, your talent for creating visually stunning environments will make you the go-to person for event design.

Event Photography

Capturing beautiful moments is an essential part of any event. As an event planner, you are constantly surrounded by picturesque settings and happy faces. Invest in a good camera and hone your photography skills to offer event photography services. By combining your organizational skills with an artistic eye, you can provide clients with a comprehensive package that covers both planning and capturing their special moments.

Event Consulting

Event planners possess a wealth of knowledge and experience in the industry. Consider offering event consulting services as a side hustle. From advising clients on budget allocation to recommending vendors and creating event timelines, your expertise can help individuals and businesses plan successful events. By offering your consulting services, you can tap into a different market and provide valuable insights to those who are looking to host their own events.

Event Hosting and Emceeing

As an event planner, you are comfortable being in the spotlight and engaging with an audience. Use your charisma and public speaking skills to offer event hosting and emceeing services. Whether it’s a conference, awards ceremony, or charity event, your ability to keep the crowd entertained and the event running smoothly will be highly valued.

Event Workshops and Training

Share your knowledge and expertise by organizing event planning workshops and training sessions. Offer insights into the industry, share tips and tricks, and provide guidance on event management. This side hustle not only allows you to supplement your income but also positions you as an authority in the field.

Event Catering

With your extensive experience in event planning, you likely have connections with various vendors, including caterers. Consider partnering with a catering service and offer event catering as a side hustle. Your understanding of clients’ needs and attention to detail will ensure a seamless dining experience for their guests.

Wrapping Up

As an event planner, your skills and expertise extend far beyond your primary job. By exploring side hustles in the event services industry, you can supplement your income, expand your professional network, and gain new experiences. Whether it’s DJ services, event photography, or event consulting, there are numerous opportunities for you to utilize your talents and passion for creating memorable experiences.

Remember, a side hustle is not only about making extra money; it’s an opportunity to explore your passions and diversify your skill set. So, embrace these side hustle ideas, market your services, and watch your income and career flourish!
