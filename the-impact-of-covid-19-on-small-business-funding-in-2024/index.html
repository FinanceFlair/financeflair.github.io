The Impact of COVID-19 on Small Business Funding in 2024


As we navigate the ongoing global situation with the COVID-19 pandemic, there has been an alarming trend unfolding behind the scenes, affecting numerous small businesses across the globe. The outbreak has disrupted traditional business models, canceled many agreements, and left entire industries in turmoil. The unanticipated pandemic consequence ultimately affects all types and sizes of businesses, with a focus on reducing expenses where all assets and employees are examined. Smaller businesses have been particularly vulnerable and are facing significant hurdles to raise capital. Over the past few years, small businesses worldwide reported challenges in securing the amount of capital they needed to deal with the hectic situation. Bull markets have ceased activity, response options being pushed back further, with big growth expectations becoming a challenger prospect only.
