How to Upsize Your Space: Creative Storage Solutions for Small Homes


Every homeowner dreams of having a spacious and organized home. However, for those living in smaller homes, this can sometimes seem like an impossible goal. The good news is that there are plenty of creative storage solutions that can maximize space and bring order to your home. In this article, we’ll explore some of these options to help you upsize your space.

  1. Utilize underutilized areas

Take a closer look at your home – there are likely areas that are often overlooked, but could serve as excellent storage spaces. For example, consider using the space under the stairs as a storage area, or turn your closet rods into a clothes-loving gallery by adding shelves above them. Make the most of alcoves and empty corners too – just be sure to organize items neatly in these spots.

  1. Befriend your walls

Maximizing wall space is an essential part of making the most of your home. Here are some ideas to do just that:

  • Shelves: Install floating shelves in various nooks and crannies to store and display items.
  • Pegboards: These can be used almost anywhere, and serve as a versatile storage solution. Attach hooks, S-hooks, and other hardware to hold all kinds of items.
  • Vertical storage: Use vertical storage options, like tension rods, hooks, or wire baskets, to create additional storage space without sacrificing floor space.
  1. Get creative with closet organization

Closets can provide a wealth of storage opportunities if you think outside the box. Here are some options to consider:

  • Use vertical space: Don’t forget about the walls of your closet – install vertical shelves or hanging rods to create more storage.
  • Add built-ins: Custom-built closets with shelves, drawers, and compartments can be an effective way to increase storage space.
  • Double up: Create a double-fold clothes rod by adding a second rod below the first one, allowing you to hang an additional row of clothes.
  • Add smarter storage solutions: Usefoldable valet rods or storage bins to hold smaller items like shoes and accessories.
  1. Expand into multi-purpose furniture

Choose furniture that serves more than one purpose to make the most of your space. Consider the following:

  • Ottomans: These can double as storage chests, as well as provide seating.
  • Tables with storage: Look for accent tables with built-in storage or shelving for added convenience.
  • Murphy beds: Pull-down or fold-up Murphy beds are perfect for making the most of space in living areas or home offices.
  1. Make use of hidden compartments

Create spaces that can be hidden from view when not in use to add versatility to your home:

  • Built-in benches: Add storage compartments under benches in your living area, entryway, or bedroom.
  • Lift-up floor boards: Hidden risers can store items, providing extra space for odds and ends.
  • Use sliding bookcases: Besides being an attractive addition to your home, sliding bookcases can offer the bonus of hidden storage.

By implementing these creative storage solutions, you’ll find it easier to maintain an orderly home, even in a small living space. Embrace your walls, utilize every corner, and invest in multi-purpose furniture to maximize space and create a cozy, organized living environment.
