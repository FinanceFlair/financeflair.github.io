The Top 10 Books on Trend Following Every Trader Should Read


Trend following is a popular trading strategy that involves identifying and capitalizing on market trends. Whether you’re a seasoned trader or just starting out, understanding the principles of trend following is essential for success in the financial markets. To help you deepen your knowledge and refine your skills in trend following, here are the top 10 books every trader should read.

1. “Trend Following: How to Make a Fortune in Bull, Bear, and Black Swan Markets” by Michael Covel

Michael Covel’s book is a comprehensive guide to trend following, covering its history, philosophy, and practical applications. This essential read provides insights from some of the most successful trend followers and offers valuable lessons for traders of all levels.

2. “Following the Trend: Diversified Managed Futures Trading” by Andreas F. Clenow

Andreas F. Clenow’s book delves into the world of managed futures and trend following strategies. With a focus on practical implementation, this book equips traders with the knowledge needed to build and manage a successful trend following portfolio.

3. “Trend Commandments: Trading for Exceptional Returns” by Michael W. Covel

In “Trend Commandments,” Michael Covel distills the essential rules of trend following into clear and actionable commandments. This book provides a concise yet impactful framework for traders to follow when navigating the complexities of market trends.

4. “Way of the Turtle: The Secret Methods that Turned Ordinary People into Legendary Traders” by Curtis M. Faith

Curtis M. Faith’s book takes readers on a journey through the famed Turtle Trading experiment, offering valuable insights into the mindset and strategies of successful trend followers. This captivating read provides a unique perspective on the application of trend following principles.

5. “Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard: How to Achieve Super Performance in Stocks in Any Market” by Mark Minervini

While not solely focused on trend following, Mark Minervini’s book is a treasure trove of wisdom for traders seeking to maximize their performance in the stock market. It contains valuable insights and techniques that can be applied to trend following strategies.

6. “Quantitative Trading: How to Build Your Own Algorithmic Trading Business” by Ernie Chan

Ernie Chan’s book offers a deep dive into the world of quantitative trading, providing practical guidance on building and testing algorithmic trading strategies, including those based on trend following principles. This insightful read is a must-have for traders interested in the intersection of trend following and quantitative analysis.

7. “Trading Systems and Methods” by Perry J. Kaufman

Perry J. Kaufman’s comprehensive book is a valuable resource for traders seeking to develop and refine their trading systems, including trend following strategies. It covers a wide range of trading methodologies and serves as a practical guide for implementing trend following principles in a systematic manner.

8. “The Complete TurtleTrader: How 23 Novice Investors Became Overnight Millionaires” by Michael W. Covel

In this captivating book, Michael Covel explores the legendary story of the Turtle Traders, shedding light on the principles and practices that led to their remarkable success as trend followers. This insightful read offers valuable lessons for aspiring traders looking to adopt a trend following approach.

9. “Trend Following with Managed Futures: The Search for Crisis Alpha” by Alex Greyserman, Kathryn Kaminski, and Mark H. Melin

This collaborative work by Greyserman, Kaminski, and Melin provides a comprehensive overview of trend following with a focus on managed futures. It offers a detailed exploration of crisis alpha and its implications for traders employing trend following strategies, making it an essential read for those seeking a deeper understanding of trend following in different market conditions.

10. “The Little Book of Trading: Trend Following Strategy for Big Winnings” by Michael W. Covel

In this concise yet impactful book, Michael Covel distills the essence of trend following into a practical guide for traders. Packed with actionable insights and real-world examples, this book serves as a valuable resource for both novice and experienced traders seeking to harness the power of trend following.

Conclusion

These top 10 books on trend following offer a wealth of knowledge and practical guidance for traders looking to enhance their understanding and application of trend following strategies. By immersing yourself in the insights and experiences shared in these books, you can elevate your trading skills and embark on a journey towards greater success in the dynamic world of financial markets. Whether you’re drawn to the historical context of trend following, the practical implementation of trading systems, or the stories of legendary trend followers, these books provide a diverse and comprehensive collection of resources to enrich your trading journey. Happy reading and may your understanding of trend following flourish as you delve into these invaluable works.
