Conclusion

Capital structure decisions are fundamental to the long-term success of any company. By examining the strategies employed by global corporate giants like Apple, Tesla, Amazon, Microsoft, Coca-Cola, and Berkshire Hathaway, we gain valuable insights into how different approaches to debt and equity can be used to optimize growth, manage risk, and enhance shareholder value.

Whether it’s using debt to fund expansion, relying on equity to fuel innovation, or maintaining a conservative balance of both, these companies show that there is no one-size-fits-all approach to capital structure. The key is finding the right balance based on the company’s stage of development, industry dynamics, risk tolerance, and growth prospects.

For businesses looking to refine their capital structure, these lessons offer a useful framework for making strategic financial decisions that will drive sustainable growth and success.
