Trend Following Books: Must-Reads for Traders


If you’re an aspiring trader or a seasoned investor looking to enhance your trading strategies, then you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we will explore some must-read trend following books that can take your trading game to the next level. These books offer valuable insights, practical advice, and real-world examples from successful traders who have mastered the art of trend following. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of trend following literature.

1. “Trend Following: How to Make a Fortune in Bull, Bear, and Black Swan Markets” by Michael W. Covel

Michael Covel’s “Trend Following” is a timeless classic that provides a comprehensive overview of the trend following approach. Covel explores the philosophy behind trend following and shares the experiences of successful trend followers. With real-life case studies and interviews, this book offers practical guidance on how to identify trends, manage risk, and develop a disciplined trading mindset. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced trader, this book is a must-read for anyone interested in trend following.

2. “Following the Trend: Diversified Managed Futures Trading” by Andreas F. Clenow

Andreas Clenow’s “Following the Trend” is a practical guide that focuses on systematic trend following strategies. Clenow provides a step-by-step approach to building and implementing trend following systems. He explains the importance of diversification, risk management, and position sizing in achieving long-term success. This book is packed with valuable insights, statistical analysis, and performance metrics that can help traders refine their trend following strategies.

3. “The Complete TurtleTrader: How 23 Novice Investors Became Overnight Millionaires” by Michael W. Covel

In “The Complete TurtleTrader,” Michael Covel tells the fascinating story of how a group of ordinary people, known as the Turtles, were taught a trend following system by legendary trader Richard Dennis. Covel explores the lessons learned from this experiment and delves into the psychology of successful trend following. This book offers valuable insights into the mindset and discipline required to succeed in the world of trend following.

4. “Trend Commandments: Trading for Exceptional Returns” by Michael W. Covel

Continuing his exploration of trend following, Michael Covel’s “Trend Commandments” presents a set of rules and principles that can guide traders in their quest for exceptional returns. Covel emphasizes the importance of sticking to a plan, managing risk, and staying disciplined in the face of market volatility. This book offers practical advice and actionable strategies that can help traders navigate the complex world of trend following.

5. “The Little Book of Trading: Trend Following Strategy for Big Winnings” by Michael W. Covel

For those looking for a concise introduction to trend following, “The Little Book of Trading” by Michael Covel is an excellent choice. In this book, Covel distills the key concepts of trend following into a compact guide that can be easily understood by traders of all levels. With practical examples and clear explanations, this book provides a solid foundation for implementing trend following strategies in your trading routine.

Conclusion

Trend following is a powerful trading strategy that has been proven effective by successful traders throughout history. The books mentioned in this post offer valuable insights, practical advice, and real-world examples that can help traders enhance their trading skills and achieve consistent profits. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced trader, these must-read trend following books are sure to provide you with the knowledge and inspiration you need to thrive in the dynamic world of trading. So, grab a cup of coffee, find a cozy spot, and start exploring these books. Your trading journey awaits!
