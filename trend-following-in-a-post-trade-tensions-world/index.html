Trend Following in a Post-Trade Tensions World


Global trade has been facing unprecedented challenges in recent years. Amidst rising geopolitical tensions, trade wars, and Brexit uncertainty, businesses and investors have been searching for more stable investment options. Enter trend following - a versatile investment strategy that can thrive in unpredictable times.

Understanding Trend Following

Trend following is a market-neutral investment strategy that involves the systematic purchase and sale of financial instruments, typically via futures contracts, in the direction of the prevailing trend of a particular market or asset class. This approach is guided by mathematical and statistical models, which automatically execute trades to amass profits, without giving any opinion on the market’s direction. The primary principle of trend following is that trends follow trends, thus, when successful, a consistent and cyclical system of exploitation arises.

Advantages in Post-Trade Tensions World

As traditional trade routes get more complicated, trend following offers a few notable advantages:

1. Resilience: Trend following strategies tend to be highly adaptable and can quickly change course in response to market cues, which can be crucial in times of volatility. Moreover, they have historically demonstrated less dependence on macroeconomic variables, such as GDP or monetary policy, so they can continue to perform well regardless of wider economic shifts.

2. Diversification: By investing across multiple markets and asset classes, trend following strategies diversify an investor’s exposure. Given the interconnectedness of the global economy, this helps hedge against emerging risks, thus boosting portfolio resilience.

3. Non-correlated returns: In contrast to traditional investments, trend following strategies are typically uncorrelated with broader equity market movements. This means they can provide a diversification and risk reduction option for investors with a well-balanced portfolio. It could act as ballast during turbulent times.

Implementing Trend Following Strategies

For investors entering the world of trend following, there are a few vital steps to remember:

1. Partner with a trusted expert: Given the nature of the strategies involved, a proper blend of technology and experienced human input can add value. Seek the assistance of reputable financial institutions or professional trend followers for guidance.

2. Establish clear objectives: Before diving into trend following, ensure you establish clear financial goals and risk appetite tailored to your needs. This will help you align your investment with the right strategy that resonates with your money management style.

3. Adopt a data-centric approach: A robust and reliable data source is crucial. Understand the underlying data sources and how they feed into and impact the strategy. Trend followers need to wrap their heads around vast and complex historical data to gain an edge.

The Future of Trend Following in a Post-Trade Tensions World

As global trade friction continues to transform the investment landscape, trend following is emerging as an attractive offering for those seeking stable returns and diversification. The strategy’s agility and multi-asset exposure make it an enticing option for investors navigating an increasingly complex world. With careful implementation and the right expertise, trend following could unlock a prosperous future for investors in post-trade tensions times.
