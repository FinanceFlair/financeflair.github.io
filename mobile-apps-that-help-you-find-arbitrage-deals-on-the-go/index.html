Mobile Apps That Help You Find Arbitrage Deals on the Go


In today’s fast-paced digital world, the ability to find profitable arbitrage opportunities while on the go is becoming more accessible thanks to the rise of mobile apps. Whether you’re an experienced investor or just someone looking for ways to make extra income, these apps allow you to identify price discrepancies across various markets, helping you make smart, profitable decisions with minimal effort. With just a few taps, you can find deals that maximize your returns by buying low and selling high. This article will explore some of the best mobile apps available that help you find arbitrage deals on the go.

What is Arbitrage?

Before diving into the apps that can help you spot arbitrage deals, it’s important to understand what arbitrage is and how it works. Arbitrage refers to the practice of buying and selling the same asset or product in different markets to take advantage of price differences. The goal is to buy at a lower price in one market and sell at a higher price in another, earning a profit in the process. This concept isn’t limited to financial markets; it can apply to anything from sneakers and electronics to real estate and even tickets for events.

For example, if you can buy a product on one e-commerce platform at a lower price than another, you could buy that product in bulk and resell it at a higher price on the other platform, earning a profit on the difference. The best part is that this can be done on a variety of platforms using tools that are now conveniently available in mobile app form.

Why Use Mobile Apps for Arbitrage?

Finding arbitrage opportunities traditionally required a lot of research and patience, often done manually by comparing prices across multiple platforms or marketplaces. With the advent of mobile apps, much of this work has been automated, making it easier to identify profitable arbitrage opportunities quickly and on the go.

Mobile apps offer several key benefits:

  • Accessibility: You can check prices and find opportunities anytime and anywhere, whether you’re commuting, traveling, or just out and about.
  • Speed: Mobile apps provide real-time data and price alerts, helping you act on opportunities as soon as they arise.
  • Convenience: Many apps integrate with multiple platforms, so you don’t have to manually search different websites or apps for deals.
  • Automation: Some apps can automatically track products and send you notifications when a price difference is detected, making the process more passive.

With that in mind, let’s dive into some of the top mobile apps that can help you find arbitrage deals and make smarter, more profitable decisions.

1. Scoutify by InventoryLab

Scoutify is a powerful mobile app designed for people looking to resell products, especially on platforms like Amazon. It’s an excellent tool for finding arbitrage deals while you’re out shopping or sourcing products in stores.

Key Features:

  • Barcode Scanning: The app allows you to scan barcodes of products in physical stores to instantly see their prices on Amazon. It compares current prices and estimates potential profits from reselling.
  • Profit Calculation: Scoutify calculates the potential profit after accounting for fees, shipping, and other expenses. This helps you make informed decisions before making a purchase.
  • Real-Time Data: The app pulls live pricing data from Amazon, so you always get up-to-date information about what a product is selling for.
  • Integration with Amazon: Since Scoutify integrates with Amazon’s marketplace, it’s easy to list items for sale directly after sourcing them, streamlining the entire process from purchase to resale.

Scoutify is ideal for people who want to engage in retail arbitrage, allowing them to make quick decisions and source products efficiently.

2. Keepa

Keepa is a well-known price tracking app that allows users to track historical price data on Amazon. This app is particularly helpful for spotting price fluctuations, which are essential for arbitrage deals.

Key Features:

  • Price History Charts: Keepa provides detailed price history charts for millions of Amazon products. You can see how a product’s price has changed over time, which can help you identify trends and patterns for profitable buys.
  • Price Alerts: Keepa allows you to set price alerts for specific products. You’ll be notified when the price drops to your desired level, which is essential for catching arbitrage opportunities.
  • Amazon Price Comparison: The app provides information about the lowest price available on Amazon, as well as price fluctuations between new and used items, allowing you to compare different product listings.

Keepa is perfect for those who want to make data-driven decisions and need a solid tool to help them track price trends over time.

3. Profit Bandit

Profit Bandit is another popular app for those involved in retail arbitrage. The app allows users to scan barcodes to instantly check the profitability of a product by comparing prices across multiple marketplaces.

Key Features:

  • Instant Profitability Analysis: Profit Bandit instantly provides a profitability analysis by factoring in Amazon fees, shipping, and other relevant costs. It also compares the prices across different marketplaces like eBay, Amazon, and Walmart.
  • Real-Time Updates: The app pulls live data from Amazon to give you up-to-the-minute price information, ensuring that you’re making decisions based on current market trends.
  • Amazon FBA Integration: Profit Bandit integrates with Amazon’s FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) program, allowing you to quickly determine whether a product is a good fit for reselling through FBA.

This app is a great choice for anyone looking to streamline their retail arbitrage efforts, especially those who prefer using Amazon FBA for their reselling business.

4. eBay

While eBay is primarily a marketplace, its mobile app can be an invaluable tool for arbitrage hunters. It allows you to compare prices, track product trends, and even discover hidden arbitrage opportunities that may not be as visible on other platforms.

Key Features:

  • Price Comparison: eBay’s app makes it easy to search for items and compare prices between various sellers. You can check current prices, view completed listings, and even track sales trends.
  • Auction Alerts: If you’re looking for specific items that are up for auction, the app allows you to set up alerts so you don’t miss out on potential arbitrage opportunities.
  • Seller Tools: eBay provides tools to help you analyze other sellers’ listings, making it easier to find gaps in pricing and identify where you can profit.

eBay’s app is useful for finding arbitrage deals not only within its marketplace but also across other platforms where eBay’s pricing might be more competitive. It’s a good tool for anyone looking to engage in online arbitrage.

5. Honey

Honey is a well-known app that helps users find the best discounts, but it can also be a fantastic tool for discovering arbitrage opportunities, especially when shopping online.

Key Features:

  • Price Tracking: Honey tracks prices on various e-commerce platforms and notifies you when a product goes on sale or hits a target price.
  • Coupon Codes: Honey automatically applies the best available coupon codes at checkout, which can lead to significant savings and opportunities for arbitrage.
  • Price History: Honey shows you the price history of products, allowing you to spot trends and identify when prices are likely to drop.

If you’re looking for an app that will help you find deals while shopping online, Honey is an excellent choice. It allows you to easily track prices and maximize savings, which is key to making successful arbitrage deals.

6. Flipp

Flipp is an app that aggregates weekly ads from major retailers and lets you browse current sales and discounts. This can be a great tool for finding local arbitrage deals, especially when it comes to buying products at a significant discount.

Key Features:

  • Weekly Circulars: Flipp aggregates flyers and ads from thousands of retailers, showing you exactly where the best deals are.
  • Price Comparison: You can compare prices across various retailers, ensuring you get the best deal on any product.
  • Digital Coupons: The app also allows you to use digital coupons, further increasing the potential for savings.

Flipp is great for those who are looking for local arbitrage opportunities, especially when retail prices vary significantly from one store to another.

7. PriceGrabber

PriceGrabber is a price comparison tool that allows you to compare prices for products across different online retailers. It can be particularly helpful for spotting arbitrage opportunities when buying and reselling products online.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive Search Engine: PriceGrabber provides a comprehensive search engine that pulls data from thousands of online stores, ensuring you always get the lowest possible prices.
  • Price Alerts: Just like Keepa, PriceGrabber allows you to set up price alerts for specific items, making it easier to act quickly when prices drop.
  • Product Reviews: In addition to price comparisons, PriceGrabber provides product reviews, which can help you evaluate whether an item is worth buying for resale.

This app is ideal for online arbitrage, as it simplifies the process of comparing prices across multiple platforms and helps you find the best deals with minimal effort.

Conclusion

Finding arbitrage opportunities while on the go has never been easier, thanks to these powerful mobile apps. Whether you’re looking to engage in retail arbitrage, online arbitrage, or just find great deals to resell, these apps provide the tools and data you need to make informed, profitable decisions. By utilizing these apps effectively, you can take advantage of price discrepancies, stay ahead of market trends, and maximize your profits—all from the convenience of your smartphone.

If you’re new to arbitrage or simply looking to streamline your process, these apps are a great place to start. With real-time data, price tracking, and easy-to-use interfaces, they’ll make your arbitrage endeavors more efficient and lucrative.
