Trend Following Podcasts: Learning on the Go


Are you tired of your daily commute being a waste of time? Do you wish you could make the most of those long drives or train rides? Well, look no further! Trend following podcasts are here to transform your daily commute into a valuable learning experience.

What is Trend Following?

Before we dive into the world of trend following podcasts, let’s first understand what trend following is all about. Trend following is an investment strategy that aims to capture long-term trends in financial markets. It involves identifying and following the direction of the market, regardless of the underlying fundamentals.

Trend following is not limited to the world of finance. It can be applied to various aspects of life, such as personal development, entrepreneurship, and even health and wellness. The core principle remains the same: identify trends and ride the wave.

Why Trend Following Podcasts?

Podcasts have exploded in popularity in recent years, and for good reason. They provide a convenient and accessible way to consume information and entertainment. Whether you’re driving to work, hitting the gym, or doing household chores, you can easily tune in to a podcast and make the most of your time.

Trend following podcasts take this convenience to a whole new level. They offer valuable insights, interviews with experts, and real-life examples of trend following success stories. By listening to these podcasts, you can learn from the best in the business and gain a deeper understanding of the trend following strategy.

Top Trend Following Podcasts

Now that you understand the benefits of trend following podcasts, let’s explore some of the top podcasts in this genre:

1. “The Trend Following Radio” by Michael Covel

Hosted by trend following expert Michael Covel, this podcast delves deep into the world of trend following. Covel interviews top trend following traders, authors, and thinkers, providing listeners with a wealth of knowledge and insights. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced investor, this podcast is a must-listen.

2. “Chat With Traders” by Aaron Fifield

While not solely focused on trend following, “Chat With Traders” features interviews with successful traders from various backgrounds, including trend following. Aaron Fifield’s conversational style and in-depth interviews make this podcast a valuable resource for anyone interested in trend following or trading in general.

3. “The Meb Faber Show” by Meb Faber

Hosted by renowned investor and author Meb Faber, this podcast covers a wide range of investment strategies, including trend following. Meb Faber interviews industry experts and shares his own insights, making this podcast a treasure trove of valuable information for trend followers.

4. “Systematic Investor Radio” by Meb Faber

Another gem by Meb Faber, “Systematic Investor Radio” focuses specifically on systematic and quantitative investing, which includes trend following. Faber dives deep into the strategies and techniques used by successful trend followers, providing listeners with actionable insights they can apply to their own investment approach.

5. “The Macro Voices Podcast” by Erik Townsend

While not solely dedicated to trend following, “The Macro Voices Podcast” covers a wide range of macroeconomic topics, including trend following strategies. Host Erik Townsend interviews leading experts in the field, providing listeners with a broader perspective on trend following and its implications in the global economy.

Conclusion

Trend following podcasts offer a unique opportunity to learn and grow while on the go. With their accessibility and valuable insights, these podcasts can transform your daily commute into a productive and enriching experience. So, grab your headphones, tune in to one of these trend following podcasts, and start learning on the go!

Remember, trend following is not limited to finance. It can be applied to various aspects of life. So, whether you’re interested in investing, personal development, or entrepreneurship, trend following podcasts have something for everyone. Happy listening!
