Best Trend Following Stocks to Watch Right Now


In an ever-evolving market landscape, identifying stocks that reliably follow trends is a strategic approach many investors swear by. Trend following, a strategy centered on capitalizing on sustained price movements, allows investors to ride the momentum of winning stocks rather than betting on reversals or speculative events. But which stocks are poised to continue their upward trajectories, and what makes them compelling choices for trend followers today?

This deep-dive explores some of the most promising trend-following stocks in the current market climate, analyzing their momentum, fundamentals, and what signals they send for the near to mid-term future.

Understanding Trend Following in Equity Markets

Before diving into specific stocks, let’s clarify what trend following implies in the context of equities. Unlike contrarian strategies, which try to buy low and sell high by predicting reversals, trend followers seek to enter trades after a given momentum has established itself. The rationale is simple: stocks that have demonstrated consistent movement in a direction tend to continue doing so until a clear change in market sentiment occurs.

Key technical indicators used by trend followers include moving averages (especially the 50-day and 200-day), Relative Strength Index (RSI), volume confirmation, and breakout patterns. Combined with fundamental context, these tools provide a blueprint to spot stocks with solid momentum.

Why Now Is a Great Time for Trend Following Strategies

The current investment environment is characterized by a mix of macroeconomic shifts — including fluctuating interest rates, evolving consumer trends, and technological advances — all of which contribute to the formation of new market trends. Additionally, investor sentiment has increasingly leaned towards sectors demonstrating clear growth drivers and resilience in volatile times, creating fertile ground for trend-following strategies to shine.

Understanding which companies are benefitting from these secular trends can reveal stocks that are more likely to sustain upward momentum.

Top Trend Following Stocks to Monitor in the Current Market

1. NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA)

NVIDIA has been a dominant force in the semiconductor and graphics processing industry. Its leadership in AI chips, data center components, and gaming has fueled a multi-quarter uptrend amplified by robust earnings beats. The stock remains above key long-term moving averages and continues to form higher highs.

NVIDIA’s recent partnerships in AI-driven applications and expanding product lines in autonomous vehicles add fundamental strength to the technical trend, making it a favored candidate for those who track momentum in tech.

2. Tesla, Inc. (TSLA)

Tesla’s volatile nature might intimidate some, but for trend followers, it offers ample momentum play opportunities. The company’s advancements in battery technology, production scaling, and expansion in energy solutions give a sturdy backbone to its price trends.

Tesla’s stock often follows extended runs tied to production milestones or innovation breakthroughs and has shown the ability to hold technical support levels before surging higher, reinforcing its status as a stock to watch during trending movements.

3. Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)

AMD continues to disrupt the chip manufacturing industry with competitive technology and aggressive market share gains. The shift towards hybrid work environments and gaming bolsters demand for AMD’s processors and graphics units.

From a trend perspective, AMD regularly forms clear breakouts from consolidation phases, supported by improving fundamentals and rising institutional interest. Its relative strength compared to peers is another marker that trend followers appreciate.

4. Shopify Inc. (SHOP)

E-commerce remains a critical growth sector, with Shopify at the forefront of enabling online retail for businesses of all sizes. Despite the sector’s cyclical volatility, Shopify’s strategic platform improvements and international expansion provide momentum signals that investors can monitor.

Shopify’s stock tends to track well with digital commerce trends and is characterized by periods of strong volume-driven upward moves, highlighting it as an actionable option for momentum-based strategies.

5. Enphase Energy, Inc. (ENPH)

Renewable energy has seen a surge in investor interest, and Enphase Energy is a key player in solar technology and energy management solutions. Enphase’s growth trajectory aligns with accelerating demand for sustainable energy products, positioning it well within trend-following frameworks.

The stock consistently trades above its 50-day and 200-day moving averages during rally phases and is notable for its capacity to form ascending triangular patterns, technical signs that trend traders find compelling.

6. CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc. (CRWD)

In cybersecurity, CrowdStrike stands out for its cloud-native platform and impressive growth metrics. The increasing importance of digital defenses has created a long runway for revenue and market expansion, which in turn supports positive price trends.

Trend followers note CrowdStrike’s ability to bounce from key support levels with higher volume, indicating ongoing investor conviction and potential for sustained price advances.

Evaluating Trend Sustainability

While identifying stocks with strong momentum is a crucial first step, the longevity of these trends depends on several factors:

  • Earnings and Revenue Growth: A sustained upward trend is more likely if earnings consistently meet or exceed expectations.
  • Sector Catalysts: Macroeconomic, regulatory, or technological catalysts can reinforce trends or cause abrupt changes. Renewable energy, cybersecurity, and fintech are examples of sectors with active tailwinds.
  • Volume Confirmation: High trading volume during upward price moves typically suggests stronger trend validity.
  • Macro Environment: Interest rates, inflation data, and geopolitical developments often influence market-wide sentiment.

Monitoring these factors alongside technical trends helps investors avoid chasing false breakouts or temporary rallies.

Risk Considerations in Trend Following

Trend following is not devoid of risks. Stocks can reverse abruptly, particularly in volatile sectors like technology or energy, where news and regulation impact sentiment quickly. Momentum can also dwindle if broader market conditions shift or if stock valuations become stretched relative to fundamentals.

Thus, effective trend following often incorporates stop-loss levels and position sizing to limit downside. Diversifying across multiple strong trends can also reduce idiosyncratic risk inherent to any single stock.

Setting Up for Success: Tools and Indicators to Track

For investors interested in harnessing trend following strategies, several tools can enhance decision-making:

  • Moving Averages: The crossover of shorter-term moving averages above longer-term ones (e.g., 50-day crossing 200-day) signals potential uptrends.
  • Relative Strength Index (RSI): An RSI between 50 and 70 suggests upward momentum without being overbought.
  • Volume Trend: Rising volume on increasing prices validates trend strength.
  • Breakout Confirmation: Confirming breakouts beyond resistance levels with follow-through movement strengthens trend signals.
  • Fundamental Updates: Earnings releases and guidance revisions can alter momentum trajectories.

Combining these tools with clear entry and exit plans builds a robust framework for trend following success.

Final Thoughts

Trend following remains a compelling strategy for capturing sustained gains in dynamic market environments. Stocks like NVIDIA, Tesla, AMD, Shopify, Enphase Energy, and CrowdStrike illustrate how technology, renewables, e-commerce, and cybersecurity sectors present meaningful momentum opportunities. The common thread among these companies is a blend of strong fundamentals, favorable sector dynamics, and technical signals supporting continued uptrends.

By carefully analyzing both price action and underlying business health, investors can position themselves to ride current trends with greater confidence. Keeping an eye on evolving news and maintaining disciplined risk management ensures that these trend following endeavors are both exciting and strategically sound.

Stay attuned to market shifts, trust the trend signals, and be prepared to adapt—momentum favors the prepared.
