Trend Following Success Stories: Inspiring Traders


In the world of trading, there are countless strategies and approaches that traders use to navigate the complex and ever-changing financial markets. One such strategy that has gained attention and recognition is trend following. Trend following is a trading approach that involves identifying and following the direction of the market’s trend to make profitable trades. While it may sound simple in theory, successful trend following requires discipline, patience, and a deep understanding of market dynamics. In this blog post, we will explore some inspiring trend following success stories of traders who have achieved remarkable results through this strategy.

The Legendary Trend Followers

Richard Dennis and William Eckhardt

One of the most famous trend following success stories dates back to the 1980s when legendary traders Richard Dennis and William Eckhardt conducted an experiment to prove that successful trading could be taught. They selected a group of individuals, known as the “Turtle Traders,” and taught them their trend following methodology. The results were astonishing, with the Turtle Traders collectively earning over $100 million in just a few years. This experiment demonstrated the power of disciplined trend following and the potential for significant financial gains.

Michael Marcus

Another inspiring success story is that of Michael Marcus, who turned a $30,000 trading account into over $80 million by following trends in the currency and commodity markets. Marcus attributed his success to his ability to identify and ride long-term trends, allowing his winning trades to run and cutting losses quickly. His story serves as a testament to the potential rewards of patient and disciplined trend following.

Modern Trend Following Titans

David Harding

David Harding, the founder of Winton Capital Management, is a prominent figure in the world of trend following. His systematic trend following approach has propelled Winton Capital Management to become one of the largest and most successful hedge funds globally. Harding’s ability to adapt to changing market conditions and his unwavering commitment to trend following has solidified his reputation as a pioneer in the industry.

Jerry Parker

Jerry Parker, also known as the “Trend Following Wizard,” is another remarkable success story in the world of trend following. After being trained by Richard Dennis as one of the original Turtle Traders, Parker went on to establish his own successful trend following firm, Chesapeake Capital. His adherence to systematic trend following principles and his ability to stay emotionally detached from market fluctuations have contributed to his enduring success over the years.

Key Takeaways

These inspiring trend following success stories underscore the potential for significant wealth creation through disciplined and patient trading. While trend following is not without its challenges, the stories of Richard Dennis, William Eckhardt, Michael Marcus, David Harding, and Jerry Parker serve as compelling examples of the rewards that can be reaped by adhering to this timeless trading strategy. Patience, discipline, risk management, and the ability to let winning trades run are recurring themes in these success stories, highlighting the essential principles of successful trend following.

In conclusion, trend following has proven to be a powerful and enduring trading strategy, as evidenced by the remarkable success stories of traders who have harnessed its potential. By studying these success stories, aspiring traders can gain valuable insights into the mindset and practices that underpin successful trend following. As the financial markets continue to evolve, the timeless principles of trend following will undoubtedly continue to inspire and guide traders in their pursuit of trading success.
