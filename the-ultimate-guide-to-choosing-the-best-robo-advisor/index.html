The Ultimate Guide to Choosing the Best Robo Advisor


Robo advisors are revolutionizing the world of investment management. They offer a low-cost, simple, and efficient way for individuals to invest their money. With so many options available, choosing the best robo advisor can be a daunting task. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of selecting the right robo advisor for your investment needs.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Robo Advisor

  1. Fees: The cost of robo advisors varies depending on the provider and the services they offer. Generally, most robo advisors charge an annual fee for managing your investment portfolio. It’s essential to understand these fees and whether they align with your financial goals and budget.

  2. Investment Strategies: Different robo advisors use various investment strategies. Some may rely on solely algorithm-based automated investments, while others might offer a hybrid approach that combines human guidance with technology. Choose a robo advisor that aligns with your investment objectives and risk tolerance.

  3. Minimum Deposit: The minimum deposit required to open an account varies between robo advisors. Some have no minimum requirement, while others may require thousands of dollars to get started. It’s crucial to consider if their minimum deposit suits your financial situation and goals.

  4. Account Types: Robo advisors offer a range of account types, such as individual, joint, and retirement accounts. Choose a robo advisor that offers the account type you need.

  5. Customer Support: Good customer support is essential in ensuring a positive investment experience. Consider a robo advisor that offers multiple support channels, such as email, phone, or live chat, to address your concerns efficiently.

  6. Insured and Regulated: Before choosing a robo advisor, ensure the company is regulated and insured to protect your investments. This ensures your money is safe and secure.

Top Robo Advisors of 2021

Now that you know what factors to consider, here’s our list of the best robo advisors for different investment needs:

  1. Betterment: Betterment is a popular choice for young investors just starting out. Their low-cost, automated investment strategy helps build a diversified portfolio with minimal human intervention. They offer retirement and tax-coordinated investing to help make the most of your investments.

  2. ** Wealthfront:** Wealthfront has a low-cost, automated investment strategy that caters to all investors. The company is known for its advanced technology and includes features like tax-loss harvesting and optimization. They also have a high minimum deposit of $100,000.

  3. Vestwell: Vestwell is an excellent option for beginner investors looking for low-cost investment options. This robo advisor is unique in that it allows users to invest in individual stocks and ETFs, alongside their automated investment strategy. Their starting deposit is only $50.

  4. Fidelity Robo Advisor: Fidelity is a well-respected name in the investment industry. Their robo advisor offers a low-cost, automated investment strategy and is suitable for all investors. Fidelity’s strength lies in its extensive selection of no-transaction fee mutual funds.

  5. Schwab Intelligent Investment: Schwab’s robo advisor is an excellent option for long-term investors who want to invest in low-cost ETFs. Schwab also offers access to professional financial consultants for personalized investment advice for an additional fee.

Choosing the Right Robo Advisor

Choosing the best robo advisor comes down to matching your investment goals with the right service provider. We’ve outlined the essential factors to consider when choosing a robo advisor and shared our picks for the top providers in 2021.

As with all investments, it’s vital to do your research and weigh the pros and cons of each robo advisor before deciding. With the right roinator, you’ll be on your way to achieving your financial goals today and in the future.
