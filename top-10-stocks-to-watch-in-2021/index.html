Top 10 Stocks to Watch in 2021


As we transition into a new year, it’s essential to identify promising investments that can bring significant returns. With countless stocks available for investment, narrowing down the list of potential winners can be overwhelming. In this article, we will explore ten such stocks that have the potential to become market leaders in 2021.

1. Microsoft Corp (MSFT)

Microsoft has consistently demonstrated its ability to adapt to market changes, positioning itself as an industry leader in cloud computing and artificial intelligence. With the digital transformation accelerating due to the ongoing pandemic, the demand for Microsoft’s diverse product offerings in areas like software, hardware, and cloud services is likely to remain robust. As a result, MSFT stock is deserving of attention from any savvy investor looking for long-term growth.

2. Amazon.com, Inc (AMZN)

Amazon has flourished in recent years, driven by its dominance in e-commerce and expansion into new marketplaces, including the smart homes and cloud segments. The increasing demand for contactless shopping and Amazon’s entrance into the food industry through its purchase of Whole Foods Market position the company for continued success.

3. Alphabet Inc (GOOGL)

As the parent company of Google, Alphabet sits at the helm of a tech empire. With their impressive suite of products, from Google Search and Chrome to Google Cloud and Android, Alphabet’s growth appears unstoppable. As investors, we should keep a close eye on Alphabet’s upcoming innovations and how they shape the future of the technology market.

4. Apple Inc (AAPL)

Apple, despite its current market capitalization, continues to expand its product offerings, capturing a growing share of the smartphone, computer, and streaming device markets. With an ever-growing customer base and the launch of new products like the Apple Watch Series 6 and the affordable iPad, Apple remains a strong contender for any investor’s portfolio.

5. Tesla, Inc (TSLA)

Tesla’s groundbreaking advances in electric vehicle technology have propelled the company into the spotlight. With its ambitious expansion into renewable energy and the anticipated release of its more affordable electric vehicles, investors should keep a close eye on the progress of this industry-leading automaker.

6. Advanced Micro Devices, Inc (AMD)

The shifting focus towards data centers and advanced electronics is pushing this semiconductor company to the forefront of the industry. With a focus on race-changing processors and graphics cards, investors could see significant returns from AMD’s continued success.

7. Zoom Video Communications, Inc (ZM)

In the midst of the pandemic, video conferencing has become more vital than ever. Zoom’s seamless integration of virtual meetings and remote collaboration has bolstered the demand for their services. Zoom’s adaptability to virtual communication needs makes it a key player in the tech market as remote work and learning are likely to remain prevalent in our post-pandemic world.

8. Doing Business (DQBB)

A newcomer to the stock market, Doing Business has impressed with its rapid growth and innovative platform. While still in its early stages, the company leverages cutting-edge technology to assist businesses in their growth, making it an exciting prospect for long-term investments.

9. BlackBerry Limited (BB)

BlackBerry’s transition from hardware to software has proven successful, and it continues to focus on cybersecurity and the Internet of Things (IoT). As businesses grapple with an increasingly connected world, BlackBerry’s expertise is likely to become more pivotal by the year, making it a must-watch stock.

10. Adobe Inc (ADBE)

Adobe is a legendary name in software and computer graphics, catering to a market that shows no signs of slowing down. With their flagship products like Photoshop and Illustrator, Adobe remains a dominant player in the creative software space. Additionally, Adobe’s digital marketing tools continue to attract businesses aiming to capitalize on online growth, making it an attractive investment prospect.

In conclusion, the stocks mentioned in this article have the potential to shape the future of technology and business. It’s essential to remain vigilant and informed about market changes to identify valuable opportunities for future investments. With the right knowledge and research, these ten companies could deliver excellent returns and establish themselves as the industry leaders of tomorrow.
