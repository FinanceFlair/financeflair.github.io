The Best Apps for Earning Passive Income in 2023


In 2023, the concept of earning passive income has evolved with the rapid advancements in technology, mobile applications, and online platforms. Whether you’re looking to diversify your income streams, achieve financial freedom, or simply make extra money while you sleep, there are numerous apps available to help you achieve your goals. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best apps to help you earn passive income in 2023 and how they can fit into your lifestyle.

Understanding Passive Income

Before diving into the best apps, it’s essential to understand what passive income is. Passive income refers to money earned with little to no effort once an initial setup or investment is made. Unlike active income, which requires continuous effort (like a 9-to-5 job), passive income allows individuals to generate revenue with minimal time investment.

Some of the most common sources of passive income include rental properties, dividend stocks, royalties from creative works, and more. However, in the modern age, mobile apps have opened up even more opportunities to earn passive income on a daily basis.

1. Sweatcoin: Earn by Walking

If you’re someone who loves to stay active and walk regularly, Sweatcoin is an app that can reward you for it. This app tracks your steps and converts them into “Sweatcoins,” a type of virtual currency. You can then exchange your Sweatcoins for various rewards, including products, services, or even cash.

Though you won’t become wealthy simply by walking, Sweatcoin offers an interesting way to monetize your daily steps while improving your fitness. The more you walk, the more coins you accumulate, creating a simple form of passive income.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the Sweatcoin app and create an account.
  • Start walking, and the app will automatically track your steps and reward you with Sweatcoins.
  • Once you accumulate enough coins, you can trade them for rewards like gift cards or services.

2. Honeygain: Make Money by Sharing Your Internet Connection

Honeygain is one of the most popular apps that allow you to earn passive income by sharing your unused internet bandwidth. Once you install Honeygain on your device, the app will use your unused internet connection to gather data for various businesses. In return, you receive credits, which can be converted into cash.

This app works best when your device is connected to Wi-Fi and you’re not actively using the internet, making it a truly passive way to earn money. While you won’t make a huge fortune with Honeygain, it’s a great way to passively earn a little extra each month.

How to Get Started:

  • Download and install Honeygain on your phone or computer.
  • Set it up, and let it run in the background.
  • The more data you share, the more you can earn.

3. Acorns: Invest and Grow Your Wealth Automatically

Acorns is an excellent app for beginners who are looking to start investing with minimal effort. The app rounds up your everyday purchases to the nearest dollar and invests the difference in a diversified portfolio. This allows you to invest small amounts of money without even thinking about it. Over time, those small investments add up, and you can generate passive income through dividends and capital gains.

Acorns is ideal for people who want to start investing but don’t know where to begin. By automating your investment strategy, Acorns takes the stress out of managing your finances.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the Acorns app and link your bank account or credit card.
  • Choose your preferred investment portfolio based on your risk tolerance.
  • Let Acorns automatically invest for you, and watch your wealth grow over time.

4. Fundrise: Real Estate Investing Made Easy

Real estate has always been a popular source of passive income, but it’s often out of reach for many due to high upfront costs. Fundrise offers a solution by allowing users to invest in real estate properties with as little as $10. The app pools together funds from various investors to finance real estate projects and pays out dividends to investors based on the success of those projects.

Fundrise offers different types of real estate investments, including both residential and commercial properties. The app also offers both short-term and long-term investment strategies, making it a versatile option for investors of all types.

How to Get Started:

  • Download Fundrise and create an account.
  • Browse through available investment opportunities and choose the one that aligns with your financial goals.
  • Deposit your funds, and Fundrise will handle the rest, allowing you to earn passive income through dividends.

5. Stash: Simple Investing for Beginners

Stash is another app designed for those who are new to investing. Similar to Acorns, Stash allows you to start investing in stocks and ETFs (Exchange Traded Funds) with as little as $5. However, Stash stands out by providing more control over your investment choices, allowing you to pick specific stocks or investment portfolios that align with your values.

Stash also offers educational resources to help users learn about investing, making it a great choice for beginners. As you continue to invest, you can earn passive income through dividends, which can be reinvested to compound your earnings over time.

How to Get Started:

  • Download Stash and sign up for an account.
  • Fund your account with a small amount of money (starting with $5).
  • Choose an investment portfolio or individual stocks based on your preferences.

6. TaskRabbit: Earn by Helping Others

While TaskRabbit may not seem like a traditional “passive” income app, it offers a unique way to earn money by completing simple tasks for others. From assembling furniture to running errands, TaskRabbit allows you to offer your services to people in your area. While you’ll need to actively perform tasks, the app allows you to set your schedule and work as much or as little as you want.

One of the key features of TaskRabbit is the ability to work on your own terms. Whether you need extra cash or want to build a side hustle, TaskRabbit makes it easy to earn money without a long-term commitment.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the TaskRabbit app and create a profile.
  • Browse through available tasks in your area.
  • Select tasks that fit your schedule and expertise, and start earning money.

7. Airbnb: Rent Out Extra Space

If you own a property or have extra space in your home, Airbnb offers a fantastic opportunity to earn passive income. Whether you’re renting out a spare bedroom or an entire property, Airbnb allows you to list your space for short-term rentals. This app has revolutionized the way people travel, making it easy for hosts to earn extra income without much effort.

The income you can generate from Airbnb depends on several factors, such as location, demand, and property size. However, once you set up your listing, the process is relatively hands-off, making it an excellent source of passive income.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the Airbnb app and create an account.
  • List your property, set the rental price, and specify availability.
  • Let Airbnb handle bookings and guest communication while you earn money from rentals.

8. YouTube: Earn Through Ad Revenue and Sponsorships

For those who enjoy creating videos, YouTube remains one of the best platforms for generating passive income. Once you’ve uploaded videos and built an audience, YouTube monetizes your content through ad revenue, channel memberships, and even sponsorship deals.

Although growing a channel can take time, the passive income potential is enormous. Once your videos gain traction, they can continue earning money long after you’ve uploaded them. Additionally, YouTube offers creators the opportunity to diversify their income streams with affiliate marketing and merchandise sales.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the YouTube app and create a channel.
  • Start creating and uploading videos that align with your interests or niche.
  • Apply for monetization once you meet YouTube’s requirements (1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours).
  • Earn revenue through ads, sponsorships, and memberships.

9. Rover: Make Money by Walking Dogs

For animal lovers, Rover is an excellent app to earn passive income while spending time with pets. Rover connects pet owners with individuals who can provide services like dog walking, pet sitting, or pet boarding. Once you’ve built a reputation and gotten positive reviews, you can earn money on autopilot by accepting dog walking or pet sitting jobs.

Rover allows you to set your own schedule, and with the right clients, you can generate steady passive income. The app also provides insurance coverage, so you can feel secure while working with pets.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the Rover app and create a profile.
  • Offer your pet services, such as dog walking or sitting.
  • Start accepting jobs and earning money.

10. Turo: Rent Out Your Car

If you own a car that you don’t use all the time, Turo allows you to rent it out to others for short-term rentals. This is especially lucrative in cities with high demand for rental cars, and Turo takes care of the insurance and booking process. You can set your own price and availability, making it a flexible way to earn passive income from your vehicle.

How to Get Started:

  • Download the Turo app and list your car.
  • Set the rental price and availability.
  • Rent out your car to others, earning money without much effort.

Conclusion

Earning passive income in 2023 is more accessible than ever, thanks to the wide variety of apps that cater to different needs, interests, and investment preferences. Whether you’re looking to monetize your spare time, invest in real estate, or earn money by sharing your internet connection, there’s an app out there to help you get started.

By leveraging these apps, you can generate consistent passive income that can supplement your regular earnings, build long-term wealth, and provide greater financial freedom. The key is to pick the right apps based on your lifestyle, goals, and available time, and start earning money on autopilot.
