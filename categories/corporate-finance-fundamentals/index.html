Net Present Value (NPV) is a fundamental capital budgeting technique that focuses on determining the present value of cash flows associated with a potential investment. By discounting future cash flows back to their present value using a predetermined discount rate, financial managers can assess whether an investment is expected to generate positive or negative returns.
