Understanding Delta Hedging: Managing Options and Risk Exposure

Delta hedging is a risk management technique used in the options market to reduce or eliminate the exposure to changes in the price of the underlying asset. It involves adjusting the position in the underlying asset to offset the changes in the value of the options position. By understanding delta hedging, investors can effectively manage their options positions and mitigate potential losses. What is Delta? Delta is a measure of the sensitivity of an option’s price to changes in the price of the underlying asset. It represents the change in the option’s price for a one-point change in the price of the underlying asset. Delta is expressed as a number between -1 and +1 for call options and between -1 and 0 for put options. ...
