Why Keeping Investors Informed is Crucial Communication with investors is essential to build trust and establish a healthy relationship. Recent studies have shown that portfolio companies with effective communication have exhibited higher rates of success and enjoy improved perceptions of their shareholders. Conversely, investors often abandon businesses that fail to provide clear updates, due to a feeling of being left in the dark. Depending on the investor's preference, set reminders to discuss year-on-year growth, share portfolio status, or highlight emerging opportunities.
