Crowdfunding for Outdoor Gear: Platforms that Fuel Adventure


Whether you’re an experienced outdoor enthusiast or a beginner looking to embark on your first adventure, having the right gear is essential. From hiking boots to camping tents, outdoor gear can be quite expensive, making it challenging for many people to pursue their passions. This is where crowdfunding platforms come in, offering a unique opportunity to fundraise for outdoor gear and turn dreams into reality.

How Crowdfunding Works

Before we dive into the specific platforms, let’s take a moment to understand how crowdfunding works. Crowdfunding is a way for individuals or organizations to raise money for a particular project or cause. It involves soliciting small contributions from a large number of people, usually through an online platform.

In the context of outdoor gear, crowdfunding allows adventurers to create campaigns to fundraise for the gear they need. They can share their stories, goals, and aspirations with potential supporters, who can contribute any amount they desire. In return, supporters often receive rewards such as discounts, exclusive merchandise, or even the opportunity to join the adventure.

Platforms that Fuel Adventure

Now that we have a grasp of the concept, let’s explore some crowdfunding platforms that specifically cater to outdoor gear enthusiasts:

1. Kickstarter

Kickstarter is one of the most popular crowdfunding platforms out there, known for supporting a wide range of creative projects. While not exclusively focused on outdoor gear, Kickstarter has successfully helped many adventurers bring their gear dreams to life. From innovative camping gadgets to sustainable hiking apparel, you can find it all on Kickstarter.

2. Indiegogo

Indiegogo is another well-known crowdfunding platform that provides a space for creators and entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas. With a dedicated section for outdoor gear projects, Indiegogo offers a variety of campaigns to support. Whether you’re searching for a high-tech backpack or a groundbreaking outdoor cooking system, Indiegogo has you covered.

3. GoFundMe

Although GoFundMe is primarily known for personal fundraising campaigns, it can also be a fantastic platform for adventurers seeking to acquire outdoor gear. GoFundMe allows individuals to tell their stories, connect with like-minded people, and raise funds for their outdoor pursuits. Whether you’re planning a thru-hike of the Appalachian Trail or a cross-country cycling adventure, GoFundMe can help you get the gear you need.

4. Patreon

Patreon is a unique crowdfunding platform that focuses on building a community around creators. While not specifically designed for outdoor gear campaigns, Patreon can be an excellent option for adventurers who want ongoing support from their audience. By offering different membership tiers, adventurers can provide exclusive content, behind-the-scenes access, and the opportunity for supporters to be a part of their journey.

The Power of Community

What sets crowdfunding platforms apart from traditional methods of funding is the power of community. These platforms bring people together who share a passion for the outdoors, creating a supportive network that goes beyond financial contributions. Backers often become cheerleaders, mentors, and friends, providing motivation and guidance throughout the adventure.

So, if you’re an outdoor enthusiast looking to fuel your adventure, consider exploring crowdfunding platforms. Not only can you raise the funds you need for your gear, but you’ll also connect with a community that shares your love for the great outdoors.

Remember, crowdfunding is about more than just the money. It’s about building relationships, telling your story, and inspiring others to pursue their own outdoor dreams. So, start your campaign today and let the adventure begin!
