Understanding Algorithmic Trading

Algorithmic trading, also known as algo-trading, utilizes mathematical models and algorithms to make trading decisions. These algorithms are programmed to analyze market data, such as price movements and volume, and automatically execute trades at optimal times and prices. By leveraging algorithmic trading, traders can remove human emotions from the decision-making process and react to market conditions swiftly.
