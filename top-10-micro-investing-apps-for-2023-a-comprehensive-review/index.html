Top 10 Micro-Investing Apps for 2023: A Comprehensive Review


Investing has evolved significantly in recent years, making it accessible to people from all walks of life. In the past, investing in stocks, bonds, and other assets required large amounts of capital and extensive financial knowledge. However, thanks to the rise of micro-investing apps, individuals can now begin their investing journey with as little as a few dollars. These apps allow users to invest small amounts of money, typically rounding up everyday purchases to the nearest dollar and investing the difference in a portfolio of stocks, ETFs, or other financial instruments.

Micro-investing has democratized the world of personal finance, offering a way for people to grow their wealth slowly but steadily. With 2023 shaping up to be a dynamic year for investment, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 micro-investing apps that are making waves. These apps offer intuitive user interfaces, low fees, and features that cater to both beginners and experienced investors alike.

1. Acorns

Acorns is one of the most well-known micro-investing apps on the market. It works by rounding up your purchases to the nearest dollar and investing the spare change in a diversified portfolio. You can choose from several investment portfolios based on your risk tolerance and financial goals.

Key Features:

  • Round-ups: Automatically rounds up purchases and invests the difference.
  • Found Money: Offers cashback from popular retailers when you make purchases.
  • Easy Portfolio Selection: Acorns offers various portfolios that range from conservative to aggressive, allowing users to select one that aligns with their financial goals.
  • Educational Resources: Acorns provides a wealth of articles and videos to help users understand the world of investing.

Pricing:

Acorns offers several pricing tiers, ranging from $3 to $5 per month, depending on the features you need. For those looking to start small with little upfront investment, this app is a fantastic option.

Best For:

  • Beginners looking to invest without a large initial deposit.
  • Users who want to save automatically while going about their daily activities.

2. Stash

Stash is a popular app that focuses on making investing simple and accessible to everyone. It allows you to start investing with as little as $5 and offers a variety of customizable investment options, including stocks, ETFs, and even fractional shares.

Key Features:

  • Fractional Shares: Invest in big-name companies like Amazon and Apple, even with a small budget.
  • Educational Content: Stash offers a lot of learning materials to help you understand the basics of investing.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Based on your risk tolerance and financial goals, Stash will provide personalized investment options.
  • Automatic Investing: You can set up automatic contributions, helping you stay disciplined in your investing journey.

Pricing:

Stash offers plans that start at $1 per month, with additional tiers for more features like retirement accounts and financial planning tools.

Best For:

  • Users who want an app that combines investing with education.
  • People who are just starting to invest and want flexibility in their portfolio choices.

3. Robinhood

Robinhood has become synonymous with commission-free trading, which makes it a favorite among both new and seasoned investors. While it started as a platform for trading individual stocks, it now also offers options for micro-investing, especially for users looking to invest in fractional shares.

Key Features:

  • Commission-Free Trades: No fees for buying or selling stocks, ETFs, or cryptocurrency.
  • Fractional Shares: Allows users to buy partial shares in expensive stocks like Amazon or Tesla.
  • Cash Management: Robinhood also offers a cash management feature, allowing you to earn interest on your uninvested funds.
  • Simple Interface: The app is user-friendly, which is a plus for new investors.

Pricing:

Robinhood offers a free basic account with the option to upgrade to Robinhood Gold for $5 per month. Robinhood Gold unlocks additional features, such as access to research and margin trading.

Best For:

  • Active investors who want to trade without paying fees.
  • Those interested in cryptocurrency investing alongside stocks.
