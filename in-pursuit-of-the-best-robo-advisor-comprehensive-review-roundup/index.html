In Pursuit of the Best Robo Advisor: Comprehensive Review Roundup


Robo advisors have grown in popularity over the past decade as an accessible, cost-effective way for individuals to manage their investment portfolios. However, there are so many options available on the market that it takes a lot of research and effort to find the perfect robo advisor that fits your unique financial goals and needs. This comprehensive review roundup aims to assist you in making that important decision.

Ranking Criteria

Before we dive into the roundup, it is crucial to establish a set of criteria to rank each robo advisor. Our key factors of consideration are:

  1. Fees
  2. Investment strategy
  3. User experience
  4. Account features
  5. Customer support

Each robo advisor’s score is determined by evaluating its performance against these parameters, resulting in an overall rating out of five starring.

The Best Robo Advisors on the Market

  1. Blooom: Founded in 2013, Blooom offers an affordable medium for managing investments with user-friendly tools and an intuitive design.

    • Fees: Starting at $30 per year
    • Investment Strategy: ETFs focused on indexing, risk-based planning, and optimization
    • User Experience: Easy to navigate and highly responsive customer support
    • Account Features: Access to daily optimization and rebalancing
    • Customer Support: Available via chat, phone, and email

  2. Wealthfront: A pioneer in the robo-advising industry, Wealthfront has consistently delivered excellent results for its clients.

    • Fees: Starting at 0.43% annually for manageable assets, decreasing to 0.25% for larger balances
    • Investment Strategy: Smart Beta Portfolio based on Modern Portfolio Theory, with tax-minimization strategies and portfolio adjustments
    • User Experience: Intuitive and attractive interface, easy to use
    • Account Features: Dedicated portfolio management, direct indexing, and more
    • Customer Support: Excellent resources available, with responsive client support

  3. Betterment: Betterment is another trailblazer in the robo-advising industry that provides a wide range of solutions for diverse client goals.

    • Fees: Starting at 0.30% annually for Digital investing, 0.40-0.91% for Premium Investing (including human advisors)
    • Investment Strategy: Index funds and ETFs, tax-optimized portfolios, and personalized recommendations
    • User Experience: Excellent platform with robust features and easy navigation
    • Account Features: Goals-based investing, sucxFFFh an retirement savings plan, and more
    • Customer Support: Available through platform support, live chat, and email

  4. Vestwell: This robo advisor is most appealing to new and inexperienced investors looking for a streamlined and supportive experience.

    • Fees: Starting at 0.24% annually
    • Investment Strategy: ETFs and index funds, low-cost investment approach, user-guided risk analysis
    • User Experience: Clean and clear interface with useful features for beginners
    • Account Features: Fractional shares, automatic diversification, portfolio rebalancing
    • Customer Support: Available through chat, phone, and email

  5. Schwab Intelligent Portfolio: Schwab has been a go-to choice for investors looking for a robust, low-cost investment platform.

    • Fees: Starting at 0.25% annually
    • Investment Strategy: ETFs and a blend of low-cost index funds, target risk investing approach, and human insights

    • Customer Support: Accessible through chat, phone, and email
