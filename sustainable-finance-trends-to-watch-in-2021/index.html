Sustainable Finance Trends to Watch in 2021


In recent years, sustainable finance has emerged as a powerful tool to drive positive change in the face of climate change, social inequality, and economic instability. As we embark upon a new year, let us explore seven significant sustainable finance trends that are set to shape the future of investments, banking, and enterprise.

1. Impact Investing

Impact investing, the practice of investing funds into companies or projects with the primary goal of generating both financial returns and positive social or environmental impact, continues to gain traction. In the coming year, more institutional investors, pension funds, and individuals are poised to allocate a substantial proportion of their assets to impact investments.

2. ESG Investment Integration

ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investing, born out of the growing acknowledgment of sustainability’s importance in investment decision making, will further penetrate the investment world in 2021. As numerous firms integrate ESG considerations into their investment analysis and portfolio management, ESG-themed investments are expected to attract a growing number of investors.

3. Cleantech Revitalization

Cleantech, the application of clean and sustainable technology to industries such as energy, agriculture, and mobility, has been permeating the global investment landscape since its emergence in the early 2000s. In 2021, the cleantech sector is set for a revitalization, receiving a significant boost from governments seeking to meet the ambitious Paris Agreement targets.

4. Sustainable Banking and Financial Products

Sustainable banking practices and financial products, fueled by a rising consumer demand for sustainable choices, will become more prevalent in 2021. As banks and financial institutions continue to develop sustainable banking policies and investment products, including green loans, Bonds, and certificates, a new era of sustainable finance is set to evolve.

5. Sustainable Infrastructure Development

From renewable energy projects and sustainable real estate developments to efficient public transportation systems, sustainable infrastructure has a vital role to play in achieving a more sustainable future. In 2021, expect to see a significant flow of funds into the development of environmentally friendly infrastructure, backed by a growing awareness of the importance of sustainable infrastructure for a greener, more prosperous future.

6. Green Finance Standardization

Moving forward, a harmonized definition and standardization of green finance is essential for investor confidence, market transparency, and effective climate action. In 2021, regulatory and industry bodies are anticipated to make strides toward the development of a consistent global approach to green finance, embedding sustainability principles at the heart of investment and banking activities.

7. Technology-Driven Sustainable Finance

As the world becomes increasingly digital, technology-driven sustainable finance solutions are set to reshape the investment landscape. From blockchain-based ESG certifications to AI-assisted sustainable investment advisory, technology is enabling financial firms to optimize their sustainable finance activities, uncover new investment opportunities, and achieve a greener financial system.

In conclusion, 2021 promises to be a pivotal year for sustainable finance, witnessing the emergence of innovative solutions and the continued commitment of investors, banks, and enterprises to realize a more sustainable future. As a responsible investor, it is crucial to engage with this evolving landscape and seek ways to incorporate sustainability considerations into your investment choices and lifestyle.
