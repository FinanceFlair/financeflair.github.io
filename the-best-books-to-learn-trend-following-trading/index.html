The Best Books to Learn Trend Following Trading


Trend following trading remains one of the most popular and effective trading strategies in the financial markets. Its appeal lies in its simplicity—capitalizing on sustained price movements—and its ability to generate profits in various market conditions. However, mastering this approach requires more than just basic knowledge; it demands a deep understanding of market psychology, risk management, and the mechanics behind price trends.

For traders eager to dive into trend following or to sharpen their techniques, a well-curated list of books can provide invaluable insights. This blog post explores some of the best books available to learn trend following trading, breaking down their core teachings and how each one can contribute to your trading success.

Understanding Trend Following Trading: A Quick Primer

Before delving into the books, a brief overview sets the stage for why trend following is important. At its core, trend following means identifying a stock, commodity, currency, or other asset that is moving persistently in one direction and riding that momentum until signs indicate the trend’s end.

Unlike day trading or short-term speculation, trend followers adopt a discipline that typically favors longer holding periods. They focus on price action rather than predicting market tops or bottoms. This principle can be applied across various asset classes and timeframes, making trend following extremely versatile.

Why Books Matter More Than Ever

In the era of TikTok and Twitter trading tips, taking the time to study comprehensive books still holds a critical advantage. Books provide detailed frameworks, historical examples, and tested methodologies. They tend to address nuance and complex concepts that bite-sized content often overlooks, such as:

  • The psychology behind sticking to a system during losses
  • The interplay between volatility and trend inception
  • Effective position sizing to control drawdowns

With that in mind, here are some of the premier books that offer a robust foundation and advanced insights into the art and science of trend following trading.

1. Trend Following: How to Make a Fortune in Bull, Bear, and Black Swan Markets by Michael W. Covel

This book is often called the bible of trend following trading. Covel captures decades of experience from legendary traders and fund managers who have employed trend systems with disciplined rigour. The book’s strength lies in its historical perspective combined with practical tactics.

Key takeaways include:

  • The psychological mindset necessary for trend following success
  • How to systematically enter and exit positions
  • Lessons from real-life traders on pitfalls and rewards

Covel also explores the impact of Black Swan events on trend followers, offering insights into how this strategy can potentially thrive when traditional investors suffer.

2. Following the Trend: Diversified Managed Futures Trading by Andreas F. Clenow

Clenow’s book builds on the practical side, using quantitative analysis and strategy design tailored for institutional and retail traders alike. It demystifies the mechanics behind managed futures—a space where trend following is a dominant strategy.

Highlights include:

  • Step-by-step modeling of trend following systems
  • Detailed explanations of using moving averages and volatility filters
  • Risk and money management principles that protect capital while maximizing growth

The clarity of explanation makes it particularly useful for traders comfortable with numbers seeking to construct or refine their own trend models.

3. The Complete TurtleTrader: How 23 Novice Investors Became Overnight Millionaires by Michael W. Covel

This one is a narrative but packed with lessons. The book chronicles the Turtle Traders experiment, where a group of individuals with little to no trading experience were taught a strict trend-following system that yielded astonishing results.

What makes it valuable:

  • Firsthand accounts of the training process
  • Illustrations of the importance of rules-based trading
  • Real-world evidence reinforcing the power of discipline and simplicity

If you enjoy stories that also teach systemization, this book offers both inspiration and practical knowledge.

4. Trade What You See: How To Capture Profits Through Advanced Pattern Recognition by Larry Pesavento and Leslie Jouflas

While trend following often relies on identifying sustained trends using indicators, this book deepens the approach by introducing pattern recognition techniques that can enhance a trend follower’s entry and exit timing.

Key features:

  • Advanced chart patterns that signal trend continuation or reversals
  • Combining price action with risk management
  • Emphasis on psychological discipline through pattern confirmation

This book is ideal for traders aiming to refine their technical toolbox while staying true to trend trading principles.

5. Unholy Grails: A New Road to Wealth by Nick Radge

Radge offers a practical and readable approach that blends trend following with risk control tailored to personal trading styles. One of the distinguishing aspects is his focus on psychological and emotional challenges that come with following trends.

Important lessons include:

  • How to develop a trading system aligned with your own risk tolerance
  • Tips for maintaining discipline during losing streaks
  • Easy-to-understand examples of trend setups

Unholy Grails encourages traders not only to build robust systems but to build mental stamina, which is critical for sticking to trend following methods long-term.

6. Quantitative Trading: How to Build Your Own Algorithmic Trading Business by Ernest P. Chan

Trend following can often be automated or enhanced through algorithmic approaches, especially when markets are heavily data-driven. Chan’s book lends itself well to anyone interested in developing trend-following algorithms or quantitative strategies.

You will gain:

  • Fundamentals of algorithmic design for trend detection
  • Insights into backtesting and optimizing strategies
  • Discussion on managing slippage and transaction costs

For traders combining tech savvy with trend following passion, this book bridges manual and automated trading worlds.

How to Choose the Right Trend Following Book for You

With so many outstanding resources, picking the right one depends on your experience level and preferences:

  • Beginners: Start with Trend Following by Covel or The Complete TurtleTrader to grasp core principles and mindset.
  • Intermediate Traders: Explore Following the Trend by Clenow or Unholy Grails by Radge to learn system design and risk nuances.
  • Advanced/Quant Traders: Quantitative Trading by Chan or Trade What You See adds sophistication for building mechanical or pattern-based strategies.

Whichever you choose, pairing book knowledge with consistent practice is crucial.

Integrating Trend Following Lessons into Your Trading Routine

Reading is an excellent start, but trading requires implementation. Here are some practical tips for integrating these lessons:

  1. Keep a Trading Journal: Document every trade—entries, exits, emotions—to identify patterns about your adherence to the system.
  2. Backtest Strategies: Use historical data to test systems learned from books before committing capital.
  3. Simulate First: Use paper trading accounts or simulation software, applying trend strategies across different market conditions.
  4. Stay Patient: Trend following thrives over time; patience is often the differentiator between profit and loss.
  5. Adapt Without Overcomplicating: Keep strategies robust but allow minor tweaks as market behaviors evolve.

Conclusion

Trend following trading is more than a technical strategy—it’s a disciplined mindset and a well-honed skillset that can open opportunities across various markets. The books highlighted here are some of the best to learn, internalize, and perfect trend following trading, each offering unique perspectives to strengthen your approach.

Whether you are just getting started or seeking to deepen your expertise, these carefully selected titles can become your compass in navigating the dynamic world of trend trading. Pair them with patience, disciplined execution, and continuous learning, and you’re setting yourself on a promising path to success.

Happy reading and successful trading!
