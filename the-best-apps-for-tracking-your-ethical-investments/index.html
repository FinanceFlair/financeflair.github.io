The Best Apps for Tracking Your Ethical Investments


In today's fast-paced and ever-changing world, investing in a socially responsible and sustainable manner is no longer just a nicety, but a necessity. As the global focus shifts towards environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations, the importance of tracking your ethical investments has never been more pressing. To help navigate this burgeoning landscape, we've curated a list of the best apps for tracking your impact-driven investments. What is Ethical Investing, Anyway? Ethical investing, also known as sustainable or socially responsible investing, is an investment approach that seeks to generate a positive impact on society, the environment, and the economy, while also providing a stable financial return. It involves investing in companies that align with your values and are committed to being a force for good. By prioritizing ESG factors alongside financial returns, you can ensure that your investments are not only profitable but also help create positive change in the world. Top Apps for Tracking Your Ethical Investments

  1. InvestMe InvestMe is a mobile app that allows you to invest in a variety of ESG-themed funds and track their performance in real-time. The app offers a user-friendly interface and provides detailed information on the investment funds you choose, including their ESG ratings and financial metrics. With InvestMe, you can easily monitor your investments, set reminders, and receive updates on market trends and performance.
  2. Robo.Wiz Robo.Wiz is a holistic investment management app that provides personalized portfolio recommendations based on your financial goals and risk tolerance. The app offers a range of ESG-screened investment options and allows you to track your investments' performance across multiple asset classes. With Robo.Wiz, you can also opt for automatic portfolio rebalancing and regular performance reviews.
  3. Honeygain Honeygain is a cloud-based platform that allows you to invest in ESG-themed indices and funds, with the option to customize your portfolio according to your values. The app offers real-time tracking of your investments, detailed ESG data, and regular market analysis. With Honeygain, you can explore global investment opportunities and stay ahead of market trends.
  4. Sympla Sympla is a user-friendly app that connects you with a global network of impact investing platforms, offering a curated selection of ESG-themed funds and investments. The app provides real-time tracking and market updates, allowing you to monitor your investments and make informed decisions about your portfolio. With Sympla, you can tap into a broader investment ecosystem and diversify your portfolio.
  5. GreenMoney GreenMoney is a comprehensive investment research and analysis tool that helps you make informed decisions about your ESG investments. The app provides in-depth reports on the ESG performance of leading global companies, offering actionable insights to help you make a positive impact. With GreenMoney, you can explore investment opportunities across various sectors and asset classes. The Case for ESG Investing While the benefits of ESG investing are clear, some argue that it may come at a higher cost, in terms of financial returns. However, research has shown that investments in companies with strong ESG credentials often outperform their counterparts in the long run. Moreover, the demand for ESG products is no longer limited to fines and regulatory pressure; forward-thinking investors recognize the potential for significant returns. Conclusion In conclusion, investing in a socially responsible and sustainable manner requires a multifaceted approach. By utilizing these top apps for tracking your ethical investments, you can stay informed, monitor your investments, and make a real difference in the world. Weaving ESG considerations into your investment strategy can not only create lasting value but also foster a more equitable, sustainable, and responsible economic landscape for everyone.
