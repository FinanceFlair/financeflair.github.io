It indicates persisting market patterns and patterns of cyclical fluctuations. This irreplaceable insight assists investors in negotiating environmental considerations affecting potential future value-generating contracts of their investment security or asset. Error Correction Apprehending significant assumptions is the second reason to repeatedly analyze historical data trends.
