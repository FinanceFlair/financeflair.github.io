Top 5 DIY Home Improvement Projects Under $100


Are you looking to spruce up your living space without breaking the bank? DIY home improvement projects can be a fun and cost-effective way to enhance the look and feel of your home. In this blog post, we will explore five creative DIY projects that you can easily complete for under $100. From adding a touch of personality to your decor to improving functionality, these projects are sure to inspire your inner DIY enthusiast.

1. Accent Wall with Paint

Creating an accent wall is a simple yet impactful way to add visual interest to any room. Choose a bold color that complements your existing decor and paint one wall to serve as a focal point. This project not only adds personality to your space but also creates a sense of depth and dimension.

To keep costs low, consider using leftover paint from previous projects or opt for budget-friendly paint options. With a little creativity and some painter’s tape, you can transform a plain wall into a stunning feature that reflects your style.

2. Upcycled Furniture

Give old furniture a new lease on life by upcycling it with a fresh coat of paint or new hardware. Visit thrift stores, garage sales, or online marketplaces to find affordable pieces that you can revamp to suit your taste. Whether it’s a worn-out dresser, a dated side table, or a mismatched chair, upcycling furniture allows you to add a unique touch to your home without spending a fortune.

Get creative with stencils, decoupage, or different paint techniques to personalize your furniture and make a statement in any room. Not only is upcycling environmentally friendly, but it also gives you the opportunity to showcase your creativity.

3. DIY Shelving

Maximize storage and display space in your home by installing DIY shelves. Whether you need extra storage in the kitchen, bedroom, or living room, custom shelves can be both functional and decorative. Consider using reclaimed wood, crates, or floating shelf brackets to create customized shelving that fits your space perfectly.

By building your own shelves, you can customize the size, shape, and finish to match your decor style. Display plants, books, or decorative items to add personality to your walls and showcase your favorite belongings.

4. Gallery Wall

Curate a gallery wall to showcase your favorite photos, artwork, and prints. Gallery walls are a versatile way to add visual interest and create a personalized display in any room. Mix and match frames in different sizes and finishes to create a cohesive yet eclectic look.

To keep costs down, consider printing photos at home or using affordable online printing services. Arrange the frames on the floor first to experiment with different layouts before hanging them on the wall. A gallery wall not only adds a decorative element but also tells a story and reflects your unique style.

5. Lighting Makeover

Upgrade your lighting fixtures to transform the ambiance of your home. Replace outdated light fixtures with modern alternatives or add new lighting elements to brighten up dim areas. Consider installing LED strip lights, pendant lights, or decorative sconces to enhance the mood and functionality of your space.

Look for budget-friendly lighting options at home improvement stores or online retailers. With a few simple tools and some basic electrical knowledge, you can easily update your lighting fixtures and create a warm and inviting atmosphere in your home.

Whether you’re a seasoned DIY enthusiast or just starting out, these five budget-friendly home improvement projects are perfect for adding style and character to your living space. Get creative, have fun, and enjoy the process of transforming your home without breaking the bank. Remember, with a little imagination and resourcefulness, you can achieve a beautiful and personalized home that truly reflects your personality.

Happy DIYing!
