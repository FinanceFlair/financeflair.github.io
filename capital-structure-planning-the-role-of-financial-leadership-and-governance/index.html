Capital Structure Planning: The Role of Financial Leadership and Governance

Strategic governance assumes paramount significance in the realm of capital structure planning, encompassing the imperatives of safeguarding ethical integrity, upholding regulatory compliance, and fostering a culture of transparency and accountability.
