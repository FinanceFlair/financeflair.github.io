Top Trend Following Blogs and Resources You Should Follow


Are you tired of following the herd and struggling to make consistent profits in the markets? Trend following can be a game-changer, but only if you're equipped with the right knowledge and tools. As a trader or investor, you need access to top-notch information and resources to help you navigate the complex world of trends and market analysis. In this post, we'll take you through the crème de la crème of trend following blogs and resources, where you'll find expert insights, actionable advice, and valuable knowledge to enhance your trading and investing skills. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, these top blogs and resources will help you stay ahead of the curve and achieve your financial goals.

  1. The Traders Fly This blog, run by a seasoned trader with over 20 years of experience, offers a wealth of information on trend following, trading strategies, and market analysis. The author's expertise spans multiple markets, including FX, futures, and equities, making it an excellent resource for traders with diverse interests. What sets The Traders Fly apart is its unique approach to trend following, focusing on the visual and quantitative aspects of market analysis. The author shares his insights on chart patterns, trend lines, and statistical models, providing readers with a comprehensive understanding of market behavior.
  2. Trend Following with Brian Harden Brian Harden is a well-known figure in the trend following community, and his blog is a treasure trove of valuable information on the subject. With a focus on trading and investing, Brian shares his insights on market trends, sentiment analysis, and risk management strategies. One of the standout features of Brian's blog is its emphasis on practical, real-world examples. He shares case studies and examples of his own trading experiences, highlighting the importance of discipline and adaptability in trend following. 3.-systemosa This blog, maintained by a professional trader with a background in computer science, takes a data-driven approach to trend following. With a focus on machine learning and statistical modeling, the author shares insights on how to use data analysis to identify and profit from market trends. What makes systemosa stand out is its deep dive into the technical aspects of trend following. The author shares code snippets and data visualizations, providing readers with a unique understanding of the mathematical and computational underpinnings of market analysis.
  3. TradingView As a community-driven platform, TradingView offers a wealth of information on trend following through its extensive library of blog posts, webinars, and videos. The platform has attracted a large following of traders, investors, and professionals, all sharing their insights and expertise on various aspects of financial markets. What sets TradingView apart is its open-source approach to trend following. The platform provides users with access to its proprietary algorithms and trading strategies, making it an ideal resource for traders looking to learn from the best in the business.
  4. For Traders By Traders As its name suggests, this blog is written by traders for traders. The site offers a wealth of information on trend following, including insights on trading strategies, market analysis, and risk management. The authors share their real-world experiences, highlighting the importance of hard work, discipline, and continuous learning in achieving success in the markets. What sets For Traders By Traders apart is its emphasis on the human side of trading. The authors share stories of their successes and failures, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the psychological and emotional aspects of market trading. Conclusion Trend following can be a powerful tool for traders and investors, but only if you have access to the right information and resources. The blogs and resources mentioned above are the cream of the crop, offering expert insights, actionable advice, and valuable knowledge to enhance your trading and investing skills. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, these top blogs and resources will help you stay ahead of the curve and achieve your financial goals.
