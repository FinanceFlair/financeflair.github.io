Top 10 Robo Advisors of 2021: Reviews and Rankings


In the fast-paced world of finance, technology has revolutionized the way we manage our investments. Robo advisors, automated platforms that provide algorithm-based financial planning services, have gained significant popularity over the years. These digital investment tools offer convenience, low fees, and personalized portfolio management, making them a go-to choice for many investors. If you are looking to navigate the world of robo advisors and find the best option for your financial goals, look no further. Here are the top 10 robo advisors of 2021, complete with reviews and rankings to help you make an informed decision.

1. Wealthfront

Wealthfront is a frontrunner in the robo advisory space, known for its diversified investment portfolios and tax-efficient strategies. With a user-friendly interface and low management fees, Wealthfront appeals to both new and experienced investors.

2. Betterment

Betterment is a highly regarded robo advisor that offers a range of investment options, including retirement accounts and socially responsible portfolios. Their goal-based tools and automatic rebalancing make it easy for investors to stay on track with their financial objectives.

3. Vanguard Personal Advisor Services

Vanguard is a trusted name in the investment world, and their Personal Advisor Services combine the expertise of financial advisors with the convenience of robo technology. With personalized advice and access to Vanguard’s renowned funds, this robo advisor is a top choice for many investors.

4. SoFi Automated Investing

SoFi’s robo advisor stands out for its competitive fees and additional perks, such as career coaching and financial planning services. For users looking for a holistic approach to financial management, SoFi’s Automated Investing platform is worth considering.

5. Charles Schwab Intelligent Portfolios

Charles Schwab’s robo advisor offers a wide range of investment options and personalized advice from certified financial planners. With no advisory fees and automatic rebalancing, this platform caters to investors of all levels.

6. Ellevest

Ellevest is a robo advisor designed specifically for women, focusing on closing the gender investment gap. Their goal-based approach and emphasis on factors like gender pay gap make Ellevest a unique and empowering choice for female investors.

7. Acorns

Acorns is a robo advisor that simplifies investing by rounding up your everyday purchases and investing the spare change. This innovative approach to saving and investing makes Acorns an attractive option for beginners and those looking to start small.

8. M1 Finance

M1 Finance combines the flexibility of a brokerage account with the automation of a robo advisor. Investors can create custom portfolios or choose from expert pies, making it a versatile option for those who want more control over their investments.

9. Blooom

Blooom focuses on retirement planning, specifically 401(k) management. With tools to optimize your employer-sponsored retirement account and personalized advice from financial experts, Blooom is ideal for individuals looking to maximize their retirement savings.

10. Wealthsimple

Wealthsimple is a robo advisor known for its socially responsible investing options and user-friendly interface. With features like Halal investing and automatic rebalancing, Wealthsimple caters to a diverse range of investors looking to align their values with their investment choices.

In conclusion, the world of robo advisors offers a plethora of choices tailored to different investor preferences and financial goals. Whether you are a seasoned investor or just starting, there is a robo advisor out there to meet your needs. By considering factors like fees, investment options, and personalized advice, you can select the best robo advisor to help you achieve your financial objectives. Explore these top 10 robo advisors of 2021 and take the first step towards a smarter and more efficient investment journey. Happy investing!
