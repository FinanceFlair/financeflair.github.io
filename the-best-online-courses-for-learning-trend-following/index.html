The Best Online Courses for Learning Trend Following


Trend following is a time-tested trading strategy embraced by individual traders and institutional investors alike. It revolves around the concept of identifying and riding market momentum for potentially consistent profits. Whether you’re a complete beginner or an experienced trader aiming to refine your skills, investing in a solid online course focused on trend following can be a game-changer. This comprehensive guide uncovers some of the best online courses currently available, helping you navigate the path to mastering trend following.

Understanding Trend Following: A Quick Overview

Before diving into course recommendations, it’s worth revisiting what makes trend following unique. Unlike market timing or fundamental analysis-heavy approaches, trend following relies heavily on market price action and behavioral patterns. Traders look for upward or downward momentum and position themselves accordingly. It involves patience, discipline, and a clear understanding of risk management.

The key benefits of learning trend following include:

  • Simplicity and clarity: The strategy relies on clear-cut rules based on price movements and technical indicators.
  • Adaptability: Effective across various markets, including stocks, futures, forex, and cryptocurrencies.
  • Risk control: Emphasizes cut-losses quickly and lets profits run, which can reduce drawdowns.

With these advantages, trend following remains a top strategy, making the right education crucial to harnessing its potential.

Criteria for Choosing the Best Online Trend Following Courses

To evaluate and select the best trend following courses, several factors were considered:

  • Instructor expertise: The teacher’s background, success, and experience with trend following.
  • Comprehensive curriculum: Depth and breadth of lessons covering entry, exit, risk management, and psychology.
  • Practical examples and exercises: Opportunities to apply concepts through real-world case studies or simulations.
  • Community and support: Access to forums, mentorship, or Q&A sessions.
  • Student feedback: Reviews from past students highlighting successes and course credibility.
  • Up-to-date content: Material reflecting the current market dynamics, including algorithmic advancements.
  • Accessibility: Flexible learning formats that accommodate different schedules.

With these in mind, here’s a rundown of top courses that stand out.

1. Trend Following Academy by Michael Covel

Michael Covel is one of the most influential figures in popularizing trend following, authoring several books and hosting a podcast. His Trend Following Academy offers a rich program that blends theory with practical application.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Foundations of trend following philosophy and mindset.
  • Detailed breakdown of trend identification techniques.
  • Technical tools like moving averages, breakout patterns, and ATR for position sizing.
  • Risk management tactics tailored to diverse markets.
  • How to develop and backtest your own trend following strategies.

Why It’s Outstanding:

This academy uniquely combines Michael Covel’s decades of experience with actionable insights. The curriculum covers everything required to build a well-rounded understanding and provides a community where students can interact and share ideas.

2. “Trend Following Fundamentals” on Udemy by Alan Hull

Udemy hosts numerous trading courses, but Alan Hull’s “Trend Following Fundamentals” rises above the noise by offering clarity and depth at an affordable price.

Course Highlights:

  • Clear explanation of trend following concepts aimed at beginners.
  • Introduction to technical indicators supporting trend trading.
  • Step-by-step methodologies for spotting trades based on price action.
  • Understanding market cycles from a trend perspective.
  • Incorporation of psychological elements influencing decisions.

Ideal For:

Traders who prefer concise, focused content delivered in an easy-to-digest format. The course also emphasizes developing discipline and patience, which are crucial for trend followers.

3. Investopedia Academy: Technical Analysis Course

While not exclusively about trend following, Investopedia Academy’s widely respected technical analysis course covers an essential component of trend following — understanding price trends and indicators.

Key Takeaways:

  • In-depth explanation of trend lines, support and resistance.
  • Use of moving averages, Bollinger Bands, and momentum indicators.
  • Combining multiple tools to confirm trends.
  • Risk management principles tailored for trend-based strategies.
  • Real examples illustrating breakout and breakdown patterns.

Why Include It:

It’s a solid foundation course that complements specialized trend following training, especially valuable for those transitioning from fundamental analysis or complete beginners in technical trading.

4. NinjaTrader Trend Following Masterclass

NinjaTrader is both a popular trading platform and education provider. Their trend following masterclass emphasizes hands-on learning with their state-of-the-art platform, suitable for those keen to automate or test strategies.

What You’ll Get:

  • Training on setting up and interpreting trend indicators within the NinjaTrader ecosystem.
  • Step-by-step tutorials on coding and backtesting automated trend following systems.
  • A balance of manual trading and algorithmic insights.
  • Focus on risk parameters such as stop losses and trailing stops.
  • Access to live trading webinars to witness trend following in action.

Best Suited For:

Active traders who are platform-focused and want an immersive experience including practical and automated trading implementations.

5. The Traders Podcast: Trend Following Series by Rob Booker

For learners who prefer an auditory and conversational style, the Traders Podcast offers an extensive series dedicated to trend following hosted by trader Rob Booker.

Benefits:

  • Interviews and discussions with veteran trend followers and quantitative traders.
  • Covers psychological, strategic, and technical aspects without heavy jargon.
  • Real trader stories and lessons learned from market experiences.
  • Easily consumable episodes ideal for on-the-go learning.

Bonus:

Free access with additional paid resources for deeper dives into specific topics.

6. SMB Capital: Advanced Trend Following Workshop

SMB Capital is a well-regarded prop trading firm known for its practical trader training. They offer an advanced workshop specifically focused on trend following techniques for active traders.

Workshop Features:

  • High-level trading strategies including multi-timeframe analysis.
  • Trading plan development tailored to individual risk appetites.
  • Real-time trade review sessions and mentorship.
  • Exploration of momentum indicators and divergence signals.
  • Advanced money management and scaling techniques.

Recommended For:

Experienced traders wanting to elevate their skillset and integrate trend following insights into their broader trading approach.

Maximizing Learning Effectiveness: Tips to Get the Most Out of These Courses

Acquiring knowledge is just the first step. To truly benefit from any trend following course, consider these strategies:

  1. Practice consistently: Apply lessons through demo accounts or backtesting software to build confidence without risking capital.
  2. Record your trades: Maintain a trading journal to track what works, mistakes, and emotional patterns.
  3. Engage with communities: Join forums, course groups, or social media communities to share insights and gain support.
  4. Stay patient: Trend following requires discipline to allow setups to mature without premature exits.
  5. Adapt and iterate: Markets evolve; continuously refine strategies based on new data and experience.

Wrapping Up: Elevate Your Trading with Trend Following Education

Trend following remains one of the most robust trading methodologies, but success depends on learning the nuances from credible sources. The courses listed offer a variety of learning styles, depths, and price points, catering to a broad spectrum of traders. Whether you’re intrigued by Michael Covel’s deep-dive academy, prefer hands-on platform training with NinjaTrader, or enjoy auditory learning via podcasts, there’s a course to match your preferences.

Investing time and effort in a well-structured course will arm you with the right tools to decipher market trends confidently and potentially unlock consistent rewards. Remember, trading is a journey, and mastering trend following is a profound step forward.

Additional Resources

If you’re interested in expanding your knowledge beyond courses, consider these books and resources often recommended alongside the courses above:

  • Trend Following by Michael W. Covel
  • Following the Trend by Andreas Clenow
  • Technical Analysis of the Financial Markets by John Murphy
  • The Complete TurtleTrader by Michael W. Covel

Pairing course learning with well-regarded literature and practice will round out a strong educational foundation.

Embrace the art and science of trend following, and happy trading!
