Top Personal Finance Podcasts to Boost Your Knowledge


Personal finance is an essential aspect of our lives, and having a solid understanding of it can significantly impact our financial well-being. With the abundance of information available online, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. This is where personal finance podcasts come to the rescue. These engaging and informative shows offer expert advice, practical tips, and inspiring stories to help you manage your finances effectively. In this post, we'll be exploring the top personal finance podcasts to boost your knowledge and take control of your financial future.

  1. The Dave Ramsey Show Started by none other than personal finance guru Dave Ramsey, this podcast is a must-listen for anyone looking to get out of debt and build wealth. With over 23 years of experience, Dave Ramsey shares practical wisdom and straightforward advice on managing your finances, paying off debt, and investing in your future. Whether you're drowning in credit card debt or trying to save for retirement, Dave Ramsey's show is an excellent resource to get you back on track. Episode Highlights:

Debt Snowball vs. Debt Avalanche: Which Method Works Best? How to Create a Budget that Actually Works for You The Benefits of Emergency Funds and How to Build One

  1. The Rad Retirement Show Hosted by acclaimed financial planner Steve Vernon, this podcast focuses on providing actionable advice on retirement planning and wealth management. Steve Vernon, a former actuary at J.P. Morgan Securities, shares his expertise on topics such as saving for retirement, investing in real estate, and navigating the world of tax-advantaged accounts. Episode Highlights:

Simple Rules for Sustainable Investing Creative Strategies for Increasing Retirement Income How to Protect Your Assets from Inflation and Taxes

  1. Planet Money From the world-renowned National Public Radio (NPR), Planet Money offers an engaging and accessible approach to personal finance. This podcast explores a wide range of topics, from basic money concepts to complex economic policies. Hosted by a diverse team of experts, Planet Money makes learning about finance fun and easy. Episode Highlights:

The Science of Money Hacks: What Works and What Doesn't? Inside the World of Cryptocurrency: Understanding Bitcoin Why Humans Make Mistakes with Money (and How to Avoid Them)

  1. Your Money or Your Life Based on the bestselling book of the same name, Your Money or Your Life podcast is a great resource for those seeking to break free from the debt trap and live a fulfilling life. Hosted by Jean Chatzky, a renowned financial journalist, and the book's authors Vicki Robin and Joe Dominguez, this show offers practical tips on saving, investing, and achieving financial independence. Episode Highlights:

How to Create a Budget that Truly Works for You Strategies for Investing in Your Future Why It's Time to Ditch the Fixed Mindset and Embrace Financial Flexibility

  1. Radical Personal Finance Gary Keller, one of the co-authors of "The Millionaire Real Estate Agent," hosts this podcast, which offers cutting-edge insights on personal finance, investing, and achieving financial freedom. Gary and his co-host, Brad, cover an array of topics, from building wealth through real estate investing to developing a money mind-set. Episode Highlights:

How to Create a Plan to Save $1 Million by Your 40s Designing Your Dream Life and How to Make it a Reality Why Money and Wealth are Not the Same (and How to Prioritize the Right One)

  1. ChooseFI For those seeking to achieve financial independence and build wealth, ChooseFI is a must-listen podcast. Founded by Jonathan Mendonsa and Brad Barrett, authors of "ChooseFI," this show offers expert insights on reducing expenses, increasing income, and investing effectively. Episode Highlights:

Strategic Debt Repayment: Which Method Works Best? Creating Multiple Revenue Streams to Build Wealth How to Choose the Right Investment Strategy for You

  1. Stacking Benjamins Each episode of this podcast offers fresh, relatable advice on personal finance, money management, and entrepreneurship. Hosted by Joe Saul-Sehy and OG, a seasoned financial expert, Stacking Benjamins helps you break free from financial chaos and live a fulfilling life. Episode Highlights:

How to Avoid Credit Card Debt: Tips, Tricks, and Games The Art of Building a Satisfying Career and Getting Paid What You're Worth Simplifying Your Spending to Maximize Your Financial Freedom

  1. The Motley Fool's Money Podcast From the well-known financial services company The Motley Fool comes this engaging and informative podcast on investing and personal finance. Hosted by a diverse team of experts, the show offers actionable advice, expert insights, and market analysis. Episode Highlights:

5 Investing Strategies to Build Your Wealth How to Invest in Your Early 20s and Set Yourself Up for Success Why Artificial Intelligence is Revolutionizing the Finance Industry

  1. The Graham Stephan Show Real estate agent and investor Graham Stephan hosts this popular podcast, which offers direct, no-nonsense advice on real estate investing, personal finance, and entrepreneurship. Graham shares his expertise on investing in rental properties, managing rental income, and creating wealth through real estate. Episode Highlights:

Investing in Rental Properties 101 How to Build a Million-Dollar Real Estate Portfolio Avoiding Common Mistakes When Investing in Real Estate

  1. Radical FIRE This podcast serves those seeking to achieve financial independence and live a life free from financial burdens. Hosted by Gary French, president of RadicalFire, the show offers expert insights on investing, retirement planning, and building wealth. Episode Highlights:

How to Live a Life of Abundance and Financial Freedom Sustainable Investing Strategies for a Prosperous Future Finding and Living Your Passion and Creativity

What to Take Away These podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge on personal finance, investing, and building wealth. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting out on your financial journey, there's something here for everyone. By incorporating these podcasts into your daily routine, you'll gain a deeper understanding of money management and develop the skills to make informed decisions about your financial future. More Personal Finance Resources To further your education and grow your financial knowledge, we recommend exploring the following resources:

Books: "The Intelligent Investor" by Benjamin Graham, "A Random Walk Down Wall Street" by Burton G. Malkiel, and "The Bogleheads' Guide to Investing" by Taylor Larimore, Mel Lindauer, and Michael LeBoeuf. Websites: The Motley Fool, Investopedia, and NerdWallet. Online Courses: Coursera's "Financial Markets" by the University of Pennsylvania, edX's "Personal Finance" by the University of Cambridge, and Udemy's "Investing like a Hedge Fund Manager."

By combining these resources with the podcasts listed above, you'll achieve a comprehensive understanding of personal finance and be well on your way to financial freedom. Happy learning and investing.
