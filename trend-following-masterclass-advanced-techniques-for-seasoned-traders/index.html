Why Trend Following?

Trend following is a popular trading strategy that focuses on identifying and capitalizing on market trends. It is based on the principle that markets tend to move in trends, and by following these trends, traders can profit from both upward and downward price movements. This strategy is favored by many seasoned traders due to its ability to generate consistent profits in various market conditions.
