How to Make $500+ Monthly with Print-on-Demand


Print-on-demand (POD) has become a popular and accessible way to generate income online. If you’re looking to make an extra $500 per month, or even more, through an online business, print-on-demand offers a low-risk and low-investment way to start. In this blog post, we will explore how to effectively make money with print-on-demand and turn it into a sustainable income stream.

What is Print-on-Demand?

Print-on-demand is a business model that allows you to design custom products that are only printed and shipped when a customer places an order. This means you don’t have to invest in inventory or deal with the complexities of fulfillment. Instead, you can create and sell products like t-shirts, mugs, posters, and more, with your unique designs printed on them.

The best part? You only pay for the product once it has been sold, which means there are no upfront costs or risks involved. This model is perfect for people looking to start a side hustle with minimal financial commitment.

How to Start a Print-on-Demand Business

Starting a print-on-demand business is easier than you might think. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started on the right foot.

Step 1: Choose Your Niche

The first step to creating a successful print-on-demand business is choosing a niche. A niche allows you to focus your marketing efforts and helps you stand out in a competitive market. It’s important to find something you’re passionate about or have knowledge of, as this will make the process more enjoyable and authentic.

Here are some popular niche ideas for print-on-demand businesses:

  • Fitness enthusiasts (e.g., workout shirts, gym accessories)
  • Pet lovers (e.g., custom pet mugs, apparel)
  • Inspirational quotes and motivational designs
  • Hobbies and activities (e.g., gardening, photography)
  • Travel and adventure
  • Custom family designs

Once you’ve chosen your niche, you can create a targeted marketing strategy that speaks directly to your audience.

Step 2: Select Your Print-on-Demand Platform

There are numerous print-on-demand platforms to choose from, each offering different features and integrations. Here are some popular platforms to consider:

  • Printful: One of the most widely used POD platforms. Printful integrates with platforms like Shopify, Etsy, and WooCommerce, making it easy to get started.
  • Printify: Offers a vast range of products and competitive pricing. Printify also integrates with major e-commerce platforms.
  • TeeSpring: Focuses primarily on apparel and is well-suited for creators looking to build a brand and sell through social media channels.
  • Redbubble: An online marketplace that allows artists to upload designs and sell products directly on their site.
  • TeePublic: Similar to Redbubble, TeePublic is an online marketplace for designers to sell their art on merchandise.

Each platform has its own strengths, so it’s essential to choose the one that aligns with your business goals. If you plan to sell directly to consumers, a platform like Shopify combined with Printful or Printify would be ideal. Alternatively, if you prefer to sell through an established marketplace, Redbubble and TeePublic can be great options.

Step 3: Design Your Products

Design is key when it comes to POD. Your designs will be the primary factor that sets you apart from other sellers. There are several ways to approach the design process:

  • Create Your Own Designs: If you have design skills, you can create your own artwork using design software like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, or Canva.
  • Hire a Designer: If you lack design skills, you can hire a freelance designer to bring your ideas to life. Platforms like Fiverr and Upwork are great places to find affordable designers.
  • Use Pre-made Templates: Some POD platforms offer pre-made design templates that you can customize to fit your brand. While this is a quicker option, it may be less unique than designing something from scratch.

Remember, the key to successful designs is to create something that resonates with your target audience. It’s also crucial to create designs that are high-quality and suitable for printing on different products.

Step 4: Set Up Your Online Store

Once you have your designs and have selected a POD platform, it’s time to set up your online store. The store is where customers will browse your products and make purchases. You can set up a store on platforms like Shopify, Etsy, or even directly through your chosen POD platform.

Here’s a quick breakdown of the steps involved in setting up your store:

  • Choose a Name: Select a name for your store that reflects your brand and niche.
  • Design Your Store: Customize your store’s appearance to match your brand identity. Many platforms provide customizable templates that make this process easy.
  • Upload Your Products: Add your designs to the store and select which products you want to sell (t-shirts, mugs, posters, etc.).
  • Set Your Pricing: Set a price for each product. Be sure to account for production costs and any platform fees when determining your pricing structure. Aim for a balance between affordability for customers and profitability for your business.
  • Write Compelling Product Descriptions: Make sure each product listing includes a detailed description that explains what the product is, the design inspiration, and why it’s special. The description should appeal to your target audience.

Step 5: Market Your Products

Marketing is essential to growing your print-on-demand business and driving sales. While it’s possible to make money through organic traffic alone, investing in some marketing strategies will help you reach a wider audience. Here are some effective marketing tactics to try:

Social Media Marketing

Social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Pinterest are powerful tools for reaching potential customers. Use these platforms to showcase your designs, share behind-the-scenes content, and interact with your followers. Building a loyal community around your brand will encourage repeat purchases and word-of-mouth promotion.

  • Instagram: Use Instagram to post images of your products, as well as lifestyle photos that show how your products can be used. Use relevant hashtags to help your posts get discovered.
  • Facebook: Facebook Groups can be a great way to connect with like-minded individuals in your niche and promote your products. You can also run targeted Facebook ads to drive traffic to your store.
  • TikTok: TikTok is a platform where viral content thrives. Try creating fun, engaging videos that show off your products or the design process.
  • Pinterest: Pinterest is a search engine that is great for driving traffic. Create visually appealing pins that link directly to your store and use keyword-rich descriptions.

Paid Advertising

If you have a marketing budget, running paid ads can help you scale your business quickly. Facebook and Instagram ads are two popular options that allow you to target specific demographics based on interests, age, and location.

Google Ads can also be effective for driving traffic to your online store by targeting specific keywords related to your products.

Influencer Marketing

Partnering with influencers in your niche can help you gain exposure and credibility. Look for influencers who have a strong following and engage with their audience authentically. You can offer them free products or a commission for every sale they generate.

Email Marketing

Building an email list is one of the most effective ways to keep your customers engaged and encourage repeat purchases. Offer a discount or a freebie in exchange for signing up to your newsletter. Send out regular updates about new products, promotions, or exclusive offers to keep your subscribers interested.

Step 6: Analyze and Optimize

Once your store is up and running, it’s important to track your sales and performance to see what’s working and where improvements can be made. Use analytics tools provided by your POD platform or third-party tools like Google Analytics to monitor your traffic, conversion rates, and sales.

  • Customer Feedback: Listen to your customers’ feedback and adjust your designs or product offerings based on what they like and don’t like.
  • Test Different Designs: Experiment with different design styles, colors, and product types to see what resonates best with your audience.
  • Optimize for SEO: Ensure that your product descriptions, titles, and tags are optimized for search engines to increase your chances of being found organically.

How Much Can You Make with Print-on-Demand?

It’s possible to make over $500 per month with a print-on-demand business, but success depends on several factors, including the quality of your designs, how effectively you market your products, and the niche you choose. If you’re dedicated and consistent, there’s potential to scale your business and generate even higher profits.

With the right strategy, it’s entirely feasible to earn $500+ per month by selling just a few products each day. For example, if your average profit per item is $10, you would need to sell 50 products a month to reach $500 in profit. However, as you expand your product range, marketing efforts, and customer base, the potential to make more will grow.

Final Thoughts

Print-on-demand offers an exciting opportunity for entrepreneurs to make money online with minimal risk and investment. By focusing on a specific niche, creating high-quality designs, selecting the right platform, and effectively marketing your products, you can build a successful business that generates a steady income.

With patience, persistence, and creativity, you can easily make $500 or more each month through print-on-demand. Start today, and with the right effort, you’ll be on your way to turning your passion into a profitable business.
