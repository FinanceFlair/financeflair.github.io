Best Robo Advisors for Retirees in 2025


As retirement approaches, ensuring your financial future remains secure becomes one of the most critical tasks. Traditional financial advisors have long been the go-to for retirement planning, but with technological advancements, robo-advisors have emerged as a powerful alternative. In 2025, robo-advisors are more sophisticated than ever, making them a viable solution for retirees who want to maximize their investment returns with minimal effort.

Robo-advisors offer personalized, automated investment strategies that can align with your retirement goals. These platforms combine technology and financial expertise, offering low fees, diversified portfolios, and easy-to-use interfaces. In this guide, we’ll explore the best robo-advisors for retirees in 2025, focusing on factors like retirement-specific features, customer service, fees, and investment strategies.

What Are Robo-Advisors?

Robo-advisors are digital platforms that provide automated financial advice and portfolio management, often using algorithms and artificial intelligence. Unlike traditional advisors, robo-advisors operate primarily online and rely on data-driven approaches to create and manage your investment portfolio. They typically ask you a series of questions to assess your financial goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon, and then use this information to construct a customized investment plan.

For retirees, robo-advisors can be an excellent choice for a more hands-off approach to managing investments. The best robo-advisors for retirees in 2025 offer automated rebalancing, tax-efficient strategies, and options for sustainable income during retirement.

Key Features Retirees Should Look for in a Robo-Advisor

Before diving into the best options for retirees, it’s essential to know what to look for in a robo-advisor. The ideal platform for retirees should have several features that cater specifically to their needs:

  1. Retirement Planning Tools: A robo-advisor should offer tools that allow you to set retirement goals and track progress toward achieving them. These tools should take into account your expected retirement date, desired lifestyle, and current savings rate.

  2. Low Fees: One of the primary benefits of using a robo-advisor is the reduced cost compared to traditional financial planners. Look for platforms with low management fees to keep more of your retirement funds working for you.

  3. Tax-Efficient Strategies: Tax efficiency becomes even more crucial in retirement as you begin to withdraw funds. Robo-advisors that optimize your tax situation—by using strategies like tax-loss harvesting—can help you keep more of your returns.

  4. Sustainable Income Options: For retirees, generating consistent income from investments is key. Robo-advisors that offer income-focused portfolios, such as those that include dividend-paying stocks or bonds, will be important for your financial stability.

  5. Socially Responsible Investing: Many retirees today are looking for investment options that align with their values. Robo-advisors that provide sustainable or socially responsible investing (SRI) options allow you to support causes that matter to you while still growing your wealth.

  6. User-Friendly Interface: As a retiree, you may not be as familiar with complex financial platforms. A robo-advisor with an easy-to-navigate interface will help you feel comfortable as you manage your investment strategy.

  7. Customer Support: Even though robo-advisors are automated, having access to customer support can be helpful for retirees who have specific questions about their portfolios or need assistance with complex scenarios.

Best Robo-Advisors for Retirees in 2025

1. Betterment

Betterment continues to be a top contender for retirees due to its advanced features and strong reputation in the robo-advisor market. The platform offers personalized financial advice, automated portfolio management, and tax-efficient strategies that are crucial for retirees.

Retirement Features:

  • Retirement Planning Tools: Betterment’s retirement planning tool helps you estimate how much you need to save for retirement and suggests adjustments if you’re falling short of your goals.
  • Tax-Loss Harvesting: Betterment’s automated tax-loss harvesting minimizes your tax bill by selling investments at a loss to offset gains.
  • Income Generation: Betterment’s portfolios include income-focused options like bonds and dividend-paying stocks to help generate consistent cash flow in retirement.

Betterment also offers socially responsible investing options for retirees who want their money to align with their values.

2. Wealthfront

Wealthfront is another great robo-advisor for retirees, known for its intelligent algorithms that create diversified, low-cost portfolios. Wealthfront is particularly beneficial for retirees who want a more hands-off approach to managing their money.

Retirement Features:

  • Retirement Planning: Wealthfront’s retirement planning tools are comprehensive, offering detailed forecasts of your retirement income and suggesting changes if necessary.
  • Tax-Efficient Strategies: Wealthfront excels in tax efficiency, offering tax-loss harvesting, retirement tax planning, and automatic rebalancing of your portfolio.
  • Fee Structure: With a fee structure of just 0.25% annually, Wealthfront is an affordable option for retirees looking to minimize investment costs.

Wealthfront also offers high-yield savings accounts for retirees who want a safer, more stable option for cash reserves in retirement.

3. Fidelity Go

Fidelity Go stands out for retirees looking for simplicity and reliability. It’s a great option for those who prefer a hands-off approach to managing their retirement portfolio while still benefiting from Fidelity’s vast financial expertise.

Retirement Features:

  • Low-Cost Management: Fidelity Go offers fee-free management for accounts under $10,000, making it one of the most affordable options for small portfolios.
  • Socially Responsible Investing: Fidelity offers a range of socially responsible investment options, making it easy for retirees to align their portfolio with their personal values.
  • Tax Optimization: While Fidelity Go doesn’t offer tax-loss harvesting like Betterment or Wealthfront, it does automatically rebalance your portfolio to keep it aligned with your retirement goals.

Fidelity’s strong reputation and customer service make it an appealing choice for retirees who prioritize security and support.

4. Personal Capital

Personal Capital is a robo-advisor that blends automated portfolio management with a more personalized approach, offering retirees access to financial advisors for one-on-one guidance.

Retirement Features:

  • Comprehensive Financial Planning: Personal Capital’s Retirement Planner tool lets you simulate different retirement scenarios and adjust your strategy as needed.
  • Fee Structure: Personal Capital charges a management fee starting at 0.89%, which is higher than many other robo-advisors. However, retirees who want access to professional financial advice may find this worthwhile.
  • Customizable Portfolios: Personal Capital offers highly customizable portfolios that allow retirees to choose from different asset classes based on their risk tolerance.

While Personal Capital is on the pricier side, it’s a great option for retirees who value personalized advice in addition to automated management.

5. Ellevest

Ellevest is designed specifically for women but is an excellent option for any retiree who values socially responsible investing and personalized financial advice. Ellevest focuses on creating portfolios that account for factors like gender pay gaps and longer life expectancies for women.

Retirement Features:

  • Goal-Oriented Planning: Ellevest’s approach to retirement planning is highly goal-oriented, allowing retirees to set specific objectives and track their progress.
  • Sustainable Investing: Ellevest offers socially responsible investing options that align with values, focusing on companies with good environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices.
  • Low Fees: Ellevest offers competitive fees starting at 0.25%, making it an affordable option for retirees looking for a balance of low cost and personalized service.

Ellevest’s retirement planning tools and focus on social responsibility make it an attractive choice for retirees who want to make a positive impact with their investments.

6. SoFi Invest

SoFi Invest provides an excellent range of retirement investment options, including automated portfolio management and a variety of income-focused strategies. SoFi stands out for retirees looking for a comprehensive platform that covers not only retirement investing but also personal loans, mortgages, and other financial services.

Retirement Features:

  • Income-Focused Portfolios: SoFi Invest offers portfolios designed to generate income through dividend-paying stocks and bonds, which is crucial for retirees looking to replace their pre-retirement income.
  • No Fees: SoFi Invest’s robo-advisor platform is free, which is a significant advantage for retirees looking to keep costs down.
  • Retirement Planning: SoFi Invest’s retirement planning tools allow you to set specific goals and track your progress toward financial independence.

SoFi Invest also offers the benefit of access to financial advisors for personalized support, making it a well-rounded option for retirees.

Conclusion

As you approach retirement in 2025, using a robo-advisor can help you secure your financial future with minimal effort and cost. Whether you’re looking for tax-efficient strategies, low fees, or income-focused investments, there’s a robo-advisor out there to suit your needs. Betterment, Wealthfront, Fidelity Go, Personal Capital, Ellevest, and SoFi Invest are among the best platforms for retirees in 2025, each offering unique features that cater to different retirement goals.

Ultimately, the best robo-advisor for you will depend on your specific financial situation, risk tolerance, and retirement objectives. By choosing a platform that aligns with your needs, you can take control of your financial future and enjoy a comfortable, secure retirement.
