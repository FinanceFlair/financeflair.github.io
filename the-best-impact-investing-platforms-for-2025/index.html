The Best Impact Investing Platforms for 2025


Impact investing is becoming an increasingly popular approach for investors who want to generate positive social and environmental outcomes while also earning financial returns. This evolving sector focuses on investments that are designed to have a measurable impact on issues such as climate change, poverty, education, and public health. As more people look to align their financial goals with their values, impact investing platforms are emerging as essential tools for making this possible.

In this article, we’ll explore the best impact investing platforms in 2025. These platforms are designed to help investors navigate the world of socially responsible investing (SRI) and environmental, social, and governance (ESG) criteria. Whether you’re an experienced investor or just beginning to explore this space, these platforms offer a range of options to match different preferences and financial goals.

1. Betterment Impact

Betterment has been a leader in the robo-advisor space for years, and their impact investing portfolio is one of the best in the industry. In 2025, Betterment Impact continues to lead the way by offering a portfolio that integrates sustainable investments aligned with your values. Their impact investing strategy includes various environmental, social, and governance (ESG) criteria, focusing on companies that are actively making a positive difference in the world.

Betterment Impact’s portfolios are customizable, meaning you can tailor your investments to match your specific interests, such as climate change, gender equality, or economic empowerment. Their transparent reporting allows you to track the impact of your investments in real-time, making it easier to see the tangible outcomes of your financial decisions.

Features:

  • Easy-to-use platform with minimal fees.
  • Customizable impact investment options.
  • Clear impact reporting to track the social and environmental outcomes of your portfolio.
  • Diverse investment choices in various sectors like clean energy, healthcare, and education.

Best for: New and seasoned investors who want a hands-off approach with tailored impact investing portfolios.

2. Moxie Future

Moxie Future is another leading impact investing platform in 2025. Focused on empowering women investors, Moxie Future offers a unique platform that integrates sustainable investing with gender lens investing. This is ideal for individuals who are particularly passionate about empowering women and supporting businesses that advance gender equality.

The platform uses gender lens criteria to evaluate investments, focusing on companies led by women or those that are dedicated to promoting gender equality in their operations. Moxie Future also offers impact-focused financial planning tools, helping you track your investments’ social and environmental outcomes. The platform is user-friendly and provides a wide range of educational resources to help you become more knowledgeable about impact investing.

Features:

  • Gender lens investing for those interested in supporting women-led businesses.
  • Transparent reporting on social and environmental impacts.
  • Educational tools to help new investors learn about impact investing.
  • Portfolio diversification with an emphasis on sustainable practices.

Best for: Investors interested in gender-focused impact investing and those seeking an educational platform.

3. Acorns Impact Portfolio

Acorns, a well-known micro-investing platform, offers an Impact Portfolio that makes it easy for users to invest small amounts of money in companies that align with their values. Whether you are new to investing or simply want to make an impact with small-scale investments, Acorns allows you to invest spare change from everyday purchases and put it into a portfolio of ESG-conscious companies.

Acorns Impact Portfolio is particularly suitable for individuals who don’t have large amounts of capital to invest but still want to make a meaningful impact. The platform automatically invests your spare change, which adds up over time. Acorns uses ETFs (exchange-traded funds) that prioritize companies with strong sustainability and corporate responsibility ratings.

Features:

  • Simple, automated impact investing for users with limited capital.
  • Round-ups and small contributions that accumulate over time.
  • Diversified ESG portfolios to choose from.
  • Low fees for investors just starting out.

Best for: Beginners or individuals who want to invest passively while making a positive impact without large upfront investments.

4. Swell Investing

Swell Investing, a subsidiary of PacWest Bancorp, is a digital investment platform focused on social impact investing. The platform allows you to invest in companies that focus on solving pressing global challenges, such as clean water, renewable energy, and social justice. In 2025, Swell continues to expand its offerings with new investment categories like ocean conservation and sustainable agriculture.

Swell’s platform provides easy access to investment opportunities in thematic portfolios designed to target social, environmental, and economic change. They also offer a robust educational library that helps investors understand how their money is making a difference in the world.

Features:

  • Focus on social and environmental issues like clean water, renewable energy, and social justice.
  • Customizable thematic portfolios based on your values.
  • Simple-to-navigate platform with clear social impact goals.
  • Robust educational resources for impact investors.

Best for: Investors looking for thematic impact investing with a focus on solving global challenges.

5. Wealthsimple Impact

Wealthsimple, a popular robo-advisor platform, offers an Impact account that allows users to invest in a diversified portfolio that aligns with sustainable and ethical values. The platform carefully selects companies with strong ESG practices, prioritizing investments in sectors such as clean energy, sustainable agriculture, and responsible technology.

Wealthsimple Impact’s portfolios are managed by professional portfolio managers, making it an ideal choice for investors who want a hands-off approach while still ensuring their investments are aligned with their values. The platform also offers low fees and transparent reporting, so you can track the performance of your investments both financially and socially.

Features:

  • Professionally managed portfolios focused on sustainability and ethical practices.
  • Transparent reporting on the social and environmental impact of investments.
  • Low-fee structure suitable for long-term investors.
  • Integration of ESG criteria in portfolio construction.

Best for: Investors who want a low-maintenance platform with professional management that emphasizes ESG values.

6. ImpactAssets

For high-net-worth individuals or accredited investors, ImpactAssets offers a comprehensive range of impact investing solutions. The platform allows users to invest in funds that target global challenges such as climate change, economic development, and healthcare. It offers a diverse range of investment options, including private equity, fixed income, and direct investments in social enterprises.

ImpactAssets is particularly appealing to experienced investors looking for a more hands-on approach to impact investing. The platform provides in-depth resources and analytics to help users evaluate the social and environmental impact of their investments. ImpactAssets also allows investors to tailor their portfolios to match their specific impact goals.

Features:

  • A broad selection of investment options, including private equity and direct investments.
  • In-depth analytics and resources for evaluating impact.
  • Personalized portfolio construction based on your impact goals.
  • Access to impact-focused financial advisors.

Best for: High-net-worth individuals and accredited investors who want to take a more active role in their impact investments.

7. Ecoland

Ecoland is an impact investing platform designed to target environmental sustainability. The platform allows users to invest in green projects and companies that focus on renewable energy, energy efficiency, and climate change mitigation. Ecoland offers both individual and community-level investment opportunities, allowing users to participate in large-scale renewable energy projects or smaller, local sustainability initiatives.

Ecoland’s approach to impact investing is grounded in the belief that environmental sustainability is not just about reducing carbon emissions, but also creating sustainable communities that thrive in harmony with the planet. The platform provides investors with detailed reports on how their funds are being used, ensuring full transparency.

Features:

  • Focus on renewable energy, energy efficiency, and other environmentally sustainable projects.
  • Investment options at both local and global levels.
  • Detailed impact reports with transparency on the use of funds.
  • Community-based investment initiatives.

Best for: Environmental enthusiasts and investors who are passionate about contributing to global sustainability efforts.

Conclusion

The world of impact investing is expanding rapidly, and the platforms mentioned here are at the forefront of this growth in 2025. From robo-advisors like Betterment Impact and Wealthsimple Impact to specialized platforms like Moxie Future and Ecoland, these platforms make it easier than ever to invest in a way that aligns with your values. Whether you’re interested in social justice, environmental sustainability, or empowering women in business, there is a platform that can help you achieve your financial and impact goals.

As impact investing continues to gain momentum, more platforms will emerge, offering even more options for investors who want to make a positive difference while achieving financial returns. By choosing the right platform, you can ensure that your investments are making the kind of impact that matters to you and the world.
