What is Envelope Budgeting?

Envelope budgeting, also known as the cash envelope system, is a time-tested method of budgeting that involves allocating cash into different envelopes designated for specific spending categories. This system helps individuals visualize and control their spending by setting aside a predetermined amount of cash for each expense category, such as groceries, entertainment, and gas. Once the money in an envelope is spent, that’s it – no more spending in that category until the next budgeting period.
