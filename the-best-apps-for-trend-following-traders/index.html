The Best Apps for Trend Following Traders


Trend following is a popular trading strategy that involves identifying the direction of an asset’s price and profiting from the trend. In today’s fast-paced financial markets, staying on top of trends and executing timely trades can be challenging. Fortunately, there are several apps available that cater specifically to trend following traders, providing them with the tools and resources needed to make informed decisions and optimize their trading performance. Here are some of the best apps for trend following traders:

1. TradingView

TradingView is a versatile app that offers a wide range of features for trend following traders. Its intuitive interface allows users to analyze charts, identify trends, and execute trades across various financial markets. The app provides real-time data and customizable technical analysis tools, enabling traders to spot trends and potential entry/exit points with ease. Additionally, TradingView’s social network aspect allows traders to share ideas and strategies, fostering a collaborative trading community.

2. TrendSpider

TrendSpider is a powerful technical analysis platform designed to assist traders in identifying and capitalizing on market trends. The app utilizes advanced algorithms to automatically analyze charts and identify trendlines, support/resistance levels, and other key technical indicators. With its automated trendline analysis and alert system, TrendSpider empowers trend following traders to stay ahead of the curve and capitalize on emerging trends efficiently.

3. StockCharts

StockCharts is a comprehensive app that provides traders with access to a wide range of technical analysis tools and charting capabilities. The app offers customizable charts with various technical indicators, allowing trend following traders to conduct in-depth analysis and identify potential trend reversals or continuations. StockCharts also provides scanning and alert features, enabling traders to monitor multiple assets simultaneously and act on developing trends promptly.

4. Thinkorswim

Thinkorswim, offered by TD Ameritrade, is a feature-rich trading app that caters to the needs of trend following traders. The platform offers advanced charting capabilities, customizable technical studies, and a robust scanning tool that allows traders to filter for assets exhibiting specific trend characteristics. Thinkorswim also provides a paper trading feature, allowing traders to test their trend following strategies in a risk-free environment before deploying them in live markets.

5. Trade Ideas

Trade Ideas is an innovative app that leverages artificial intelligence to provide trend following traders with actionable trading opportunities. The app’s proprietary algorithms scan the market in real-time, identifying potential trends and generating trading ideas based on predefined criteria. With its customizable alert system and backtesting capabilities, Trade Ideas equips traders with the tools needed to capitalize on trends and adapt to changing market conditions effectively.

In conclusion, the best apps for trend following traders offer a combination of advanced technical analysis tools, real-time data, and customizable features that empower traders to identify and capitalize on market trends. Whether you’re an experienced trend following trader or just getting started, incorporating these apps into your trading toolkit can enhance your ability to navigate dynamic market environments and make well-informed trading decisions.
