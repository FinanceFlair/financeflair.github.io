The Best Cryptocurrency Podcasts to Follow in 2024


Cryptocurrency is constantly evolving, and staying up to date with the latest trends, news, and developments is essential for anyone interested in the space. One of the most convenient and effective ways to stay informed is through cryptocurrency podcasts. Whether you’re an experienced investor, a blockchain enthusiast, or a beginner looking to understand the fundamentals, podcasts offer valuable insights and perspectives from industry leaders and experts.

In 2024, the podcasting landscape continues to thrive, offering a diverse range of shows catering to different aspects of cryptocurrency. From in-depth interviews with thought leaders to market analysis and breaking news, there’s something for everyone in the crypto world. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best cryptocurrency podcasts to follow this year.

1. Unchained by Laura Shin

“Unchained” is one of the most popular and respected cryptocurrency podcasts, and for good reason. Hosted by veteran journalist Laura Shin, the show dives deep into the world of digital currencies, blockchain technology, and the decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem. Laura’s background in journalism allows her to ask insightful questions and engage with some of the most influential figures in the crypto space.

Listeners can expect a wide range of topics covered, including interviews with industry leaders, updates on regulatory developments, and discussions on the latest crypto trends. If you’re looking to understand the fundamental aspects of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, “Unchained” is a must-follow.

Why You Should Listen:

  • In-depth interviews with industry experts
  • Focus on both macro and micro trends in the crypto world
  • Covers regulatory and legal issues surrounding cryptocurrency

2. The Pomp Podcast

Hosted by Anthony Pompliano, “The Pomp Podcast” has gained a massive following in the crypto community. Known for his bold opinions and no-nonsense approach, Anthony covers a wide range of topics, from Bitcoin and cryptocurrency to broader economic issues. His podcast features interviews with some of the most influential people in finance, technology, and politics, offering listeners valuable insights into the intersection of cryptocurrency and the traditional financial world.

The Pomp Podcast stands out due to its focus on Bitcoin and its role in reshaping the financial system. If you’re particularly interested in Bitcoin’s potential to disrupt global finance, this podcast is for you.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Strong focus on Bitcoin and its impact on global finance
  • Interviews with a diverse range of influential figures
  • Engaging and thought-provoking discussions

3. The Bad Crypto Podcast

If you’re looking for a lighter, more entertaining approach to cryptocurrency, “The Bad Crypto Podcast” is a great choice. Hosted by Joel Comm and Travis Wright, this podcast takes a fun and casual approach to crypto topics while still providing useful information. They cover everything from the latest crypto news to discussions on various altcoins, DeFi projects, and blockchain innovations.

While the tone is more relaxed compared to other podcasts, “The Bad Crypto Podcast” still provides valuable insights and serves as an excellent introduction to the crypto world for beginners. The hosts’ humor and personalities make learning about crypto a more enjoyable experience.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Lighthearted and humorous approach to crypto topics
  • Accessible for beginners
  • Covers a broad range of topics, from news to interviews

4. The Chain Reaction Podcast

For those interested in the latest developments in blockchain technology, “The Chain Reaction Podcast” is a must-listen. Hosted by Lucas Campbell and the team at TechCrunch, this podcast focuses on the cutting-edge innovations in blockchain, Web3, and cryptocurrency. The podcast brings on experts, founders, and creators from the blockchain ecosystem to discuss new projects, innovations, and the future of decentralized technology.

Whether you’re a developer, entrepreneur, or investor, “The Chain Reaction Podcast” provides valuable insights into the ongoing evolution of blockchain and its potential applications beyond just cryptocurrency.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Focuses on the technological advancements of blockchain and Web3
  • Expert guests and deep dives into new projects
  • Great for listeners interested in the future of decentralized technology

5. Crypto Frontiers

“Crypto Frontiers” is a great podcast for anyone interested in the intersection of cryptocurrency and decentralized finance (DeFi). Hosted by blockchain professionals and enthusiasts, the podcast offers a closer look at the technical aspects of cryptocurrency, smart contracts, and DeFi platforms. If you’re keen on learning more about how the crypto world is evolving beyond Bitcoin, this podcast dives into topics like yield farming, staking, decentralized exchanges (DEXs), and more.

In addition to DeFi, “Crypto Frontiers” covers other critical topics such as security, privacy, and the scalability of blockchain networks. If you’re looking to enhance your knowledge of the technical side of crypto, this podcast is a valuable resource.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Focus on decentralized finance and smart contracts
  • In-depth technical discussions
  • Provides a comprehensive understanding of the DeFi ecosystem

6. The Coin Bureau Podcast

Coin Bureau has long been known for its high-quality, educational content on cryptocurrency. The “Coin Bureau Podcast” is an extension of this, offering listeners a more conversational take on the latest developments in the crypto space. Hosted by Guy, who is well-known for his YouTube channel, the podcast features discussions on crypto news, market trends, and deep dives into specific projects and tokens.

One of the highlights of the Coin Bureau Podcast is Guy’s ability to explain complex crypto topics in an easy-to-understand manner. If you’re looking for a podcast that covers a wide range of crypto topics with clarity and insight, the “Coin Bureau Podcast” is a great choice.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Clear and educational explanations of complex topics
  • Covers a wide variety of crypto-related subjects
  • Insightful and balanced approach to the crypto market

7. CRYPTO 101

“CRYPTO 101” is perfect for beginners and those who are just starting their journey into the world of cryptocurrency. Hosted by Matthew Aaron, this podcast aims to simplify the concepts behind blockchain, digital currencies, and the crypto market. It’s an excellent resource for those who want to understand the basics before diving deeper into the more technical aspects of crypto.

The show features interviews with industry experts, offering listeners practical advice on how to navigate the crypto space. Topics covered include cryptocurrency wallets, exchanges, security best practices, and the fundamentals of blockchain technology. For newcomers, “CRYPTO 101” serves as a solid foundation to build upon.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Great for beginners looking to learn the basics of crypto
  • Focuses on practical advice and security tips
  • Interviews with experts to help guide your journey

8. What Bitcoin Did

Hosted by Peter McCormack, “What Bitcoin Did” is dedicated entirely to Bitcoin and its potential to reshape the financial landscape. The podcast features interviews with Bitcoin advocates, developers, and investors, offering deep insights into Bitcoin’s role as a store of value, a medium of exchange, and its potential to replace traditional financial systems.

The show takes an in-depth look at Bitcoin’s history, technology, and its future. If you’re passionate about Bitcoin and want to stay up-to-date with the latest developments, “What Bitcoin Did” is a must-follow.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Entirely focused on Bitcoin and its role in the financial ecosystem
  • Expert interviews and detailed analysis
  • Offers insights into the future of Bitcoin and its impact on the world

9. The Defiant Podcast

For those who are deeply involved in decentralized finance (DeFi) and the emerging Web3 space, “The Defiant Podcast” is a go-to resource. Hosted by Camila Russo, the founder of The Defiant, this podcast offers a deep dive into the world of decentralized finance, smart contracts, NFTs, and more.

The Defiant Podcast often features interviews with prominent figures in the DeFi space, discussing everything from innovative projects to the future of financial markets. If you’re keen to learn more about the cutting-edge developments in the world of DeFi, this podcast is a valuable source of information.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Focuses on decentralized finance and Web3 technologies
  • Interviews with leaders in the DeFi space
  • Provides an inside look into the future of finance

10. The Blockchain Show

“The Blockchain Show” is a podcast that covers a broad range of topics within the blockchain and cryptocurrency sectors. Hosted by Matthew Aaron and others, the show dives into the technical side of blockchain technology while also exploring its real-world applications in various industries, from finance to supply chain management.

The podcast features interviews with developers, entrepreneurs, and innovators in the blockchain space, offering listeners valuable insights into the latest trends and advancements in the ecosystem. Whether you’re interested in the technical side of blockchain or its potential for revolutionizing various industries, “The Blockchain Show” is a great resource.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Covers a wide range of blockchain-related topics
  • Focuses on both technical aspects and real-world applications
  • Engaging interviews with industry experts

Conclusion

The cryptocurrency space continues to grow at an exponential rate, and staying informed is essential for anyone looking to succeed in this exciting and rapidly changing industry. By following these top cryptocurrency podcasts, you can stay up to date on the latest news, trends, and developments while also gaining deeper insights into the technology and the people behind the innovations.

Whether you’re a newcomer or a seasoned veteran, there’s a podcast on this list to suit your needs. From Bitcoin and DeFi to blockchain technology and NFTs, these shows cover a wide range of topics and provide a diverse range of perspectives that will help you navigate the crypto landscape in 2024. So, plug in your headphones, press play, and start learning!
