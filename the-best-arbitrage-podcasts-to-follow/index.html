The Best Arbitrage Podcasts to Follow


Arbitrage trading is a strategy that has captivated traders and investors alike for years. With its focus on exploiting price differences between markets, it’s no wonder that many professionals and enthusiasts alike are eager to learn more about how to master this method. One of the best ways to stay updated on the latest trends, strategies, and expert insights is through podcasts. Podcasts provide a convenient and engaging way to learn while on the go, whether you’re driving, exercising, or even relaxing at home.

If you’re looking for insightful content on arbitrage, you’re in luck. There are several high-quality podcasts out there that delve deep into the intricacies of arbitrage trading. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best arbitrage podcasts to follow in 2025, helping you expand your knowledge and sharpen your trading skills.

1. The Arbitrage Podcast

Aimed specifically at those who are serious about arbitrage, this podcast is an excellent resource for both beginners and experienced traders. Hosted by industry veterans, it provides in-depth discussions about various arbitrage strategies, including spatial arbitrage, statistical arbitrage, and cross-border opportunities. The hosts break down complex concepts into digestible segments, making it an ideal choice for traders looking to deepen their understanding of the subject.

The podcast also features expert guests who share their experiences and insights, offering practical tips on how to leverage market inefficiencies. Additionally, listeners can expect to learn about the latest trends in arbitrage, including opportunities that arise in rapidly evolving markets like cryptocurrencies, stocks, and commodities.

What to Expect:

  • In-depth analysis of arbitrage strategies
  • Expert guest interviews
  • Discussions on market inefficiencies
  • Focus on crypto and stock market arbitrage

Why Follow? If you’re looking for a podcast that provides valuable information on the technical and strategic aspects of arbitrage trading, this is a must-listen. It’s perfect for traders who want to stay ahead of the curve and apply new strategies to their portfolios.

2. Market Movers Podcast

The “Market Movers” podcast focuses on the broader world of trading, but it has plenty of episodes dedicated to arbitrage and its role in modern markets. Hosted by market analysts, this show covers a wide range of topics including arbitrage in different asset classes like stocks, forex, and commodities. While the focus isn’t solely on arbitrage, it does an excellent job of discussing how arbitrage trading fits into the larger trading ecosystem.

Listeners can expect to learn about the practical challenges faced by arbitrageurs and how to overcome them. The hosts also discuss trends that might affect arbitrage opportunities, such as changes in market volatility, liquidity, and technology advancements.

What to Expect:

  • Discussions on arbitrage across asset classes
  • Practical insights into market conditions
  • Analysis of macroeconomic factors affecting arbitrage opportunities
  • Thoughtful interviews with traders and analysts

Why Follow? For anyone interested in understanding how arbitrage fits into the broader financial markets, this podcast is a great choice. It’s perfect for those who want a comprehensive overview and who enjoy in-depth discussions on various trading strategies, including arbitrage.

3. The Arbitrage Trading Show

Hosted by seasoned traders who specialize in arbitrage, “The Arbitrage Trading Show” is dedicated entirely to this niche. The podcast covers everything from traditional arbitrage strategies to more innovative approaches, such as algorithmic and high-frequency trading. Whether you’re interested in classic risk-free arbitrage opportunities or newer methods involving technology, this show has something to offer.

One of the unique features of this podcast is its focus on algorithmic trading, which has revolutionized the way many traders engage in arbitrage. The hosts frequently discuss how algorithms can be used to identify and exploit arbitrage opportunities in real-time, a critical topic for anyone interested in automated trading systems.

What to Expect:

  • Focus on high-frequency and algorithmic trading
  • Expert advice on finding arbitrage opportunities
  • Strategies for risk management and capital allocation
  • Insights into the intersection of technology and arbitrage

Why Follow? If you’re interested in taking your arbitrage trading to the next level by incorporating technology and automation, this podcast is a perfect fit. It offers valuable insights into how the future of trading might look and how you can stay ahead of the competition.

4. The Trading Coach Podcast

While “The Trading Coach Podcast” isn’t exclusively focused on arbitrage, it has a wealth of episodes dedicated to arbitrage trading strategies. Hosted by professional trader and educator, Akil Stokes, the show provides valuable lessons on how to develop a winning mindset and how to execute arbitrage strategies in real-world trading scenarios.

The podcast often breaks down real trades, showing how arbitrage was used to exploit price differences between different markets. This step-by-step approach allows listeners to understand the thought process behind each trade, making it easier to apply similar strategies to their own portfolios.

What to Expect:

  • Practical lessons on executing arbitrage strategies
  • Mindset and psychology tips for traders
  • Real-world trade breakdowns
  • A focus on long-term success and consistent performance

Why Follow? If you’re a trader looking to combine your arbitrage skills with a strong psychological approach to trading, this podcast offers plenty of actionable advice. Akil’s teaching style is very approachable, making it ideal for traders at all levels.

5. Financial Independence Podcast

For those who want to integrate arbitrage into their broader financial strategy, the “Financial Independence Podcast” is an excellent resource. This podcast covers a variety of topics related to financial freedom, from saving and investing to more niche strategies like arbitrage. The hosts delve into how arbitrage can be used as a tool for building wealth and generating passive income.

Episodes often feature interviews with successful entrepreneurs and traders who have used arbitrage to accelerate their financial independence journeys. If you’re interested in understanding how arbitrage can be part of a larger wealth-building strategy, this podcast is a must-listen.

What to Expect:

  • Insights into how arbitrage fits into financial independence strategies
  • Real-life case studies of individuals using arbitrage for wealth building
  • Guest interviews with entrepreneurs and financial experts
  • Tips on building passive income and financial freedom

Why Follow? If you’re aiming for financial independence and want to understand how arbitrage can fit into that picture, this podcast is a fantastic resource. It’s especially valuable for anyone looking to use arbitrage as part of a larger plan to achieve financial goals.

6. The Financial Markets Podcast

Another great resource for those interested in the intersection of arbitrage and the financial markets is “The Financial Markets Podcast.” Hosted by financial market experts, this show delves into arbitrage within various markets such as equities, commodities, forex, and digital assets. The hosts break down how price discrepancies occur and offer practical advice on how to exploit them, making it a valuable resource for traders who want to improve their arbitrage strategies.

The podcast also touches on important topics such as risk management, market regulations, and the impact of technological advancements on arbitrage opportunities. It’s a comprehensive podcast for anyone looking to keep up with developments in the global financial markets.

What to Expect:

  • In-depth discussions on arbitrage in global markets
  • Focus on risk management and market regulations
  • Insights into how technological advancements shape arbitrage
  • Analysis of market conditions and their effect on arbitrage opportunities

Why Follow? This podcast is ideal for anyone who wants to explore arbitrage in the context of the broader financial landscape. The discussions are both insightful and thought-provoking, making it a must-follow for those who want to stay updated on the latest developments.

7. The Forex Arbitrage Podcast

If you’re specifically interested in forex arbitrage, then “The Forex Arbitrage Podcast” is a go-to resource. Hosted by a team of forex traders, this show provides an in-depth look at how arbitrage can be used in the foreign exchange market. From basic concepts like triangular arbitrage to more advanced strategies like currency arbitrage, this podcast covers it all.

The hosts regularly discuss different forex arbitrage techniques and provide listeners with tools to identify and exploit price discrepancies across different forex pairs. Whether you’re a novice or an experienced trader, there’s always something new to learn on this podcast.

What to Expect:

  • Deep dives into forex arbitrage strategies
  • Tips on how to identify forex price discrepancies
  • Tutorials on executing forex arbitrage trades
  • Analysis of the global forex market

Why Follow? For anyone involved in forex trading, this podcast is a treasure trove of valuable information. It’s the ideal podcast for anyone who wants to learn how to apply arbitrage techniques specifically in the currency market.

8. Crypto Arbitrage Podcast

For those with an interest in cryptocurrency trading, the “Crypto Arbitrage Podcast” is an essential listen. This podcast focuses specifically on the rapidly growing world of crypto arbitrage, offering advice on how to capitalize on price differences between cryptocurrency exchanges. The hosts explore different types of crypto arbitrage, such as spatial and triangular arbitrage, and provide practical strategies for executing profitable trades.

Crypto markets can be highly volatile, which makes arbitrage an even more attractive option for traders. This podcast is an excellent resource for anyone who wants to stay informed about the latest developments in crypto arbitrage, from the basics to advanced strategies.

What to Expect:

  • Insights into crypto arbitrage opportunities
  • Tutorials on executing profitable crypto arbitrage trades
  • Analysis of the latest trends in the cryptocurrency market
  • Discussions on risk management in crypto arbitrage

Why Follow? For cryptocurrency traders looking to maximize profits through arbitrage, this podcast provides a wealth of knowledge. It’s a must-follow for anyone who wants to understand how arbitrage fits into the crypto ecosystem.

Arbitrage is a fascinating and dynamic field that offers plenty of opportunities for traders and investors alike. Whether you’re interested in forex, stocks, crypto, or commodities, there are numerous podcasts that can help you stay informed and develop your arbitrage skills. The podcasts mentioned in this post represent some of the best resources available for anyone looking to dive deep into the world of arbitrage trading.

By following these podcasts, you’ll gain access to expert insights, practical strategies, and real-world examples that will help you navigate the complexities of arbitrage. So, tune in to these shows, expand your knowledge, and start honing your arbitrage trading skills today!
