How to Make $200/Day Grocery Delivering with Instacart


In today’s gig economy, there are numerous ways to make money, but one of the most popular and rewarding methods is by working with delivery services like Instacart. For those who enjoy flexibility in their work schedule, getting started as a grocery delivery driver can be a profitable side hustle or even a full-time income. In this post, we’ll explore how to make $200 per day grocery delivering with Instacart, what it takes to succeed, and some key strategies that can help maximize your earnings.

What is Instacart?

Instacart is a grocery delivery service that partners with local grocery stores to offer same-day delivery of groceries to customers. Customers order groceries through the Instacart app or website, and personal shoppers (also called “shoppers” or “delivery drivers”) fulfill those orders by shopping at the store and delivering the groceries directly to the customer’s door.

The beauty of working with Instacart is that you can set your own hours, work as much or as little as you want, and earn money by shopping and delivering groceries. Instacart also offers tips, which can significantly boost your earnings.

Now, let’s break down exactly how you can make $200 per day with Instacart.

Step 1: Sign Up and Get Approved

Before you can start earning money, you’ll need to sign up as an Instacart shopper. The process is simple and straightforward.

  1. Sign Up Online: Visit the Instacart website or download the app to create an account. You’ll need to provide some basic information, such as your name, contact details, and vehicle information (if you plan to drive).

  2. Background Check: Instacart performs a background check to ensure that you’re eligible to work. This usually takes a few days.

  3. Approval: Once you pass the background check, you’ll receive confirmation and can start working as a shopper. You’ll also have the option to attend an orientation session, which helps you get familiar with the app, policies, and expectations.

Once you’re approved, it’s time to get started!

Step 2: Understand How Instacart Pay Works

Instacart pay is based on multiple factors, so understanding how your earnings are calculated can help you optimize your work. Generally, your earnings come from:

  • Base Pay: This is the fixed amount you’ll earn for each order you complete. It’s determined by the order size, how long it will take to shop, and the distance you’ll need to drive for the delivery.

  • Tips: Customers have the option to tip you for your service, which can significantly boost your earnings. You can receive tips both through the app and in cash.

  • Promotions and Bonuses: Instacart frequently offers incentives like peak pay (extra earnings during busy times) and bonuses for completing a set number of orders within a specific timeframe.

Instacart also offers two types of shoppers: Full-Service Shoppers (those who both shop and deliver) and In-Store Shoppers (those who only shop). To earn $200 a day, you’ll want to become a Full-Service Shopper, as this allows you to maximize your potential income by delivering groceries as well.

Step 3: Determine the Ideal Hours for Work

One of the key components of earning $200 a day with Instacart is choosing the right hours to work. Some times of the day are busier than others, and working during these peak times can help you complete more orders and earn more money.

Morning Hours (8 AM - 11 AM)

Mornings tend to be slower but can still offer good opportunities, especially if you focus on small orders or local delivery areas. Many people are preparing for their day, so they may prefer grocery delivery during this time.

Afternoon Hours (12 PM - 4 PM)

Afternoon hours can vary, but lunch hours can be particularly profitable because many people prefer to get their grocery shopping done before they head home from work. People often order groceries for delivery while at work.

Evening Hours (5 PM - 9 PM)

The evening shift is when the demand for grocery deliveries is at its peak. Customers are returning home from work and looking to get their groceries delivered after a long day. This time frame is an excellent opportunity to earn the highest possible pay, especially when you combine base pay, tips, and any bonuses.

Step 4: Optimize Your Delivery Strategy

To consistently make $200 or more per day, you need to adopt strategies that maximize your efficiency and earnings potential. Here are some tips:

1. Focus on Large Orders

Large orders usually translate to more money. When you see an order that involves a high volume of items or a large total, these orders are often worth your time. They will pay better than smaller orders and often involve shorter driving distances.

2. Take Advantage of Peak Pay

Instacart offers “peak pay” during high-demand hours. These periods can significantly increase your earnings per order, so try to schedule your work around these times. Keep an eye on the app for any notifications about peak pay.

3. Work in High-Demand Areas

Some areas have higher demand than others, and working in a busy urban area or near larger grocery stores can help you get more orders. Look for neighborhoods with higher concentrations of people, especially near apartment complexes or areas where people may be more inclined to use grocery delivery services.

4. Don’t Forget to Communicate with Customers

Customer satisfaction is key. Always make sure to communicate clearly and politely with your customers, especially if there are any issues with their order (such as out-of-stock items). If you provide excellent service, customers may reward you with a higher tip.

5. Use the Instacart Shopper App to Track Your Progress

The Instacart Shopper app has a built-in feature to track your earnings. This allows you to set goals and see how much you’re earning in real-time. Use this tool to track your progress throughout the day and adjust your strategy accordingly.

Step 5: Manage Your Expenses

While making $200 a day is definitely achievable with Instacart, it’s important to keep track of your expenses to ensure that your profits remain high. As an independent contractor, you’re responsible for your vehicle and other business expenses, such as:

  • Fuel: The more you drive, the more you’ll spend on gas. To minimize fuel costs, try to plan your routes efficiently and avoid unnecessary detours.

  • Car Maintenance: Regular car maintenance is essential for keeping your vehicle in good condition. Set aside money for oil changes, tire rotations, and other maintenance tasks to keep your car running smoothly.

  • Supplies: While Instacart provides some of the essential tools for delivery, you may want to invest in items like insulated bags, a phone mount, or a cooler for keeping perishable items fresh.

By keeping track of your expenses and making small adjustments to reduce unnecessary costs, you can ensure that your daily profits remain as high as possible.

Step 6: Stay Motivated and Consistent

Consistency is key to making $200 a day with Instacart. While it may take some time to get used to the job, the more you work, the more you’ll learn about the best strategies for earning. It’s important to stay motivated and disciplined, especially on days when things aren’t going as smoothly.

Remember that this is a flexible gig, so you can adjust your schedule as needed. Whether you work full-time or part-time, it’s important to maintain a consistent work routine in order to reach your goal of $200 a day.

Step 7: Utilize Customer Tips to Boost Your Earnings

Customer tips are an essential part of your total earnings when working with Instacart. While base pay is important, tips can make a huge difference in how much you take home each day.

Here are a few tips for maximizing your tips:

  1. Provide Excellent Customer Service: Always be friendly, helpful, and proactive. If an item is out of stock, offer a suitable replacement and keep the customer informed. A positive shopping experience can result in a bigger tip.

  2. Be Prompt: Customers appreciate timely deliveries. The quicker you complete an order, the happier the customer will be, and the more likely they are to tip well.

  3. Personalize the Experience: A simple thank-you note or a friendly interaction can go a long way in improving customer satisfaction and encouraging generous tips.

Conclusion

Making $200 a day grocery delivering with Instacart is an achievable goal, but it requires a combination of strategy, efficiency, and excellent customer service. By signing up, understanding how pay works, choosing the right hours, and focusing on maximizing your earnings through large orders, peak pay, and tips, you can start seeing impressive results.

While it may take some time to reach $200 in a day consistently, with perseverance and the right approach, you’ll be able to enjoy the financial freedom that comes with grocery delivery. Happy shopping, and here’s to reaching your income goals!
