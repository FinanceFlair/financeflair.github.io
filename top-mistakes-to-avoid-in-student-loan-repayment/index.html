Top Mistakes to Avoid in Student Loan Repayment


Managing student loan debt can be daunting, especially for fresh graduates or individuals who have taken on large sums of debt. With so many repayment options and strategies available, it's easy to get overwhelmed and make mistakes that can have long-term consequences. Ignoring payment plans and delaying payments can also result in late fees, penalties, and worse, default on the loan. Pay attention to payment schedules, and prioritize payments whenever possible. Making timely payments and exploring income-driven repayment plans can help manage debt more effectively.
