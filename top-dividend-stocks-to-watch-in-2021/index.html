Top Dividend Stocks to Watch in 2021


The year 2021 has proven to be a promising one for investors looking to capitalize on the resilience of dividend-paying stocks. As the global economy continues its recovery from the effects of the pandemic, now is an ideal time to explore reliable dividend stocks that can provide both income and growth. In this article, we will examine some of the top dividend stocks to watch in 2021, taking into consideration their track record, growth potential, and unwitting risk factors.

First up is Johnson & Johnson (NYSE: JNJ). This healthcare conglomerate has a long and storied history of paying dividends, with an uninterrupted history dating back to 1944. As one of the world’s largest and most diverse companies, Johnson & Johnson operates in multiple sectors such as pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and consumer products. Investors can benefit from their diverse product offerings while enjoying a competitive dividend yield of approximately 2.5%.

Another exciting pick is Procter & Gamble (NYSE: PG). This consumer goods giant is known for its stable and reliable dividend payments. With a yield close to 2.5%, P&G has consistently maintained its dividend growth throughout the years. The company’s diverse product offerings and strong brand recognition ensure that it remains a top choice for investors seeking a stable dividend income.

The Coca-Cola Company (NYSE: KO) is another classic dividend stock to consider. Known for its iconic beverage products, Coke has been paying dividends since 1919. With a current yield of around 3%, investors can benefit from the company’s global distribution network and brand recognition. As the world embraces a return to normalcy, Coca-Cola is well-positioned to capitalize on increased demand for its products.

Johnson Controls (NYSE: JCI) is a global leader in smart and sustainable building solutions. Dividend seekers can benefit from their approximately 2.5% dividend yield while enjoying the company’s strong position in the rapidly growing smart building technology market. Johnson Controls’ focus on sustainability and energy-efficient solutions positions them well for long-term growth.

IBM (NYSE: IBM) is a technology giant with a rich history of dividend payments, dating back to 1916. As the company undergoes a transformation to focus on the high-growth areas such as cloud services and AI, it offers an approximately 4.3% dividend yield, making it a solid choice for income-seeking investors.

Lastly, we have ** Chevron Corporation** (NYSE: CVX), an oil and gas giant known for its stable and reliable dividends. With a current yield of around 5.3%, investors can benefit from Chevron’s expertise in exploring and producing energy resources while enjoying a generous dividend payout.

In conclusion, the aforementioned dividend stocks offer a mix of a stable and growing dividend yields, diverse industries, and global presence. For investors seeking both income and growth, these companies stand out as top choices to watch in 2021. Remember to perform your own research, stay diligent and diversify your investment portfolio. Happy investing!
