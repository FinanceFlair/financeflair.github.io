  1. Coca-Cola (KO) Soft drinks giant Coca-Cola has faced declining sales in recent years, leading to a significant drop in its stock price. However, the company's innovative technology, strategic partnerships, and consistently expanding user base make it a compelling buy opportunity. With a proven track record of innovation and a growing pipeline of products, Micron is well-positioned for long-term growth.
