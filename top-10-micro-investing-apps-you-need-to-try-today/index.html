Top 10 Micro Investing Apps You Need to Try Today


In today’s fast-paced financial landscape, many individuals are seeking ways to invest without committing large sums of money. Micro investing apps have emerged as a popular solution, allowing users to invest small amounts and build wealth over time. If you’re looking to dip your toes into the world of investing or simply want to diversify your portfolio, here are ten micro investing apps that you should consider trying today.

1. Acorns

Acorns is one of the pioneers in the micro investing space, making it easy for users to start investing with spare change. The app rounds up your purchases to the nearest dollar and invests the difference in a diversified portfolio of ETFs. This automated approach takes the hassle out of investing, allowing you to grow your wealth without having to think about it. With features like recurring investments and educational resources, Acorns is perfect for beginners looking to get started.

Key Features:

  • Round-up transactions for effortless investing
  • Diversified portfolios tailored to your risk tolerance
  • Automatic rebalancing and reinvestment of dividends
  • Educational content to help you understand investing

2. Stash

Stash offers a user-friendly platform that combines micro investing with personal finance management. Users can invest in fractional shares of stocks and ETFs, allowing for a more tailored investment experience. Stash also provides personalized recommendations based on your financial goals and interests, making it ideal for those who want to align their investments with their values.

Key Features:

  • Fractional shares for affordable investing
  • Personalized investment recommendations
  • Educational resources to enhance financial literacy
  • Stash banking for seamless money management

3. Robinhood

Robinhood revolutionized the investing landscape by offering commission-free trading of stocks, ETFs, and cryptocurrencies. While it isn’t strictly a micro investing app, it allows users to start investing with any amount of money, including fractional shares. The app’s sleek design and easy-to-use interface make it appealing to both novice and experienced investors.

Key Features:

  • Commission-free trading with no minimum balance
  • Fractional shares to diversify your portfolio
  • Access to cryptocurrencies and options trading
  • Real-time market data and customizable alerts

4. Betterment

Betterment is a robo-advisor that simplifies the investing process by creating personalized portfolios based on your financial goals and risk tolerance. With no minimum investment amount, Betterment allows users to start investing with whatever they have, making it accessible for everyone. The app also offers automatic rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting, optimizing your investment strategy over time.

Key Features:

  • Personalized portfolios designed by financial experts
  • Automatic rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting
  • Goal-based investing to align with your financial objectives
  • Educational resources to improve investment knowledge

5. M1 Finance

M1 Finance combines the best of both worlds by allowing users to create customized portfolios while also offering the option to invest in pre-built portfolios. The app enables fractional investing, meaning you can own a piece of high-value stocks without having to buy a full share. M1 Finance also provides automated rebalancing, making it easier to maintain your desired asset allocation.

Key Features:

  • Customizable portfolios with fractional shares
  • Automated rebalancing to keep your investments on track
  • Access to a wide variety of stocks and ETFs
  • No management fees and commission-free trading

6. Fundrise

If you’re interested in real estate investing, Fundrise is a great option for micro investors. This platform allows you to invest in commercial real estate projects with as little as $500. Fundrise offers a diversified portfolio of real estate investments, providing exposure to an asset class that traditionally required significant capital. The app also features automatic reinvestment of dividends to maximize growth.

Key Features:

  • Invest in commercial real estate with low minimums
  • Diversified portfolios for risk management
  • Automatic reinvestment of dividends
  • Detailed performance tracking and insights

7. SoFi Invest

SoFi Invest is a multifaceted investing platform that offers both active and automated investing options. Users can choose to invest in fractional shares of stocks or let SoFi’s robo-advisor manage their investments for them. With no commissions and no minimum investment requirements, SoFi Invest is an excellent choice for those looking to start investing with small amounts of money.

Key Features:

  • Fractional shares for accessible investing
  • Active and automated investing options
  • No commissions or account minimums
  • Educational resources and community support

8. Clink

Clink is a unique micro investing app that allows users to invest their spare change from everyday purchases. The app rounds up transactions and invests the difference in a diversified portfolio. Users can also set up automated deposits to grow their investment accounts over time. Clink’s gamified approach to saving and investing makes it an engaging option for millennials and Gen Z.

Key Features:

  • Round-up transactions for effortless investing
  • Automated deposits for consistent growth
  • Engaging interface with gamified features
  • Access to diversified portfolios tailored to your goals

9. Wealthsimple

Wealthsimple is a robo-advisor that simplifies the investing process by creating personalized portfolios based on your financial goals and risk tolerance. With no minimum investment requirement, Wealthsimple is accessible for new investors. The app emphasizes socially responsible investing, allowing users to align their investments with their values.

Key Features:

  • Personalized portfolios crafted by financial experts
  • No minimum investment required
  • Emphasis on socially responsible investing options
  • Automatic rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting

10. Acorns Later

Acorns Later is an extension of the Acorns platform that focuses on retirement investing. This app allows users to set up an IRA and automate their contributions. With the same round-up feature that Acorns is known for, Acorns Later makes it simple to save for retirement without the stress of managing your investments actively.

Key Features:

  • Automated retirement investing with round-ups
  • Various IRA options to suit your needs
  • Seamless integration with the Acorns app
  • Educational resources to help you plan for retirement

Conclusion

Micro investing apps have made investing more accessible than ever, allowing individuals to start building their wealth with small amounts of money. Whether you’re interested in stocks, ETFs, or real estate, there’s an app tailored to your needs. By choosing the right micro investing platform, you can take control of your financial future without the need for a large initial investment. Explore these ten micro investing apps today and take the first step toward growing your wealth!
