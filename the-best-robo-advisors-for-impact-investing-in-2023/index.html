The Best Robo-Advisors for Impact Investing in 2023


Impact investing has become increasingly popular in recent years as more investors seek to align their portfolios with their values. Instead of just focusing on financial returns, impact investors aim to generate positive social and environmental change alongside financial gain. One of the best ways to engage in impact investing is through robo-advisors, which provide automated, algorithm-driven financial planning services. These platforms have grown in popularity due to their ease of use, low fees, and accessibility. But not all robo-advisors are created equal, especially when it comes to impact investing.

In this blog post, we will explore the best robo-advisors for impact investing in 2023. Whether you’re a seasoned investor or new to the world of socially responsible investing (SRI), these platforms can help you invest with a purpose. Let’s dive in.

What Is Impact Investing?

Impact investing refers to investments made with the intention to generate positive, measurable social and environmental impact, alongside a financial return. This investment strategy focuses on creating change in areas such as climate change, renewable energy, affordable housing, gender equality, and more. Unlike traditional investments that prioritize only financial returns, impact investing strives to make a difference in the world while also achieving a reasonable return on investment.

Impact investing has gained momentum in recent years, driven by growing awareness of social and environmental issues. Investors are no longer satisfied with merely avoiding harm through traditional investments; they want to actively promote change. As a result, the demand for tools that allow investors to make informed decisions in this space has skyrocketed.

How Do Robo-Advisors Fit into Impact Investing?

Robo-advisors are online platforms that use algorithms to manage and optimize investment portfolios. These platforms offer low-cost solutions by automating the process of portfolio selection, rebalancing, and asset allocation. They have made investing accessible to a broader audience, offering features that allow individuals to get started with minimal capital.

For impact investing, robo-advisors often allow users to select specific values or goals they wish to prioritize, such as environmental sustainability, diversity, or community development. The robo-advisor then creates a portfolio that reflects these preferences while managing risk and maximizing returns.

While many robo-advisors offer a broad range of portfolios, only some specialize in impact investing. These platforms are crucial for those who want to take a hands-off approach to their impact investments while ensuring their money supports causes that matter to them.

The Best Robo-Advisors for Impact Investing in 2023

1. Betterment

Betterment is one of the most well-known robo-advisors, and it has made strides in the impact investing space. The platform offers socially responsible investing (SRI) portfolios designed to align with the investor’s values. Betterment’s SRI portfolios include companies that focus on areas such as renewable energy, clean technology, and social equality.

What makes Betterment stand out is its customization. Investors can tailor their portfolios to reflect their personal values and social goals. Additionally, Betterment uses low-cost exchange-traded funds (ETFs) that track socially responsible indexes, offering a broad range of companies involved in impact-related initiatives.

Betterment also makes it easy for investors to start with low minimum investments, and its fees are competitive. The platform uses modern portfolio theory to manage risk and optimize returns, which is ideal for those who want both financial and social returns.

Key Features:

  • Customizable portfolios with a focus on sustainability, social responsibility, and environmental impact.
  • Low minimum investment requirements.
  • Competitive management fees.
  • Automated tax-loss harvesting for tax efficiency.

2. Wealthfront

Wealthfront is another top robo-advisor that offers a unique socially responsible investing portfolio. The platform offers a “Socially Responsible Investing” portfolio that includes companies committed to environmental sustainability, social good, and ethical practices.

Wealthfront takes a scientific approach to investing, using financial planning tools to help users make informed decisions. Their SRI portfolio includes a wide range of companies that focus on low-carbon energy, clean water, gender equality, and fair labor practices. The robo-advisor is also transparent about the companies included in its portfolios, giving investors clarity about where their money is going.

In addition to its SRI portfolio, Wealthfront provides features like tax-loss harvesting, which can help reduce your tax liability while enhancing the returns on your impact investments. Wealthfront also offers financial planning tools and goal-setting features to help investors achieve both their social and financial goals.

Key Features:

  • Socially responsible portfolios focusing on environmental sustainability and social issues.
  • Automatic tax-loss harvesting to maximize after-tax returns.
  • Transparent portfolio options with a focus on ethical practices.
  • Goal-setting tools for personalized financial planning.

3. Ellevest

Ellevest is a robo-advisor specifically designed to cater to women investors, but its approach to impact investing is relevant to anyone interested in socially responsible portfolios. Ellevest offers portfolios that focus on sustainable investing, gender equality, and social justice.

The platform’s goal is to create a more inclusive financial system, and it integrates this mission into its investment strategy. Ellevest’s impact investing portfolios are made up of ETFs that prioritize companies making strides toward improving gender equality, renewable energy, and economic empowerment for marginalized communities.

Ellevest stands out because it takes a gender-conscious approach to investing. The platform considers factors like the gender pay gap, investing in women-led companies, and companies focused on improving workplace equality. This is ideal for socially conscious investors who want to ensure their money supports causes that empower women and create social equity.

Key Features:

  • Impact investing portfolios focused on gender equality, social justice, and sustainability.
  • Tailored investment recommendations based on individual goals.
  • Gender-conscious investing approach.
  • Low minimum investment and affordable fees.

4. SoFi Invest

SoFi Invest is a robo-advisor that provides socially responsible investment options through its “Socially Responsible Portfolio” feature. This portfolio focuses on companies with high ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) scores and supports causes like clean energy, climate change, diversity, and corporate transparency.

What makes SoFi Invest appealing is its user-friendly platform and low-fee structure. SoFi offers commission-free trades, which reduces the overall cost of investing. In addition to impact investing portfolios, SoFi also offers traditional investment options, making it easy for users to diversify their portfolios while staying true to their values.

SoFi Invest also provides personalized financial advice and guidance, making it a great option for those who want to integrate impact investing into a broader financial plan. The platform is accessible and beginner-friendly, making it a solid choice for new investors interested in socially responsible investing.

Key Features:

  • Low-fee structure with commission-free trades.
  • ESG-focused investment portfolios targeting sustainable and ethical companies.
  • Personalized financial advice.
  • User-friendly interface and mobile app.

5. Swell Investing

Swell Investing is a robo-advisor that specializes entirely in impact investing. The platform allows users to invest in portfolios aligned with six key impact themes: clean water, renewable energy, zero waste, green tech, sustainable food, and climate change. Swell’s mission is to help investors make a difference while generating a reasonable financial return.

Swell Investing is unique because it offers fully automated, hands-off investment management. The platform selects companies that prioritize environmental sustainability and social impact, allowing investors to focus on making a difference without needing to do the heavy lifting themselves.

Though Swell requires a higher minimum investment compared to other platforms, it’s a great option for those who are serious about impact investing and want to ensure their portfolios are aligned with their values. It’s also a good choice for people looking for a platform that specializes entirely in impact investing.

Key Features:

  • Focused exclusively on impact investing, with portfolios based on environmental and social issues.
  • Automated investment management.
  • Clean, easy-to-use platform.
  • Focus on renewable energy, clean water, and other critical global challenges.

Why Choose Robo-Advisors for Impact Investing?

Robo-advisors are an excellent tool for anyone interested in impact investing for several reasons:

  • Low Fees: Robo-advisors typically charge lower management fees than traditional financial advisors, making them an affordable option for investors.
  • Automation: These platforms take care of portfolio management, rebalancing, and tax optimization, allowing investors to focus on other aspects of their lives.
  • Personalization: Many robo-advisors allow investors to tailor their portfolios according to their social and environmental preferences.
  • Accessibility: Robo-advisors make it easy for anyone to get started with impact investing, even with a small initial investment.

Conclusion

Impact investing is no longer just a niche market; it’s a mainstream investment strategy for those who want to make a difference in the world. The best robo-advisors for impact investing in 2023 offer a variety of portfolios that cater to different social and environmental goals, allowing investors to put their money to work for positive change.

Whether you’re interested in renewable energy, gender equality, or social justice, there’s a robo-advisor that can help you align your financial goals with your values. Platforms like Betterment, Wealthfront, Ellevest, SoFi Invest, and Swell Investing make it easy to get started with impact investing, offering both financial and social returns.

Investing with purpose is not just about making a profit—it’s about creating a better world for future generations. If you’re ready to take the next step in impact investing, these robo-advisors offer a convenient and effective way to make your money work for you and for the causes you care about.
