Best Robo-Advisors for Tax-Loss Harvesting in 2024


Tax-loss harvesting is a popular investment strategy that involves selling securities that have declined in value to offset gains from other investments and reduce tax liability. What is Tax-Loss Harvesting?

Tax-loss harvesting is a tax-efficient investment strategy that involves selling securities that have declined in value to offset gains from other investments. The goal is to reduce tax liability by minimizing the amount of capital gains taxes owed. This strategy is particularly useful for investors who have held securities for a short period of time and have realized a gain.

How Do Robo-Advisors Offer Tax-Loss Harvesting?

Robo-advisors use sophisticated algorithms and data analysis to identify securities that have declined in value and sell them to offset gains from other investments. They then reinvest the proceeds in a tax-efficient manner, such as in a tax-loss harvested index fund or an exchange-traded fund (ETF). This process is repeated regularly to ensure that the portfolio remains tax-efficient.
