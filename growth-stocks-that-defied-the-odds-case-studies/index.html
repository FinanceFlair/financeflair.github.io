Growth Stocks That Defied the Odds: Case Studies


Market performance has long been driven by countless, intangible factors. Timing, economic condition, industry trends and company performance all play a crucial role in determining their stock price movements. Here, we will dive into real growth stocks’ successes that seemingly defied odds to analyze what can be learned from them.

Underdog Story 1: Netflix(NASDAQ: NFLX)

When Netflix first listed in 2002, its stock price didn’t fare well mainly due to the public’s skepticism about its capability to compete in the crowded VHS/DVD rental market. However, followed by the transition to the streaming era, Netflix took over the market like wildfire. Despite a drastic increase in competition from Amazon Prime and Disney+, Netflix continues to dominate the market. The key factors behind its phenomenal growth are content production with Netflix Originals and technology incorporation into the entertainment delivery system. Despite the turbulent stock market, their focus on innovation has maintained and grown their market dominance.

Underdog Story 2: Shopify (NYSE: SHOP)

Shopify went public in 2015 and unfortunately began with public investors worrying about its ability to scale and compete in the crowded e-commerce market. However, this company reallocated their resources into development, ending up with a wildly successful Software as a Service (SaaS) that provides budding entrepreneurs to set up their online shops. Shopify’s year-over-year growth helps defy the odds of failure that might beset a typical e-commerce platform. Furthermore, the SaaS model has provided a helpful revenue stream, causing Shopify to be especially attractive in the market.

Underdog Story 3: Zoom (NASDAQ: ZM)

In March 2020, Zoom meetings became ubiquitous as people adjusted to social distancing prompts. Prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, Zoom was just another video conferencing app; their primary competitor, Skype already existed in the market. Zoom’s impeccable service and perfect timing helped seize the opportunity in front of them. Zoom’s statistics reflect that customer engagement and revenue have surged amidst the unsolicited pandemic popularity. Even now, as society amends its stance on remote working, Zoom presents a crucial tool for modern communication.

These companies have proven not all growth stocks’ success is tied to market conditions and timing. Beyond that, their undeterred path to innovation has born success from once under-appreciated endeavours. Both Netflix and Shopify were seen as threats in their early days but positioned themselves into industry pioneers. Likewise, underdog Zoom hastened to grab the unprecedented demand for remote communication during a transformative period in history. Their paths serve as a reminder that a strong, defined strategy can help a company remain in the race.

While these stocks will always hold some risks, their strength in defiance of the odds show that paradoxically, the unexpected can be a great catalyst for long-term growth.

Note: It’s essential to consult a financial planner and execute due diligence when considering individual stocks for investment.
