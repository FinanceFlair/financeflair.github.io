The Best Real Estate Podcasts for Investors


As a real estate investor, staying informed about the latest market trends, strategies, and best practices is crucial for success. One of the most effective ways to achieve this is by listening to real estate podcasts. These audio resources offer valuable insights, expert advice, and real-world experiences from investors, agents, and industry experts. In this comprehensive guide, we'll delve into the best real estate podcasts for investors, highlighting the must-listen shows that will help you navigate the complex world of real estate investing with confidence. Top Podcasts for Beginner Investors If you're new to real estate investing, it's essential to start with the basics. Here are some top podcasts that cover the fundamentals and provide a solid foundation for your learning journey:

The Real Estate Guys Radio Show: Hosts Harold Olivieri and Robert Helms take you on a comprehensive tour of the world of real estate investing, covering topics such as tax advantages, leveraging, and cash-flow management. The BiggerPockets Podcast: Co-hosts David Greene and Rob Abasheikh draw from their extensive experience as investors and share actionable tips, guidelines, and real-life examples to help you build successful real estate investment portfolios. Plan for Prosperity: Lorey and Tom aired their first show in 2006. Their guests include experienced real estate investors who share their experiences and insights on managing wealth and assets.

Specialized Podcasts for Advanced Investors If you're looking for podcasts that focus on more advanced concepts and strategies, here are some top shows to consider:

The Real Estate Guys Radio Show's "Ask Harold & Jason": This podcast focuses on a wide range of real estate-related topics with established real estate investors and experts. Personal Stories and Experiences There are certain podcasts specifically designed to feature interviews with experienced real estate investors and professionals who share their personal stories and insights:

The Real Estate Players: Hosted by Todd Miller, this podcast takes a deep dive into the strategies and experiences of successful real estate investors and business owners. Success in the real estate business is less a solo activity and more about leadership in multi-discipline teams and product development. Flip Talk Radio: Hosted by Robyn Thompson and Paul Knaster, this podcast features interviews with investors who share their strategies, successes, and failures in the world of real estate investing.
