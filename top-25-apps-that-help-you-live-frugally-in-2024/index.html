Top 25 Apps That Help You Live Frugally in 2024


Living frugally has become an increasingly popular lifestyle choice for many, not only as a means to stretch every dollar but also to create long-term financial stability. Thanks to the explosion of mobile technology, there are now dozens of applications designed specifically to help manage expenses, find discounts, track budgets, and make smarter money decisions. Whether you’re striving to save for a major purchase, reduce debt, or simply get better control over your spending, these tools are more useful than ever.

Let’s dive into 25 of the best apps available in 2024 that will help you live frugally without sacrificing quality of life.

1. Mint – Comprehensive Budget Management

Mint remains a staple in personal finance management, combining billing reminders, budget tracking, and expense categorization. Its intuitive interface syncs with bank accounts, credit cards, and bills to give you a holistic overview of your finances, making it easier to spot where you can cut back.

Key Features:

  • Custom budget goals and spending alerts.
  • Free credit score monitoring.
  • Investment tracking for broader money insight.

Why it helps: Mint encourages conscious spending by showing real-time updates and financial trends, empowering you to live within your means.

2. YNAB (You Need a Budget) – Proactive Budgeting Philosophy

YNAB is popular for a reason: its unique approach to budgeting challenges the conventional methods. Instead of tracking past spending, YNAB encourages users to allocate every dollar in advance, promoting forward-thinking money habits.

Key Features:

  • Goal-setting and progress tracking.
  • Real-time syncing with bank accounts.
  • Educational resources for financial literacy.

Why it helps: It shifts the mindset from reactive to proactive budgeting, minimizing impulse spending and improving saving habits.

3. Honey – Automatic Coupon & Promo Code Finder

Honey excels at uncovering deals when shopping online. It automatically scans for valid coupons, promo codes, or cash-back opportunities at checkout for thousands of retailers.

Key Features:

  • One-click application of discounts.
  • Price tracking alert for desired products.
  • Reward points that convert to gift cards.

Why it helps: It ensures you never miss out on savings and maximizes the value of your purchases without extra effort.

4. Splitwise – Fair Expense Sharing

If you regularly share expenses with roommates, friends, or family, Splitwise makes bookkeeping hassle-free. It tracks payments and IOUs, calculates who owes what, and sends reminders, simplifying complex shared spending situations.

Key Features:

  • Multi-currency support.
  • Group expense management.
  • Integrations with payment platforms like PayPal and Venmo.

Why it helps: Eliminates awkward conversations about money and prevents overpaying on shared bills.

5. Rakuten – Cash Back on Everyday Purchases

Formerly Ebates, Rakuten offers cashback deals when you shop via its platform either online or in stores. It partners with thousands of businesses, providing percentage returns that add up over time.

Key Features:

  • Browser extension for automatic cashback.
  • Mobile app with exclusive coupons.
  • Fast payouts via PayPal or check.

Why it helps: Earning cash back on routine purchases makes frugality effortless and rewarding.

6. Acorns – Automatic Micro-Investment Savings

Acorns rounds up everyday transactions to the nearest dollar and invests the spare change into portfolios tailored to your risk preferences.

Key Features:

  • Withdrawable micro-investments.
  • Found money rewards from partner brands.
  • Financial education tools inside the app.

Why it helps: It nudges you toward saving and investing passively, which can grow into substantial funds over time.

7. Goodbudget – Envelope Budgeting for Discipline

Inspired by the classic envelope budgeting technique, Goodbudget uses virtual envelopes to allocate funds for different categories. This method prevents overspending by limiting transactions to what’s available in each envelope.

Key Features:

  • Syncs across multiple devices.
  • Visual charts for monitoring budgets.
  • Planning tools for irregular expenses like gifts or medical bills.

Why it helps: It fosters awareness and discipline through real-time budgeting limits.

8. Ibotta – Exclusive In-Store & Online Rebates

Ibotta is a rebate app that pays you back for your grocery, drugstore, and retail purchases, both online and offline.

Key Features:

  • Scan receipts or link loyalty cards.
  • Weekly bonuses and challenges for extra savings.
  • Wide variety of participating retailers and products.

Why it helps: Offers a straightforward way to slash grocery bills and earn cash back without changing your shopping routines.

9. Truebill – Subscription Management & Negotiation

Many people waste money on forgotten subscriptions. Truebill audits your recurring expenses and helps cancel unused services. It even negotiates bills on your behalf to snag better deals.

Key Features:

  • Track and cancel subscriptions with ease.
  • Bill negotiation service.
  • Personalized budgeting tools.

Why it helps: Eliminates “leakage” in spending that quietly drains your bank account.

10. PocketGuard – Simplified Budgeting and Expense Tracking

PocketGuard categorizes your income and expenses and shows you the “In My Pocket” amount — what’s left to spend after bills and savings goals.

Key Features:

  • Intuitive spending limits.
  • Alerts for overspending risks.
  • Bill reminders and trends overview.

Why it helps: Gives you a realistic view of disposable income, preventing budget blowouts.

11. Walmart Grocery – Low-Cost Food Shopping Made Simple

Using Walmart’s app can reduce grocery bills thanks to its everyday low prices and digital coupons. You can also order for pickup or delivery with designated savings offers.

Key Features:

  • Extensive grocery selection.
  • Weekly rollback specials.
  • Easy reordering of favorites.

Why it helps: Cuts costs through convenience and consistent store-wide discounts.

12. Dosh – Automatic Cashback While You Shop or Dine

Dosh allows you to earn cashback automatically when you link your credit or debit cards and shop at participating merchants in stores, online, or dine out.

Key Features:

  • Hands-free cashback.
  • Hotel bookings earning rewards.
  • Referral bonuses and gift cards.

Why it helps: Passive accumulation of savings turns daily transactions into investments.

13. GasBuddy – Save on Fuel Costs

Fuel expenses can take a large bite out of a budget, especially if you drive frequently. GasBuddy provides gas price comparisons based on your location.

Key Features:

  • Real-time fuel price updates.
  • User-reported gas station info.
  • Rewards program for discounts.

Why it helps: Helps find the cheapest fuel option nearby, trimming transportation costs.

14. Flipp – Weekly Ads and Digital Coupons Aggregator

Flipp collects flyers and coupons from several big retailers, helping you plan shopping trips to get the most bang for your buck.

Key Features:

  • Searchable local weekly ads.
  • Coupon clipping from digital catalogues.
  • Shopping list integration.

Why it helps: Maximizes savings by organizing local deals in one easy-to-access place.

15. ShopSavvy – Barcode Scanner for Price Comparison

ShopSavvy lets you scan product barcodes in-store and instantly compares prices online and nearby stores.

Key Features:

  • Alerts on better deals.
  • Product reviews and ratings.
  • Watch list for desired items.

Why it helps: Immediately assures you aren’t overpaying, empowering smarter purchase decisions.

16. EveryDollar – Simple Budgeting with Dave Ramsey Approach

Created by finance guru Dave Ramsey, EveryDollar promotes a zero-based budget that assigns every dollar a role before spender.

Key Features:

  • Easy budgeting and goal tracking.
  • Connects to bank accounts.
  • Debt paydown assistance.

Why it helps: Keeps budgeting straightforward and focused on financial goals.

17. RetailMeNot – Coupons and Deals Finder

RetailMeNot aggregates coupons, cashback offers, and deals for a huge variety of stores both online and in-person.

Key Features:

  • One-tap coupon application.
  • Cashback from partners.
  • Holiday and flash deal alerts.

Why it helps: Helps spot limited-time savings you might otherwise miss.

18. Clarity Money – Financial Overview and Management

Clarity Money shows all your financial accounts in one place, recommends ways to save, and streamlines bill payments.

Key Features:

  • Subscription tracking and cancellation.
  • Credit score monitoring.
  • Savings goals setup.

Why it helps: Offers a clear picture of your finances with actionable insights.

19. Tally – Smart Debt Management

If debt feels overwhelming, Tally automates credit card payments to save on interest and reduce balances faster.

Key Features:

  • Automated payments prioritized by interest rates.
  • Credit line monitoring.
  • User-friendly progress tracking.

Why it helps: Efficiently cuts interest costs and improves credit over time.

20. Fetch Rewards – Scan Receipts to Earn Points

Fetch Rewards turns grocery receipts into points redeemable for gift cards by scanning any store’s receipts.

Key Features:

  • Easy receipt scanning.
  • Bonus points for select brands.
  • Wide redemption options.

Why it helps: Passive earnings through everyday purchases that stack up.

21. Billshark – Bill Negotiation Service

Billshark employs experts to negotiate bills on your behalf—especially cable, phone, and internet.

Key Features:

  • No upfront fees, only a percentage of savings.
  • Unlimited bill negotiation.
  • Email support and status updates.

Why it helps: Saves money on fixed costs without intervention.

22. Wally – Expense Tracker with Receipt Capture

Wally enables you to log expenses by scanning receipts and categorizes spending patterns.

Key Features:

  • Detailed financial analytics.
  • Currency conversion for travelers.
  • Income and expense calendar.

Why it helps: Enhances awareness of spending habits and highlights saving opportunities.

23. Groupon – Deals on Activities and Goods

Groupon offers discounts on everything from dining and entertainment to goods and travel.

Key Features:

  • Location-based deal discovery.
  • Easy redemption with mobile vouchers.
  • Regular flash sales.

Why it helps: Accesses experiences and items at a fraction of regular prices.

24. Acorns Later – Automated Retirement Savings

Complementing Acorns, this app rounds up purchases and contributes to a retirement account, simplifying long-term planning.

Key Features:

  • Tax-advantaged retirement accounts.
  • Automatic micro-investing.
  • Education resources for retirement.

Why it helps: Builds retirement savings steadily with minimal effort.

25. Digit – Smart Automated Savings

Digit analyzes your spending and automatically transfers small, manageable amounts to a savings account, adjusting to your lifestyle.

Key Features:

  • Automated daily savings.
  • Overdraft protection.
  • Goals and milestone celebrations.

Why it helps: Makes saving effortless by removing manual steps.

Final Thoughts

In an era where every cent counts, arming yourself with tools to manage your money efficiently is a game-changer. The apps listed above offer diverse ways to monitor, save, invest, and cut costs, making frugal living a sustainable, even enjoyable, choice. Harnessing technology in 2024 empowers you to build better habits, avoid unnecessary expenses, and focus your resources on what truly matters.

Whether you aim to live simply, save aggressively, or master the nuances of money management, these apps provide guidance and automation that adapt to your unique financial situation. Combine several based on your needs to multiply the benefits and step confidently toward a financially secure future.
